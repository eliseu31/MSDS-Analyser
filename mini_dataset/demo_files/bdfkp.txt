=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:FLUSH W LG AMTS H*2O FOR 15 MIN, GET MEDICAL ATTN. IF
    NEEDED; SKIN: FLUSH W LG AMTS SOAP & H*2O;INGEST:RINSE MOUTH,DRINK
    WATER,GASTRIC LAUAGE(STOMACH WASH),FOLLOWED BY SALINE CATHARSIS, &
    IMMEDIA TE MED ATTN:KIDNEY INJURY RISK.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:H*2O,DRY CHEM,FOAM,CO*2(GLYCERINE IS COMBUSTBLE).
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CNTND BRTHG APP.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:GLYCERIN COMPONENT DECOMPOSES UPON
    HEATING ABOVE 290 TO FORM CORROSIVE GAS(ACROLEIN).

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:EVACUATE UNPROTECTED PERSONS. ELIM IGNITION
    SOURCES. STOP LEAK. H*2O SPRAY COOL CONT/DIVERT SPILL FROM
    FIRE/HEAT. PROVIDE VENTILATION. ABSORB W COMMERCIAL
    ABSORBENT,SHOVEL IN TO METAL DRUMS;LG SPILL:D IKE W COMMERCIAL
    ABSORBENT,PUMP INTO COVERED DRUMS

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN COOL,DRY,WELL VENTILATED,LOW
    FIRE RISK AREA. PROTECT FROM PHYSICAL DAMAGE. AVOID ANY PHYSICAL
    CONTACT. KEEP CONTAINERS TIGHTLY CLOSED.
Other Precautions:AVOID PROLONGED OR REPEATED CONTACT W CHEMICAL.
    REMOVE & WASH CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. DO NOT EAT OR DRINK OR SMOKE
    WHILE WITH THIS PRODUCT UNTIL AFTER APPROPRIATE PERSONAL HYGIENE.
