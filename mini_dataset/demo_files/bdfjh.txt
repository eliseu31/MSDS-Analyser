======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T STORE ABOVE 120F. STORE LARGE
    QUANTITIES IN BUILDINGS DESIGNED FOR STORAGE OF NFPA CLASS ONE
    FLAMMABLE/COMBUSTIBLES.
Other Precautions:CONTAINERS SHOULD BE GROUNDED WHEN POURING. AVOID
    FREE FALL OF LIQUID IN EXCESS OF A FEW INCHES.
