=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:IF DIFFICULT BREATHING MOVE TO FRESH AIR. CONTINUE DIFFICULT:
    GET MED AID IMMED. NO BREATH: USE CPR/GET MED HELP. EYES: FLUSH
    IMMED W/LRG AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR 15 MIN. GET MED AID. SKIN: WASH
    AFFECTED  AREA W/SOAP/WATER. REMOVE SOILED CLOTHING. GET MED AID IF
    IRRIT PERSISTS. SWALLOWED: GET MED AID IMMED. DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING. ASPIRATION OF VOMITUS CAN CAUSE CHEMICAL PNEUMONITIS
    WHICH CAN BE FATAL.
