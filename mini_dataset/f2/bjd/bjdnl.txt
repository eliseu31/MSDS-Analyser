=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF UNCON BUT BRTHING, GIVE OXYG;
    IF NOT, ADMIN CPR. KEEP WARM/REST. GET MD. EYE: REMOVE CONT LENSES.
    THOROUGHLY FLUSH EYES W/H*20 15 MIN. DRESS. DO NOT REMOVE DUST.
    CALL MD . FLASH BURNS: APPLY COLD/ICE 5-10 MIN.REPEAT. DO NOT RUB
    OR USE OINTMENT/DROPS W/O MD PRESCRIPT. GET MD. SEE SUPP DATA.
