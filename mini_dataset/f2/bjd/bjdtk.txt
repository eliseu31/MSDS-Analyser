=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:EYE & SKIN IRRITANT; MAY RESULT IN
    DERMATITIS. OTHER EFFECTS:RESPIRATORY TRACT IRRITATION, ANESTHESIA
    FROM HIGH CONCENTRATE, NAUSEA, VOMITING, LIVER & KIDNEY DAMAGE,
    ABSORPTION THROUGH SKIN, BIRTH & R EPRODUCTIVE SYS EFFECTS IN SOME
    LAB ANIMALS & BRAIN CELL DAMAGE MAY RESULT FROM LONG TERM
    INHALATION OF TOLUENE VAPOR.
Effects of Overexposure:IRRITATION TO RESPIRATORY TRACT, EYES, SKIN;
    NAUSEA, VOMITING, BREATHING DIFFICULTIES, DIZZINESS OR
    LIGHTHEADINESS FROM BREATHING HIGH VAPOR CONCENTRATION.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:DERMATITIS, RESPIRATORY TRACT
    IRRITATION, LIVER & KIDNEY DAMAGE & BRAIN CELL DAMAGE. HAS SHOWN
    BIRTH & REPRODUCTIVE SYSTEM EFFECTS IN SOME LAB ANIMALS.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE CONTACT: FLUSH WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR 15
    MINUTES. SKIN CONTACT: WASH AREA WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER &
    REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF
    BREATHING STOPS,  GIVE CPR. INGESTION: CONTACT POISON CONTROL
    CENTER IMMEDIATELY REGARDING VOMITING.  GET MEDICAL ATTENTION
    IMMEDIATELY FOR ANY OF THESE CONDITIONS.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:32 F/0 C
Extinguishing Media:USE CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, OR DRY CHEMICAL FIRE
    FIGHTING APPARTUS.
Fire Fighting Procedures:KEEP WORK AREAS CLEAR OF HOT METAL SURFACES &
    OTHER SOURCES OF IGNITION.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:USE SELF CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS. 
    WATER MAY BE UNSUITABLE AS EXTINGUISHING MEDIA BUT HELPFUL TO KEEP
    ADJACENT CONTAINERS COOL. AVOID SPREADING FIRE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:KEEP SPILL AWAY FROM IGNITE SOURCES & HOT
    METAL SURFACES. FLUSH SPILLED MATERIAL INTO RETAINING AREAS OR
    CONTAINERS WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER. SMALL AMOUNTS OF SPILLED
    MATERIAL MAY BE ABSORBED INTO  AN APPROPRIATE ABSORBANT.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP PRODUCT CONTAINERS COOL, DRY &
    AWAY FROM IGNITE SOURCES. USE & STORE WITH ADEQUATE VENTILATION.
    KEEP CONTAINERS CLOSED WHEN NOT IN USE.
Other Precautions:PERSONNEL SHOULD AVOID INHALATION OF VAPORS. 
    PERSONNEL CONTACT WITH PRODUCT SHOULD BE AVOIDED.  SHOULD CONTACT
    BE MADE, REMOVE SATURATED CLOTHING AND FLUSH AFFECTED AREAS WITH
    WATER.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:USE OF RESPIRATORY PROTECTION DEPENDS ON VAPOR
    CONCENTRATION ABOVE THE TIME-WEIGHTED TLV; USE A NIOSH APPROVED
    CARTRIDGE RESPIRATOR OR GAS MASK.
Ventilation:MECHANICAL OR LOCAL EXHAUST VENTILATION TO REDUCE TLV TO
    APPROVED LEVEL.
Protective Gloves:IMPERMEABLE GLOVES.
Eye Protection:SAFETY GLASSES,CHEM GOGGLES & FACESHIELD
Other Protective Equipment:RESPIRATORY MASKS OR ENVIRONMENTAL
