=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE/SKIN: WASH W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER, SOAP ORMILD
    DETERGENT. INGESTION: GIVE THE PERSON LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER,
    THEN INDUCE VOMITING. SEEK MED ATT. INHALATION: IF PERSON HAS
    BREATHED IN LARGE AM OUNTS, MOVE TO FRESH AIR AT ONCE. IF BREATHING
    HAS STOPPED, PERFORM ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION.
