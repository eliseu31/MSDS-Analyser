=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. RESTORE BREATHING. CALL MD.
    EYES:FLUSH IMMED WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR 15 MIN. CALL MD.
    SKIN:WASH AFFECTED AREAS WITH SOAP AND WATER. REMOVE CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHING. SEE  MD IF IRRITATION PERSISTS. INGEST:DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING. CONSULT MD OR POISON CONTROL CENTER IMMED. TREAT
    SYMPTOMATICALLY.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:CLSD CONTRS MAY EXPLODE WHEN EXPOSED
    TO EXTREME HEAT.STORE AWAY FROM HIGH TEMP,SPARKS AND FLAMES.AVOID
    PRLNG SKIN CONTACT.DO NOT BREATH SPRAY MIST.
Other Precautions:GROUND CONTRS WHILE POURING AND LIMIT FREE FALL TO A
    FEW INCHES TO PREVENT STATIC SPARKS.AVOID SPONT COMBUST OF COMTAMD
    RAGS AND OTHER IGNITABLE ORG ACCUM BY IMMED IMMERSION IN WATER.DO
    NOT CUT,PUNCTU RE OR WELD ON OR NEAR THE CONTAINER.
