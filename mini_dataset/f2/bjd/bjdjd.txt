    MINUTES. GET IMMEDIATE MEDICAL ATTEN. SKIN: WASH SKIN THOROUGHLY
    WITH SOAP AND WATER. REMOVE AND  WASH CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE.
    INHALATION:  MOVE PERSON TO FRESH AIR, IF BREATHINGHAS STOPPED GIVE
    CPR/OXYGEN. INGESTION: IF SWALLOWED DILUTE BY GIVING 2 GLASSES OF
    WATER, SEE PHYSICIAN.
