=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.RESUSCITATE IF NOT
    BREATHING.GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.EYES:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH PLENTY
    OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES.GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.SKIN:WASH WITH SOAP
    AND WATER.INGESTI ON:DILUTE WITH WATER OR MILK, THEN EMESIS IF
    INDICATED, ACTIVATED CHARCOAL AND A SALINE CATHARTIC SHOULD BE
    ADMINISTERED.
