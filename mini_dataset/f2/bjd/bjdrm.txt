=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:INHALE:ACUTE OVEREXPOS CAN LEAD TO
    CENTRAL NERV SYST DEPRESS. CHRONIC:REPORTED REPEAT/PROLONGED
    OVEREXPOS W/PERMANENT BRAIN & NERV SYST DAMAGE. OVEREXPOS TO XYLOL
    & TOLUENE IN LAB ANIMALS HAS SHOWN LI VER ABNORMALITIES, KIDNEY,
    LUNG, SPLEEN, EYE DAMAGE, ANEMIA & BLOOD ABNORMALITIES.
    HUMANS:LIVER & CARDIAC ABNORMALITIES.
Effects of Overexposure:INHALE: HEADACHES, DIZZINESS, NAUSEA AND LOSS
    OF CONSCIOUSNESS. SKIN: PROLONGED CONTACT CAN CAUSE DEFATTING &
    DRYING OF THE SKIN WHICH MAY RESULT IN SKIN IRRITATION &
    DERMATITIS. EYES: ACUTE EXPOSURES  MAY RESULT IN SLIGHT EYE
    IRRITATION. CHRONIC EXPOSURES MAY BE MORE IRRITATING.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALE: REMOVE VICTIM TO FRESH AIR & PROVIDE OXYGEN IF
    BREATHING IS DIFFICULT. GIVE CPR IF NOT BREATHING. GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION. SKIN: WASH WITH SOAP & WATER. REMOVE CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHING; REUSE ONLY  WHEN CLEANED. IF PERSISTENT IRRITATION
    OCCURS, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15
    MINUTES WHILE HOLDING EYELIDS OPEN. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:-40 F/-40 C
Lower Limits:0.8
Extinguishing Media:FOAM, CO2 OR DRY CHEMICAL.
Fire Fighting Procedures:FULL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT, INCLUDING
    SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS, IS NEEDED TO PROTECT
    FIREFIGHTERS FROM ANY HAZARDOUS COMBUSTION PRODUCTS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:CANS WILL RUPTURE FROM INTERNAL PRESSURE
    AT ABOUT 190 F AND DISCHARGE FLAMMABLE CONTENTS. WHEN HEATED TO
    DECOMPOSITION, TOXIC FUMES ARE FORMED.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:REMOVE ALL SOURCES OF IGNITION (FLAMES, HOT
    SURFACES AND ELECTRICAL, STATIC OR FRICTIONAL SPARKS). AVOID
    BREATHING VAPORS. VENTILATE AREA. REMOVE WITH INERT ABSORBENT.
    REFER TO OTHER PROTECTIVE MEASUR ES.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DO NOT STORE ABOVE 120 F. STORE LARGE
    QUANTITIES IN BUILDINGS DESIGNED & PROTECTED FOR STORAGE OF NFPA
    CLASS IA FLAMMABLE LIQUIDS.
Other Precautions:DON'T SPRAY IN EYES. DON'T PUNCTURE/INCINERATE CAN.
    DON'T STICK PIN OR ANY SHARP OBJECT INTO OPENING ON TOP OF CAN.
    FINGER MUST NOT PROTRUDE OVER FRONT OF SPRAY BUTTON. INTENDED
    MISUSE BY DELIBERATE C ONCENTRATE/INHALING MAY BE HARMFUL/FATAL

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============
