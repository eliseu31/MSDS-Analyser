First Aid:FLUSH EYES W/WATER 30 MIN.EYELIDS OPEN. GET MD. SKIN:REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING,WASH W/SOAP & WATER. GET MD.INHALATION:REMOVE
    TO FRESH AIR. ADMIN OXYGEN IF BREATHING DIFFICULT. GET MD.
    INGESTION:DO  NOT INDUCE VOMITING.GIVE MILK/WATER TO DILUTE.
    IMMEDIATELY CONSULT PHYSICIAN.
