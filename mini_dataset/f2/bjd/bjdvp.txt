=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR & CALL MD. NOTE TO MD: THERE IS NO
    SPECIFIC ANTIDOTE. INHALE: GIVE ART. RESP. IF NOT BREATHING. GIVE
    O2. EYES: FLUSH W/ H2O FOR 15 MIN. SKIN: WARM FROSTBITE AREA W/
    WATER (<105F).  MASSIVE EXPOSURE: REMOVE CLOTHES & SHOWER W/ WARM
    WATER.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Fire Fighting Procedures:USE MEDIA APPROPRIATE FOR SURROUNDING FIRE.
    EVACUATE PERSONNEL FROM DANGER. DELUG CONTAINER W/ WATER SPRAY FROM
    MAX DISTANCE TO COOL CONTAINERS/MOVE CONTAINERS
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:GAS CANNOT BURN. CONTAINER MAY RUPTURE
    DUE TO HEAT OF FIRE. KEEP CONTAINER TO TEMP <52C. MOST CONTAINERS
    WILL VENT WHEN EXPOSED TO ELEVATED TEMPS.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:EVACUATE PERSONNEL FROM DANGER AREA. USE SCBA
    IN O2 DEPLETED ATMOSPHERES. VENTILATE AREA. TEST AREA FOR
    SUFFICIENT 02 PRIOR TO RE-ENTRY OF PERSONNEL
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:USE ADEQUATE VENTILATION. MIXING 2 OR
    MORE GASES OR LIQUIFIED GASES MAY COMBINE TO CREATE UNEXPECTED
    HAZARDS. OBTAIN SAFETY INFO BEFORE MIXING
Other Precautions:NEVER WORK ON A PRESSURIZED SYS. IF THERE IS A LEAK,
    CLOSE THE CYLINDER VALVE, BLOW DOWN THE SYS BY VENTING TO A SAFE
    PLACE, REPAIR THE LEAK
