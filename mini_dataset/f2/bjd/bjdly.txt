First Aid:IF SWALLOWED, DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. CALL A PHYSICIAN
    IMMEDIATELY. IN CASE OF EYE CONTACT IRRIGATE WITH WATER FOR 15
    MINUTES. CALL A PHYSICIAN. SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. WASH
    WITH SOAP AND  WATER. IF IRRITATION PERSISTS, GET MEDICAL ADVICE.
