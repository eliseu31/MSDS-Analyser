    CONTAM CLOTHES, BEFORE REUSING.    INHAL: REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE. IF
    BREATHIN G STOPPED/DIFFICULT ADMIN CPR OR OXYGEN.SEEK MED AID.
    INGEST: DON'T INDUCE VOMIT. GIVE 1 GLASS MILK OR 1-2 OZ(30 TO

    MOUTH IF UNCONCIOUS

Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR COMPLETE FIRE SERVICE PROTECTIVE
    EQUIPMENT. FULL-FACE MSHA/NIOSH APPROVED  SCBA. USE WATER TO COOL

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN AREAS/BLDGS DESIGNED TO
