=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: IMM FLUSH W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WTR 15 MINS. SKIN: WASH
    WITH SOAP AND WATER  IF RED, ITCHING,BURNING PERSISTS,SEEK MED AID.
    WASH CONTAM CLOTHES, BEFORE REUSING. INHAL: REMOVE FROM EXPO IF
    BREATHING  DIFFICULT/STOPPED ADMIN CPR OR OXYGEN.INGEST: DON'T
    INDUCE VOMIT. GIVE 1 GLASS MILK OR 1-2 OZ (30 TO 60G) ACTIVATED  
    CHARCOAL IN WATER AS TOLERATED. SEEK MED AID. IF UNCONSCIOUS DON'T
    GIVE BY MOUTH
