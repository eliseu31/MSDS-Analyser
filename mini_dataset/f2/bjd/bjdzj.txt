=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:IN CASE OF PROLONGED SKIN CONTACT, FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF
    WATER. IN CASE OF EYE CONTACT, FLUSH EYE WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES.
    INHALATION IS UNLIKELY. IN CASE OF INGESTION, GIVE VICTIM
    APPROPRIATE DOSE O F EFFERVESCENT CALCIUM GLUCONATE (IN COPIOUS
    AMOUNT OF WATER OR MILK). CONTACT PHYSICIAN.
