=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:CAN CAUSE SEVERE IRRITATION TO EYES &
    SKIN. INHALING VAPORS CAN CAUSE NASAL & RESPIRATORY IRRIT,
    DIZZINESS, WEAKNESS, FATIGUE, NAUSEA, HEADACHE, UNCONSCIOUSNESS &
    ASPHYXIATION. SWALLOWING SUBSTANCE CA N CAUSE GASTROINTESTINGAL
    IRRIT, VOMITING, DIARRHEA, ASPIRATION OF MATERIAL INTO LUNGS,
    CHEMICAL PNEUMONITIS & DEATH.
Effects of Overexposure:EYES: IRRITATION, REDNESS, TEARING, BLURRED
    VISION. SKIN: IRRITATION, DEFATTING, DERMATITIS. BREATHING VAPORS:
    NASAL & RESPIRATORY IRRITATION, DIZZINESS, WEAKNESS, FATIGUE,
    NAUSEA, HEADACHE, UNCONSCIO USNESS, ASPHYXIATION. SWALLOWING:
    GASTROINTESTINAL IRRITATION, VOMITING & DIARRHEA.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: WASH EXPOSED AREA W/SOAP & WATER, REMOVE CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHING. WASH CONTAMINATED CLOTHING BEFORE RE-USE. EYE: FLUSH
    W/LARGE AMTS OF WATER LIFTING UPPER/LOWER LIDS OCCASIONALLY.
    SWALLOWED: DO NO T INDUCE VOMITING. KEEP PERSON WARM & QUIET.
    BREATHING VAPORS: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING DIFFICULT GIVE
    CO2. IF BREATHING STOPPED GIVE CPR. GET MEDICAL ATTEN IMMED FOR ANY
    OF THESE CONDITIONS.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ELIMINATE ALL IGNITE SOURCES. KEEP SPECTATORS
    AWAY. CONFINE & DIKE SPILL AT SOURCE TO PREVENT SPREADING, PUMP LIQ
    TO SALVAGE TANK. PREVENT CONTAMINATION OF SEWERS & GROUNDWATER.
    NOTIFY AUTHORITIES OF  SPILL.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID STORING IN HIGH TEMPERATURE OR
    NEAR OPEN FLAME. STORE DRUM OUT OF SUN & AWAY FROM HEAT.
Other Precautions:DO NOT WELD OR FLAME-CUT AN EMPTY DRUM. GROUND & BOND
    WHEN POURING, PREVENT LEAKAGE & DO NOT STORE OR USE NEAR HEAT,
    SPARK, OR FLAME. OBSERVE PRECAUTIONS FOR DISPOSAL OF HAZARDOUS
    INGREDIENTS.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:IF TLV OF PRODUCT OR ANY COMPONENT IS EXCEEDED,
