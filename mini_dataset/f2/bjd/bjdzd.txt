=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.RESUSCITATE IF NOT
    BREATHING.GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.EYES:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH PLENTY
    OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES HOLDING EYELIDS OPEN.GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION.SKIN:REMOVE CONT AMINATED CLOTHING. WASH WITH SOAP AND
    WATER.IF IRRITATION PERSISTS, GET MEDICAL ADVICE.INGESTION:INDUCE
    VOMITING.GET IMMEDIATE MEDICAL ATTENTION.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A COOL, DRY AREA.
