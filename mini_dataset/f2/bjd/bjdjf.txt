=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SWALLOWED: DRINK 1 OR 2 GLASSES OF WATER TO DILUTE. DO NOT
    INDUCE VOMIT. CALL A PHYSICIAN IMMEDIATELY. INHALE: REMOVE FROM
    EXPOSURE & CALL PHYSICIAN. IF BREATH HAS STOPPED OR IRREGULAR,
    START CPR OR A DMINISTER OXYGEN. IF SPLASHED ON SKIN ORCLOTHES,
    REMOVE CLOTHES & WASH SKIN WITH SOAP AND WATER. IF SPLASHED INTO
    EYES, FLUSH WITH WATER AND CONSULT PHYSICIAN.
