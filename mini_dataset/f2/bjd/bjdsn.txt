=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:THORO WASH AREA W/SOAP & WATER. REMOVE CONTAM CLOTHES.
    LAUNDER CLOTHES BEFORE RE-USE. EYES:FLUSH W/LG AMTS OF WATER
    LIFTING LIDS, GET MED ATTN. SWALLOW:DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING, KEEP
    PERSON WARM, Q UIET & GET MED ATTN. ASPIRATION DUE TO VOMITING CAN
    CAUSE CHEM PNEUMONITIS WHICH CAN BE FATAL. INHALE:REMOVE PERSON TO
    FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING DIFF, ADMIN OXYGEN. IF STOPPED ADMIN CPR.
    GET MED ATTN.
