=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:THOROUGHLY WASH EXPOSED AREA W/SOAP & WATER. REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING; LAUNDER BEFORE RE-USE. REMOVE CONTAMINATED
    SHOES PROMPTLY & DISCARD.  EYES:FLUSH W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER.
    SWALLOWED:DO  NOT INDUCE VOMITING. KEEP PERSON WARM, QUITE & GET
    MEDICAL ATTENTION.
