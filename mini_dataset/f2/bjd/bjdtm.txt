Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:CENTRAL NERVOUS SYSTEM DEPRESSION AND
    NARCOTIC EFFECTS.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:PERCHLOROETHYLENE IS LISTED AS A

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGESTION: GET MEDICAL HELP. EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR
    MINIMUM OF 5 MINUTES. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. CALL A
    PHYSICIAN. GIVE MOUTH-TO-MOUTH RESUCITATION IF BREATHING STOPS. AND
    SKIN: WASH WIT H WATER.
