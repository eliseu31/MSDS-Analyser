=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    EYES:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. GET
    MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN: WASH WITH SOAP ANSD WATER,REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING.  LAUNDER BEFORE RESUE THE CLOTHING; INGEST:
    TOXIC BY INGESTION. INDUCE VOMITING IMMEDIATELY AND CONSULT
    MEDICAL.
