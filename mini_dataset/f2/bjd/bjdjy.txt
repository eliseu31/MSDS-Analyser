=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: FLUSH W/PLENTY WATER 15 MIN. LIFTING LIDS. GET MED ATTN.
    INHAL:REMOVE VICTIM TO FRESH AIR & PROVIDE OXYGEN/CPR IF BREATH IS
    DIFF. GET MED ATTN. SKIN: FLUSHWITH SOAP & WATER, REMOVE CONTAM
    CLOTHIN G. DO NOT REUSE UNTIL LAUNDERED. INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMIT.IF VOMIT OCCURS SPONTANEOUSLY, KEEP HEAD BELOW HIPS TO
    PREVENT ASPIRATION OF LIQ INTO THE LUNGS GET MED ATTN. CONSCIOUS
    ADMIN PINT WATER
