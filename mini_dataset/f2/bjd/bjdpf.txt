=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:REMOVE CONTAM CLOTHING&FLUSH W/WATER. INHAL:REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR.GET MD IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST.EYES:FLUSH IMMED WITH WATER
    FOR SEVERAL MINUTES.INGEST:IF CONSCIOUS, GIVE TWO GLASSES OF WATER
    AND INDUC E VOMIT.GET MD IMMED.IF MEDICAL ADVICE IS DELAYED, AND
    THE PERSON HAS SWALLOWED MODERATE VOLUMES OF ETHYLENE GLYCOL (A FEW
    OUNCES), THEN GIVE THREE TO FOUR OUNCES OF HARD LIQUOR SUCH AS
    WHISKEY.
