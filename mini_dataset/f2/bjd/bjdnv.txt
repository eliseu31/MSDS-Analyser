=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES- FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER. CALL A PHYSICIAN IF
    IRRITATION PERSISTS.  SKIN- WASH AFFECTED AREA WITH SOAP AND WATER.
     INHALATION- REMOVE PERSON TO FRESH AIR.  INGESTION- NONE NORMALLY
    REQUIRED.

Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:NO UNUSUAL FIRE OR EXPLOSION HAZARDS ARE

    CONTAINER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A COOL, DRY, WELL VENTILATED
    AREA.
Other Precautions:DO NOT BREATHE DUST/MIST. AVOID CONTACT WITH EYES.
    USE WITH ADEQUATE VENTILATION. WASH THOROUGHLY AFTER HANDLING.
