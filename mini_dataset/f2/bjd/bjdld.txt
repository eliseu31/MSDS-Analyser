=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:FIRST AID-EYE CONTACT:FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER.SEE DOCTOR
    IMMEDIATELY.SKIN CONTACT:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING.WASH WITH
    SOAP AND WATER.IF IRRITATION PERSISTS.SEE DOCTOR.INHALATION:REMOVE
    TO FRESH  AIR.GIVE OXYGEN/CPR IF NEEDED,SEE DOCTOR.INGESTION:FLUSH
    MOUTH WITH WATER.SEE DOCTOR.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:USE WATER FOG, CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, OR DRY
    CHEMICAL AS APPROPIATE FOR SURROUNDING FIRE AND MATERIALS.
Fire Fighting Procedures:FIRE FIGHTERS SHOULD USE NIOSH APPROVED SCBA &
    FULL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT WHEN FIGHTING CHEMICAL FIRE. USE WATER
    SPRAY TO COOL NEARBY CONTAINERS EXPOSED TO FIRE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:DUST MAY BE EXPLOSIVE IF MIXED WITH AIR
    IN CRITICAL PROPORTIONS AND IN THE PRESENCE OF AN IGNITION SOURCE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WEAR APPROVED RESPIRATOR AND GLOVES. VACUUM OR
    SWEEP UP SPILLAGE. AVOID CREATING DUST. PLACE SPILLAGE IN
    APPROPIATE CONTAINER FOR WASTE DISPOSAL. WASH CONTAMINATED CLOTHING
    BEFORE REUSE. VENTILATE ARE A AND WASH SPILL SITE.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN AIRTIGHT CONTAINER. PROTECT
    FROM LIGHT AND EXTREME HEAT. USE WITH ADEQUATE DUST CONTROL.
Other Precautions:AVOID CONTACT WITH EYES, SKIN OR CLOTHING. AVOID
    BREATHING DUST.
