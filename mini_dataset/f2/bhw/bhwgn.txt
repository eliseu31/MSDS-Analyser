=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES.CONSULT A
    PHYSICIAN.SKIN:WASH WITH SOAP AND WATER.GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IF
    ADVERSE EFFECTS ARISE.INGESTION:DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.CONSULT A
    PHYSICIAN.INHA LATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.ADMINISTER OXYGEN OR
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION AS NEEDED.
