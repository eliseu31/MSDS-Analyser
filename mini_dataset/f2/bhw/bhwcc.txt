=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:YES    IARC:YES	  OSHA:YES
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:ADVERSE EFFECTS TO THE BLOOD FORMING,
    NERVOUS, URINARY, REPROD SYSTEMS EFFECTS. TYPICAL X-RAY CHANGES &
    CHRONIC LUNG DISEASE. DISTURBANCE OF SLEEP & FATIGUE. REOCCURING
    OVEREXP MAY RESULT IN LIVER & K IDNEY INJURY. SECONDARY ANEMIA WITH
    & INCREASE IN WHITE BLOOD CELLS; FATTY DEGENER, SWELLING, EXCESS OF
    BLOOD IN ORGANS
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:SHOWN MUTAGENIC ACTIVITY IN LAB CELL
    CULTURE TESTS. CANCER  HAZARD BASED ON TESTS W/LAB ANIMALS. MAY
    CREATE CANCER RISKS
Effects of Overexposure:MAY CAUSE NOSE & THROAT IRRITATION, NERVOUS
    SYSTEM DEPRESSION CHARACTERIZED BY; HEADACHE, DIZZINESS, NAUSEA,
    STAGGERING GAIT, CONFUSION, UNCONSCIOUSNESS, REPEAT/PROLONGED
    OVEREXPOSURE TO SOLVENTS WITH  PERMANENT BRAIN & NERVOUS SYSTEM
    DAMAGEIRRITATE OR BURNING OF EYES. SKIN; IRRITATION WITH DISCOMFORT
    AND DERMATITIS.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR, NO BREATHING GIVE
    CPR/PREFERABLY MOUTH-TO-MOUTHBREATH IS DIFF, CONSULT PHYSICIAN.
    INGESTION: GASTRO-INTEST DISTRESS. CALL PHYSICIAN IMMED & HAVE
    NAMES OF INGREDIENTS A VAILABLE. SKIN: FLUSH IMMED WITH PLENTYOF
    WATER 15 MIN. WASH WITH SOAP & WATER, IF IRRITATION OCCURS, CONTACT
    PHYSICIANEYES: FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES,
    CONTACT A PHYSICIAN.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:CC
Flash Point:20 F/ -7 C
Lower Limits:.8
Upper Limits:36.5
Extinguishing Media:FOAM, CARBON DIOXIDE, DRY CHEMICAL.

    PRE-  VENT SKIN CONTACT AND BREATHING OF VAPOR. CONFINE AND REMOVE
    WITH INERT ABSORBENT.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:OBSERVE LABEL PRECAUTIONS. KEEP AWAY
    FROM HEAT,	 SPARKS AND FLAME. CLOSE CONTAINER AFTER EACH USE.
    GROUND CONTAINERS WHEN POURING.
Other Precautions:WASH THOROUGHLY AFTER HANDLING AND BEFORE EATING OR
    SMOKING. DO NOT STORE ABOVE 120 F. DO NOT SAND, FLAME CUT, BRAZE OR
    WELD DRY COATING WITHOUT A NIOSH/MSHA   APPROVED RESPIRATOR OR
    APPROPRIATE VENTI LATION.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:DO NOT BREATHE VAPORS OR MISTS. WEAR A
    NIOSH/MSHA (TC-23C) FOR USE WITH PAINTS  DURING APPLICATION AND
    UNTIL ALL VAPORS AND SPRAY MISTS ARE EXHAUSTED. FOLLOW   THE
    RESPIRATOR MANUFACTURER'S DIRECTIONS  FOR RESPIRATOR USE.
Ventilation:SUFFICIENT VENT IN VOL/PATTERN, TO KEEP CONTAM BELOW APPLI-
    CABLE OSHA REQUIREMENTS AND OTHER SUGGESTED EXPOSURE LIMITS.
Protective Gloves:NEOPRENE GLOVES & COVERALLS RECOMMENDED.
Eye Protection:SAFETY GLASSES/SPLASH GUARDS/SIDE SHIELD
Other Protective Equipment:GOGGLES ARE PREFERRED TO PREVENT EYE
    IRRITATION. DON'T USE  PROTECTIVE CREAMS FOR PROTECTION, USE FOR
    EASE OF CLEAN-UP.
Supplemental Safety and Health
APPLICATION: AIR OR AIRLESS EQUIPMENT MAY BE USED. PAINTS MAY BE HEATED
