=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:ACUTE: INHALATION OF VAPORS MAY
    CAUSE/MISTS MAY CAUSE IRRITATION OF NOSE, THROAT & RESPIRATORY
    TRACT. CONTACT MAY IRRITATE EYES. INGESTION MAY BE HARMFUL OR
    FATAL; ASPIRATION MAY CAUSE CHEMICAL PNEUMO NITIS. CHRONIC:
    PROLONGED/REPEATED EXPOSURE MAY CAUSE SKIN DISORDERS SUCH AS
    DERMATITIS, FOLLICULITIS OR OIL ACNE.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:THE PRODUCT CONTAINS NO INGREDIENT THAT
    IS LISTED BY IARC, NTP OR OSHA AS A CARCINOGEN.
Effects of Overexposure:EYE: IRRITATION. SKIN: IRRITATION, DERMATITIS,
    OIL ACNE, FOLLICULITIS. INHALED: (HIGH TEMP VAPORS) IRRITATION TO
    NOSE, THROAT. INGESTED: NAUSEA, VOMITING, ASPIRATION INTO LUNGS.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:PERSONS WITH A HISTORY OF AILMENTS
    OR WITH A PRE-EXISTING DISEASE INVOLVING THE EYES, SKIN, OR
    RESPIRATORY TRACT MAY BE AT INCREASED RISK FROM EXPOSURE.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:IN ALL CASES GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. EYES-FLUSH WITH WATER FOR
    15 MINUTES. SKIN-REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHES. WASH WITH MILD SOAP &
    WATER. INHALED-REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE OXYGEN OR ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRAT ION AS NEEDED. INGESTED-DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING! IN GENERAL
    NO TREATMENT IS NEEDED UNLESS LARGE QUANTITIES INGESTED; HOWEVER,
    GET MEDICAL ADVICE.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:430F,221C
Extinguishing Media:USE WATER FOG, CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, OR DRY
    CHEMICAL. DO NOT USE DIRECT STREAM OF WATER; OIL WILL FLOAT & MAY
    REIGNITE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:DO NOT ENTER ENCLOSED SPACE WITHOUT FULL
    BUNKER GEAR INCLUDING POSITIVE PRESSURE NIOSH APPROVED SCBA. USE
    WATER SPRAY TO COOL NEARBY CONTAINERS EXPOSED TO FIRE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:MATERIAL WILL NOT BURN UNLESS PRE-HEATED.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:MAY BURN. USE CAUTION. WEAR PROTECTIVE
    EQUIPMENT. SHUT OFF LEAK IF W/O RISK. DIKE & CONTAIN. REMOVE WITH
    VACUUM TRUCK OR PUMP TO STORAGE/SALVAGE VESSELS. SOAK UP REMAINDER
    WITH SAND, CLAY, ETC. FLUSH  AREA W/ WATER. SMALL SPILL: ABSORB
    WITHABSORBENT.
Neutralizing Agent:NO INFORMATION GIVEN ON MSDS BY SUPPLIER

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A COOL, DRY, WELL VENTILATED
    AREA. PROTECT CONTAINERS FROM HEAT AND FLAMES.
