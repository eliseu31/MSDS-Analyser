=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:FLUSH WITH LOTS OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES HOLDING LIDS
    OPEN.GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.HOT PRODUCT:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH COOL
    WATER FOR 15 MINUTES & GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.SKIN:REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED CLOTH ING/SHOES,WIPE.FLUSH WITH WATER,WASH WITH SOAP &
    WATER.IF IRRIT:GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.HOT PRODUCT:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH
    WITH COOL WATER FOR 15 MINUTES.CAREFULLY REMOVE CLOTHING,IF
    STUCK,CUT (SEE ING 5,6)
