=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

LD50 LC50 Mixture:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.
Routes of Entry: Inhalation:NO	Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:ACUTE: IRRITATING TO THE EYE. MAY
    CAUSE BURNS TO SKIN.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:THIS PRODUCT IS NOT LISTED BY IARC, NTP,
    OR OSHA AS A CARCINOGEN.
Effects of Overexposure:SKIN CONTACT WITH MOISTURE OR PERSPIRATION MAY
    CAUSE BURNS DUE TO THE HEAT OF REACTION. EYE CONTACT CAN CAUSE
    IRRITATION. INHALATION CAN CAUSE IRRITATION TO THE MUCOUS
    MEMBRANES. INGESTION CAN CAUSE B URNS OF MOUTH AND ESOPHOGUS.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:NONE

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES
    HOLDING EYELIDS OPEN. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN:WIPE EXCESS METAL
    FROM SKIN. WASH SKIN WITH SOAP AND WATER. REMOVE CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHING. I F IRRITATION PERSISTS, GET MEDICAL ADVICE. INGESTION:DO
    NOT INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE NOTHING BY MOUTH IF UNCONSCIOUS. GET
    IMMEDIATE MEDICAL ATTENTION

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NONE
Extinguishing Media:G-1 POWDER, "PYRENE",DRY LIME (NOT LIMESTONE). DO
    NOT USE WATER.SPECIAL MIXTURES OF DRY CHEM IS ONLY EXTINGUISHING
    AGENT
Fire Fighting Procedures:DO NOT USE WATER. FIRE FIGHTERS SHOULD USE
    NIOSH APPROVED SCBA & FULL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT WHEN FIGHTING
    CHEMICAL FIRE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:EVOLVES HYDROGEN GAS WHEN HEATED OR IN
    CONTACT WITH ACIDS,MOISTURE. FINELY DIVIDED CALCIUM IS CONSIDERED
    PYROPHORIC AND WILL CAUSE EXPLOSION WHEN IGNITED

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WEAR PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT AND CLOTHING.
    COLLECT SPILLED MATERIAL FOR RECLAMATION OR DISPOSAL IN SEALED
    CONTAINERS. AVOID INHALATION OF DUST. AVOID CONTACT WITH WATER.
Neutralizing Agent:NOT APPLICABLE.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A COOL, DRY, WELL VENTILATED
    AREA. KEEP CONTAINERS TIGHTLY CLOSED WHEN NOT IN USE. NO SMOKING OR
    OPEN FLAME WHEN OPENING CANS/DRUMS.
Other Precautions:DO NOT TAKE INTERNALLY. DO NOT BREATHE DUST. AVOID
    CONTACT WITH EYES AND SKIN. WASH THOROUGHLY AFTER HANDLING. AVOID
    CONTACT WITH SKIN AND CLOTHING. AVOID EXPOSURE TO MOISTURE/WATER.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:USE NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED DUST RESPIRATOR IN DUSTY
    ENVIRONMENTS.
Ventilation:MECHANICAL (GENERAL) ROOM VENTILATION IS RECOMMENDED TO
    EXHAUST AWAY THE EVOLVED HYDROGEN FROM STORAGE OR WORK AREAS.
Protective Gloves:LEATHER OR RUBBER GLOVES.
Eye Protection:SAFETY GLASSES OR GOGGLES
Other Protective Equipment:EYE WASH STATION.
Work Hygienic Practices:OBSERVE GOOD PERSONAL HYGIENE PRACTICES AND
