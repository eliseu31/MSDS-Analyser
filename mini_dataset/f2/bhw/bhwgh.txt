=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH EYES WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES,GET
    MEDICAL ATTENTION.SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING.WASH AFFECTED
    AREA WITH WATER.INGESTION:IF PERSON IS ALERT,GIVE AMPLE WATER AND
    INDUCE VOM ITING.GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH
    AIR.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:CARBON DIOXIDE,DRY CHEMICAL,FOAM.
Fire Fighting Procedures:FIREFIGHTER SHOULD WEAR SELF-CONTAINED
    BREATHING APPARATUS.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SMALL SPILL:WIPE UP OR ABSORB WITH VERMICULITE
    OR OTHER ABSORBANT.COLLECT IN SEALED CONTAINERS.WASH AREA WITH
    SOAPY WATER.RINSE.LARGE SPILL:DIKE AREA.SHOVEL OR PUMP TO
    DRUM/SALVAGE TANK.ABSORB RESIDUE  WITH SAND,VERMICULITE,OTHER
    ABSORBENT (SEE SUPP)
Neutralizing Agent:N/K
