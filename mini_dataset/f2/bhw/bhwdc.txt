=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:ACUTE: CONTACT WITH SKIN AND EYES MAY
    CAUSE IRRITATION AND/OR BURNS. INHALATION OF DUST MAY CAUSE
    IRRITATION OF NOSE AND THROAT. INGESTION MAY CAUSE BURNS TO MUCOUS
    MEMBRANES OF MOUTH, THROAT AND STOM ACH MAY OCCUR. CHRONIC:
    PROLONGED INHALATION MAY CAUSE LUNG DAMAGE.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:NO INGREDIENT OF A CONCENTRATION OF 0.1%
    OR GREATER IS LISTED AS A CARCINOGEN OR SUSPECTED CARCINOGEN.
Effects of Overexposure:EYES-REDNESS, IRRITATION. SKIN-REDNESS, BURNS.
    INHALED-COUGH, CHEST DISCOMFORT. INGESTED-BURNS TO MUCOUS
    MEMBRANES, STOMACH PAIN.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:ASTHMA AND INFLAMMATORY OR FIBROTIC
    PULMONARY DISEASE.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALED-REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. EYES-FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15
    MINUTES. SKIN-WASH WITH SOAP & WATER. INGESTED-IF CONSCIOUS, DRINK
    2 GLASSES OF WATER. CONTACT PHYSICIAN TO GET PERMISSION TO INDUCE
    VOMITING.  NOTE TO PHYSICIAN: PRODUCT IS A DESSICCANT AND MAY
    PRODUCE HEAT IF IT ADORBS WATERS. THE USED PRODUCT MAY CONTAIN
    MATERIAL OF A HAZARDOUS NATURE. IDENTIFY THAT MATERIAL AND TREAT
    SYMPTOMATICALLY.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NON-FLAMMABLE
Extinguishing Media:USE MEDIA APPROPRIATE FOR SURROUNDING FIRE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS AND
    FULL PROTECTIVE GERA.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SWEEP UP THE SPILL AREA. COLLECT AND PLACE THE
    SPILLED MATERIAL IN A WASTE DISPOSAL CONTAINER. AVOID RAISING DUST.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:USE WITH ADEQUATE VENTILATION. OPEN
    CONTAINER SLOWLY. AVOID BREATHING DUST. AVOID CONTACT WITH EYES AND
    SKIN.
Other Precautions:DO NOT PUT IN MOUTH OR POUR LIQUID INTO PRODUCT;
    BURNS MAY RESULT. DUST MAY IRRITATE EYES, NOSE, THROAT AND SKIN.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:IF ENGINEERING CONTROLS FAIL OR NON-ROUTINE USE
    OR AN EMERGENCY OCCURS; WEAR AN MSHA/NIOSH APPROVED RESPIRATOR WITH
    DUST CARTRIDGE OR AN AIR-SUPPLIED RESPIRATOR OR SCBA, AS REQUIRED.
    USE IAW 29 CFR 19 10.134.
Ventilation:USE ADEQUATE MECHANICAL VENTILATION OR LOCAL EXHAUST TO
    MAINTAIN EXPOSURE BELOW TLV(S).
Protective Gloves:WORK GLOVES.
Eye Protection:SAFETY GLASSES/CHEMICAL SPLASH GOGGLES.
Other Protective Equipment:SELECT IAW 29 CFR 1910.132 & 1910.133.
Work Hygienic Practices:WASH HANDS AFTER HANDLING AND BEFORE EATING,
    DRINKING, OR SMOKING. LAUNDER CONTAMINATED CLOTHES BEFORE REUSE.
Supplemental Safety and Health
AVOID PROLONGED OR REPEATED EXPOSURE. DO NOT GET ON SKIN OR IN EYES.
