=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:FLUSH WITH CLEAN,LUKEWARM,LOW PRESSURE WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MINUTES,OCCASIONALLY LIFTING LIDS.GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION.SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING.WASH THOROUGHLY WITH
    SOAP & WATER.INGEST:DO  NOT INDUCE VOMIT.GIVE NOTHING TO
    UNCONSCIOUS PERSON.GET MED ATTN.INHAL:REMOVE FROM EXPOS.RESTORE
    BREATH.ASTHMATIC SYMPTOMS MAY BE IMMED/DELAYED SEVERAL HOURS.GET
    MEDICAL ATTENTION.
