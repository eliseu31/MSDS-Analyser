=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH EYES WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES.CALL
    PHYSICIAN.SKIN:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES.REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING.CALL PHYSICIAN.INGESTION:DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING.IF CO NSCIOUS GIVE WATER.GIVE 1 OUNCE OF MILKOF MAGNESIA
    IN 1 OUNCE OF WATER OR THREE EGG WHITES.CALL
    PHYSICIAN.INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.GIVE ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRATION IF NECESSARY.CALL PHYSICIAN.
