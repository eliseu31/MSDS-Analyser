=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE:FLUSH WITH CLEAR WATER FOR 15 MINUTES OR UNTIL IRRITATION
    SUBSIDES; SKIN:REMOVE ANY CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND WASH SKIN
    THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP & WATER; INHALE:UNLIKELY, IF OVERCOME BY VAPOR
    FROM HOT  PRODUCT,REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE AND CALL PHYSICAIN, ADMISTER
    OXYGEN IF NEEDED; INGEST:DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING, CALL A PHYSICIAN
    IMMEDIATELY.
