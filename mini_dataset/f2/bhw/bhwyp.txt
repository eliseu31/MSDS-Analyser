=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION:REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE, TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY;
    EYES/SKIN:FLUSH WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES. GET MEDICAL ASSISTANCE IF EYE IRRITATION PERSISTS OR
    ALLERGIC SKIN REACTION  DEVELOPS; INGESTION:IF SWALLOWED, INDUCE
    VOMITING IMMEDIATELY AS DIRECTED BY MEDICAL PERSONNEL. CALL A
    PHYSICIAN OR POISON CONTROL CENTER IMMEDIATELY. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING
    BY MOUTH TO A UNCONSCIOUS.
