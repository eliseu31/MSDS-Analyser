    SUBSIDES. IF IRRITATION PERSISTS, CALL A PHYSICIAN. SKIN CONTACT:
    REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND WASH THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP & WATER.
    INHAL ATION: IF OVERCOME BY VAPORS, REMOVE TOFRESH AIR, CALL A
    PHYSICIAN. INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING, CALL A PHYSICIAN
