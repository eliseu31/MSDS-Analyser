=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.RESTORE RESPIRATION IF
    NECESSARY. EYE CONTACT:  FLUSH IMMEDIATELY W/COPIOUS QTYS OF WTR
    FOR 15 MINS MINIMUM. CONSULT PHYSICIAN. DERMAL CONTACT: WASH
    AFFECTED AREA W/SOA P & WTR.REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING CONSULT
    PHYSICIAN IS IRRITATION PERSISTS. INGESTION: DRINK 1/2 GLASSES WTR
    TO DILUTE.DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.IF VOMITING OCCURS KEEP HEAD BELOW
    KNEE LEVEL.
