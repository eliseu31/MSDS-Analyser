=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR; EYES/SKIN:FLUSH WITH LARGE
    AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES.REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND
    SHOES. GET MEDICAL HELP IF SYMPTOMS ARE PRESENT AFTER FLUSHING;
    INGESTION:IF  SWALLOWED, GIVE 1-2 GLASSES OF WATER. SEEK MED HELP.
