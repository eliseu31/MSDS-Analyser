=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SWALLOWING: INDUCE VOMIT IMMED BY GIVING TWO GLASSES
    WATER/STICKING FINGER DOWN THROAT. GET MED ATTN. INHALATION: REMOVE
    TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING STOPPED GIVECPR. GET MED ATTN. EYE:
    FLUSH WITH LARGE  AMOUNTS OF WATER, LIFTING/LOWERING
    LIDSOCCASIONALLY. CONTINUE FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN. GET MED ATTN. SKIN:
    WASH AFFECTED   AREA WITH SOAP AND WATER. GET MED ATTN IF
    IRRITATION PERSISTS.
