=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:NO  Ingestion:NO
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:INHALATION CAN OCCUR THROUGH NUISANCE
    DUST. EXPOSURE TO GLASS FIBERS SOMETIMES CAUSES IRRITATION OF THE
    SKIN AND, LESS FRQUENTLY, IRRITATION OF THE EYES, NOSE OR THROAT.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:NONE OF THE COMPOUNDS IN THIS PRODUCT IS
    LISTED BY IARC, NTP, OR OSHA AS A CARCINOGEN.
Effects of Overexposure:RASPY THROAT, REDNESS AND IRRITATION OF THE
    SKIN.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:PERSONS WITH A HISTORY OF AILMENTS
    OR WITH A PRE-EXISTING DISEASE INVOLVING THE RESPIRATORY TRACT MAY
    BE AT INCREASED RISK FROM EXPOSURE.
