    AND CNS EFFECTS

Effects of Overexposure:INHALATION: COUGHING, WHEEZING. EYES: REDNESS,

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES AND SKIN: FLUSH WITH LARGE AMOUNT OF WATER FOR ABOUT
    15-20 MINUTES; SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION. INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH
    AIR. INGESTION:SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.
