=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:FLUSH WITH CLEAN,LUKEWARM,LOW PRESSURE H*2O FOR AT LEAST
    15 MINUTES,OCCASIONALLY LIFTING LIDS.GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION.SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING.WASH THOROUGHLY WITH
    SOAP & WATER.INGESTION:D O NOT INDUCE VOMIT.GIVE NOTHING TO
    UNCONSCIOUS PERSON.GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.INHALATION:REMOVE FROM
    EXPOSURE.RESTORE BREATH.ASTHMATIC SYMP MAY BE IMMED OR DELAYED
    SEVERAL HOURS.GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN BUILDING COMPLYING WITH OSHA
    1910.106.AVOID STORAGE NEAR HIGH TEMPERATURES,OPEN FLAMES,SPARK
    SOURCES.STORE IN TIGHTLY CLOSED CONTAINERS.
Other Precautions:STORE IN WELL VENTILATED AREAS.KEEP CONTAINERS TIGHT
    & UPRIGHT.PREVENT PROLONGED INHALATION.AVOID SKIN/EYE CONTACT.DO
    NOT TAKE INTERNALLY.READ & UNDERSTAND MANUFACTURERS SAFETY
    PRECAUTIONS BEFORE HAND LING.SMOKE IN SMOKING AREAS ONLY.
