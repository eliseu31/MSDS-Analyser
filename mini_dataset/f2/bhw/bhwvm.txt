=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:MATERIAL IS AN IRRITANT. EYE CONTACT:
    MAY CAUSE BURNING, TEARING, REDNESS, & SWELLING.  SKIN CONTACT: MAY
    CAUSE SKIN IRRITATION, REDNESS, BURNING, DRYING, & CRACKING.
    BREATHING: MAY CAUSE IRRITATION O F THE NOSE AND THROAT AND SIGNS
    OF NERVOUS DEPRESSION (E.G. DROWSINESS, DIZZINESS, LOSS OF
    COORDINATION & FATIGUE).  MAY CAUSE LIVER & KIDNEY DAMAGE. 
    INGESTION: MAY CAUSE IRRITATION OF THE DIGESTIVE  TRACT & SIGNS OF
    NERVOUS DEPRESSION.  ASPIRATION HAZARD - THIS MATERIAL CAN ENTER
    LUNGS DURING SWALLOWING OR VOMITING AND CAUSE LUNG INFLAMMATION AND
    DAMAGE.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:THIS SUBSTANCE HAS NOT BEEN IDENTIFIED
    AS A CARCINOGEN OR PROBABLE CARCINOGEN BY NTP, IARC OR OSHA.
Effects of Overexposure:EYE CONTACT: IRRITATION, BURNING, TEARING,
    REDNESS & SWELLING.  SKIN CONTACT: IRRITATION, REDNESS, BURNING,
    DRYING & CRACKING.	INHALATION: MAY CAUSE IRRITATION, LIVER DAMAGE
    & KIDNEY DAMAGE.  INGESTI ON: IRRITATION OF THE DIGESTIVE TRACT &
    SIGNS OF NERVOUS DEPRESSION (E.G. DROWSINESS, DIZZINESS, LOSS OF
    COORDINATION & FATIGUE)
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:PERSONS WITH A HISTORY OF AILMENTS
    OR WITH A PRE-EXISTING DISEASE INVOLVING THE EYES, SKIN, OR
    RESPIRATORY TRACT MAY BE AT INCREASED RISK FROM EXPOSURE.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:  IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES.
     SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION.  SKIN: REMOVE CONTAIMINATED CLOTHING. 
    WASH WITH SOAP AND WATER.  IF IRRITATION PERSISTS, SEEK MEDICAL
    ATTENTION.	 INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. RESUSCITATE IF NOT
    BREATHING. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.  INGESTION: SEEK EMERGENCY
    MEDICAL ATTENTION.	INDUCE VOMITING IF VICTIM IS CONSCIOUS AND
    ALERT.  GIVE NOTHING B Y MOUTH IF UNCONSCIOUS.  IF POSSIBLE, DO NOT
    LEAVE VICTIM UNATTENDED.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:=-40.C, -40.F
Lower Limits:1.8
Upper Limits:12.0
Extinguishing Media:SMALL FIRES:  DRY CHEMICAL, CARBON DIOXIDE OR FOAM.
     LARGE FIRES: DRY CHEMICAL OR FOAM.  WATER MAY BE INEFFECTIVE ON
    THIS PRODUCT.
Fire Fighting Procedures:THE USE OF SCBA IS RECOMMENDED FOR FIRE
    FIGHTERS.  WATER SPAY MAY BE USEFUL TO MINIMIZE VAPORS AND COOLING
    CONTAINERS BUT USE CAUTION TO PREVENT SPREADING OF BURNING LIQUID
    BY THE COOLING WATER.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:THIS MATERIAL IS FLAMMABLE AND MAY BE
    IGNITED BY HEAT, SPARKS, FLAME OR STATIC ELECTRICITY.  HEATED CAN
    MAY BURST.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:STAY UPWIND & AWAY FROM SPILL.  KEEP ALL
    SOURCES OF IGNITION AND HOT METAL SURFACES AWAY FROM SPILL.  IF
    SPILL IS INDOORS, VENTILATE AREA OF SPILL.	FOAM MAY BE USED TO
    SUPPRESS VAPORS.  KEEP OUT OF D RAINS, SEWERS OR WATERWAYS.  USE
    SAND OR INERT MATERIAL TO DAM OR CONTAIN SPILL.  DO NOT FLUSH AREA
    WITH WATER; USE ABSORBENT PADS.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP CONTAINERS TIGHTLY CLOSED, COOL,
    DRY & AWAY FROM SOURCES OF IGNITION.  USE & STORE THIS PRODUCT WITH
    ADEQUATE VENTILATION.  DO NOT PRESSURIZE, CUT, WELD, BRAZE, SOLDER,
    DRILL, GRIND OR EXPOSE SUC H CONTAINERS TO HEAT, FLAMES, OR SPARKS.
Other Precautions:EMPTY CONTAINERS RETAIN RESIDUE AND CAN BE DANGEROUS.
    DO NOT PRESSURIZE, CUT, WELD, ETC. ON THE CONTAINER.  BEFORE
    WORKING ON OR IN TANKS WHICH CONTAIN OR HAVE CONTAINED THIS
    PRODUCT, REFER TO OSHA RE GULATIONS AND/OR ANSI Z49.1 PERTAINING TO
    CLEANING, REPAIRING, WELDING OR OTHER CONTEMPLATED OPERATI ONS.
