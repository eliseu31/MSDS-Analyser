=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES;
    INGESTION:IF SWALLOWED AND CONSCIOUS,RINSE MOUTH & INDUCE VOMITING
    IMMEDIATELY BY GIVING 1 OR 2 GLASSES OF WATER NEVER GIVE ANYTHING
    BY MOUTH T O AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. CALL A PHYSICIAN OR POISON
    CONTROL CENTER IMMEDIATELY; SKIN: WASH AFTER EACH CONTACT. GET
    MEDICAL HELP IF SYMPTOMS OCCUR.
