=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:FLUSH WITH LOTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES,LIFTING
    LIDS OCCASIONALLY.GET MEDICAL AID.SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHING.WASH THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP & WATER.IF IRRIT OCCURS,GET
    MEDICAL AID.INGE STION:DO NOT INDUCE VOMIT.GET MEDICAL
    AID.INHALATION:REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE.IF BREATH IS STOPPED OR
    DIFFICULT,ADMINISTER ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION OR OXYGEN AS
    INDICATED.IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST,CONSULT MD.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:REMOVE ALL IGNITION SOURCES.INCREASE
    VENTILATION TO DISPERSE VAPOR.DIKE LARGE SPILLS TO PREVENT RUNOFF
    INTO SEWERS/WATERWAYS.REMOVE MATERIAL BY
    SHOVELING,WIPING,ETC.SWEEP/CLEAN WITH INERT MATERIALS (S
    AND,VERMICULITE,ETC) & PLACE IN CLOSED CONTAINER.
Neutralizing Agent:N/K 

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID PROLONGED/REPEATED SKIN
    CONTACT.AVOID EYE CONTACT.STORE IN COOL,DRY AREA OUT OF DIRECT
    SUNLIGHT.USE ADEQUATE VENTILATION.
Other Precautions:BOND & GROUND CONTAINERS FOR TRANSFER TO PREVENT
    STATIC SPARKS.STYRENE VAPORS ARE UNINHIBITED & MAY CAUSE BLOCKAGE
    OF VENTS DUE TO POLYMERIZATION (PREVENT WITH PERIODIC
    INSPECTION).AVOID IMPROPER ADDI TION OF PROMOTERS & OXIDIZING
    AGENTS.
