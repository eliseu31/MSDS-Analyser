    ATTENTION.SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING.WASH AFFECTED AREA
    THOROUGHLY WITH WATER.INGESTION:DILUTE WITH LARGE QUANTITIES OF
    WATER.INDUCE  VOMITING ONLY IF VICTIM IS CONSCIOUS AND ALERT.GET
    IMMEDIATE MEDICAL ATTENTION.INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
