=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:ACUTE: INHALATION: IRRITATION OF THE
    RESPIRATORY TRACT AND ACUTE NERVOUS SYSTEM DEPRESSION. SKIN & EYE
    CONTACT: PRIMARY IRRITATION. CHRONIC: REPEATED OR PROLONGED
    OVEREXPOSURE MAY CAUSE DEFATTING OF T HE SKIN.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:NONE OF THE COMPOUNDS IN THIS PRODUCT IS
    LISTED BY IARC, NTP, OR OSHA AS A CARCINOGEN.
Effects of Overexposure:INHALATION: HEADACHE, WEAKNESS, DIZZINESS,
    INCOORDINATION AND POSSIBLE UNCONSCIOUSNESS OR COMA. CONTACT:
    REDNESS, CRACKING, BLISTERING, IRRITATION AND DERMATITIS.
    INGESTION:MAY CAUSE NAUSEA, VOMITING,  AND DIARRHEA. ASPIRATION OF
    PRODUCT INTO THE LUNG MAY CAUSE CHEMICAL PNEUMONITIS WHICH CAN BE
    FATAL.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:PERSONS WITH PRE-EXISTING DISEASE
    IN OR A HISTORY OF AILMENTS INVOLVING THE SKIN, EYE, RESPIRATORY
    TRACT, LIVER, KIDNEY, OR CNS ARE AT GREATER RISK OF DEVELOPING
    ADVERSE EFFECTS WHEN EXPOSED.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE, RESTORE BREATHING IF
    STOPPED. GET MEDICAL AID. EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF
    WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. GET MEDICAL AID. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY WITH
    SOAP AND WATER . IF IRRITATION DEVELOPS OR PERSISTS, CONSULT WITH A
    PHYSICIAN. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND LAUNDER BEFORE REUSE.
    INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE GLASS OF MILK IF CONSCIOUS.
    GET MED AID.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:60.0F,15.6C
Extinguishing Media:CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, OR DRY CHEMICAL.
Fire Fighting Procedures:FIRE FIGHTERS SHOULD USE NIOSH APPROVED SCBA &
    FULL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT WHEN FIGHTING CHEMICAL FIRE. USE WATER
    SPRAY TO COOL NEARBY CONTAINERS EXPOSED TO FIRE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:FIRE OR EXCESSIVE HEAT MAY CAUSE
    PRODUCTION OF HAZARDOUS VAPORS WHICH MAY FORM EXPLOSIVE MIXTURES IN
    AIR OR TRAVEL ALONG GROUND TO IGNITION SOURCE & FLASH BACK.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:REMOVE ALL SOURCES OF IGNITION. AVOID
    BREATHING VAPORS. VENTILATE AREA. AVOID CONTACT WITH HOT LIQUID AND
    FUMES. CONTAIN AND REMOVE WITH INERT ABSORBENT AND NON-SPARKING
    TOOLS. REACTS WITH OXIDIZING M ATERIALS. RQ IS 100 LBS OR MORE.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A COOL, DRY, WELL VENTILATED
    AREA OR BUILDING WHICHCOMPLIES WITH OSHA 1910.106. KEEP IN A
    CLOSED, LABELED CONTAINER. PROTECT FROM DAMAGE.
Other Precautions:KEEP CONTAINERS CLOSED WHEN MATERIAL IS NOT IN USE.
    GROUND AND BOND DRUMS. DO NOT USE PRESSURE TO EMPTY DRUMS. KEEP
    AWAY FROM HEAT AND OPEN FLAME. DISCARD CONTAMINATED FOOTWEAR. DO
    NOT WEAR CONTAMINAT ED CLOTHING. MAINTAIN GOOD HOUSEKEEPING.

Respiratory Protection:OUTDOORS: NIOSH APPROVED RESPIRATOR FOR PAINT,
