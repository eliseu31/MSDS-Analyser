=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING HAS STOPPED, GIVE
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION, SEE A PHYSICIAN. EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER 15
    MINUTES AND GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. IF SWALLOWED: DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING, CALL A P HYSICIAN.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:21 F/-6 C
Lower Limits:1.8
Upper Limits:10.1
Extinguishing Media:CARBON DIOXIDE, DRY CHEMICAL, OR ALCOHOL TYPE FOAM.
Fire Fighting Procedures:SELF CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS. FULL
    PROTECTIVE CLOTHING. COOL ADJACENT CONTAINERS WITH WATER SPRAY.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:SOLVENT VAPOR (HEAVIER THAN AIR) CAN MOVE
    BY AIR CURRENT AND BE IGNITED BY SPARKS, FLAMES, ELECTRIC
    DISCHARGE, ETC.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:EVACUATE NON-ESSENTIAL PERSONNEL. VENTILATE &
    REMOVE ALL SOURCES OF IGNITION. COVER SPILL WITH ABSORBENT
    MATERIAL. NOTIFY AUTHORITIES. TRANSFER TO CONTAINERS FOR RECOVERY
    OR DISPOSAL.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP AWAY FROM HEAT, SPARKS AND
    FLAMES. AVOID BREATHING VAPOR. KEEP CONTAINER CLOSED. USE WITH
    ADEQUATE VENTILATION.
Other Precautions:GROUND CONTAINER DURING STORAGE AND TRANSFER
    OPERATIONS.
