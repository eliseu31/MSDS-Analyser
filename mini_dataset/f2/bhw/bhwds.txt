=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NONE
Extinguishing Media:USE WATER TO HANDLE LITHIUM BATTERY FIRES.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS.
    POISONOUS,FLAMMABLE GASES MAY BE RELEASED DURING FIRE SITUATION.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:CELL WILL VENT OR RUPTURE WHEN THE
    INTERNAL PRESSURE BUILDS UP TO 200F (OR 294 PSIG). USE WATER TO
    COOL THE FIRE & REDUCE THE OVERALL HAZARD.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE AT WELL-VENTILATED STRUCTURE,IN
    A SPRINKLER-PROTECTED,NON-COMBUSTIBLE STRUCTURE WITH ADEQUATE
    CLEARANCEBETWEEN WALL AND BATTERY STACKS.
Other Precautions:PUBLICATIONS FOR DETAIL HANDLING: 1.N. IRVING SAX
    "DANGEROUS PROPERTIES OF INDUSTRIAL MATERIALS: REINHOLD; 2.
    MATHESON GAS DATA BOOK;MATHESON,EAST RUTHERFORD, N.J.
