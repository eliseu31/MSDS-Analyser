=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN-THOROUGHLY WASH EXPOSED AREA WITH SOAP AND WATER.
    EYES-RINSE WITH WATER FOR 15 MIN. LIFTING UPPER AND LOWER LIDS
    OCCASIONALLY. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. INGESTION-DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING. KEEP PERSON  WARM, QUIET, AND GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    INHALATION-REMOVE VICTIM TO FRESH AIR. GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION
    IF BREATHING HAS STOPPED.
