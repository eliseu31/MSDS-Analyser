=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES.SEE DOCTOR. SKIN:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER
    WHILE REMOVING CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND SHOES.IF IRRITATION
    PERSIST,SEE DOC TOR. INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.IF NOT
    BREATHING GIVE CPR.IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT,GIVE OXYGEN.SEE
    DOCTOR. INGESTION:DO NOT INDUCE VOMIT.SEE DOCTOR.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:=31.1C, 88.F
Lower Limits:1.1
Upper Limits:6.1
Extinguishing Media:USE WATER FOG, CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, OR DRY
    CHEMICAL.
Fire Fighting Procedures:FIRE FIGHTERS SHOULD USE NIOSH APPROVED SCBA &
    FULL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT WHEN FIGHTING CHEMICAL FIRE. USE WATER
    SPRAY TO COOL NEARBY CONTAINERS EXPOSED TO FIRE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:POLYMERIZATION WILL TAKE PLACE UNDER FIRE
    CONDITIONS AND CONTAINER MAY RUPTURE VIOLENTLY. COOL FIRE EXPOSED
    CONTAINERS WITH WATER.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ABSORB WITH SUITABLE AGENT, SUCH AS SAND OR
    VERMICULITE.WASH AREA WITH SOAP AND WATER. SOME ABSORBENTS SUCH AS
    ZORB-ALL MAY INITIATE POLYMERIZATION.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE APPLICABLE FOR THIS MATERIAL

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN COOL, DRY, WELL VENTILATED
    AREA. PROTECT FROM PHYSICAL DAMAGE, HEAT AND IGNITION SOURCES. KEEP
    CONTAINERS CLOSED.
Other Precautions:NONE.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:ATMOSPHERIC LEVELS SHOULD BE MAINTAINED BELOW
    THE EXPOSURE GUIDELINE. WHEN RESPIRATORY PROTECTION IS REQUIRED FOR
    CERTAIN OPERATIONS, USE AN APPROVED AIR-PURIFYING RESPIRATOR.
Ventilation:MECHANICAL (GENERAL) VENTILATION IS REQUIRED. LOCAL EXHAUST
    MAY BE REQUIRED IF WORK AREA IS NOT VENTED.
Protective Gloves:IMPERVIOUS.
Eye Protection:CHEMICAL GOGGLES OR FULL FACE RESPIRATOR
Other Protective Equipment:IMPERVIOUS PROTECTIVE CLOTHING. USE OF
    BOOTS, APRON OR FULL-BODY SUIT WILL DEPEND ON TYPE OF OPERATION.
Work Hygienic Practices:WASH THROUGHLY AFTER HANDLING AND BEFORE
    EATING. LAUNDER CONTAMINATED CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE. DISCARD
    CONTAMINATED SHOES.
Supplemental Safety and Health
NOTE TO PHYSICIAN:BECAUSE RAPID ABSORPTION MAY OCCUR THROUGH LUNGS IF
    ASPIRATED,THE DECISION TO INDUCE VOMIT SHOULD BE MADE BY A
    PHYSICIAN. IF LAVAGE IS PERFORMED,SUGGEST ENDOTRACHEAL AND/OR
    ESOPHGEAL  CONTROL. IF BURN IS PRESENT,TREAT AS ANY THERMAL BURN
    AFTER DECONTAMINATION.NO SPECIFIC ANTIDOTE.SUPPORTIVE CARE.

=================  Stability and Reactivity Data  =================

Stability Condition to Avoid:POLYMERIZES EASILY. AVOID TEMPERATURES

    INHIBITOR & OXIGN
