=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:NO
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:SEE "OVEREXPOSURE SYMPTOMS"
Effects of Overexposure:EYE:MAY CAUSE TEMPORARY IRRITATION; SKIN:MAY
    DRY OUT SKIN, ABSORBTION IS NOT LIKELY; INHALE:WARNING SIGNS ARE
    DIZZINESS AT 1000 PPM, UNCONCIOUSNESS AT 10,000 PPM.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE FRESH AIR/OXYGEN IF
    NEEDED; EYES:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES;
    SKIN:WASH WITH SOAP AND WATER; INGEST:UNLIKELY TO OCCUR, IF
    INGESTED, DO NO T INDUCE VOMITING, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NONE
Extinguishing Media:WATER FOG.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:VAPORS OF THIS SOLVENT MAY DEVELOP A
    FLAMMABLE ATMOSPHERE IN CONFINED AREAS.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:MOP UP, WIPE UP, OR SOAK UP WITH ABSORBENT.
    REMOVE TO OUT OF DOORS.
Neutralizing Agent:NOT APPLICABLE.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID BREATHING VAPORS,STORE IN A COOL
    DRY PLACE,CONCENTRATED VAPORS ARE HEAVIER THAN AIR AND WILL COLLECT
    IN LOW AREAS - DO NOT ENTER THESE AREAS.
Other Precautions:KEEP AWAY FROM METALLIC ALUMINUM AND ZINC POWDERS.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:NOT NORMALLY NEEDED. IN EMERGENCIES, USE
    APPROVED AIR PURIFYING TYPE.
Ventilation:NORMAL VENTILATION.
Protective Gloves:NOT NORMALLY NEEDED.
Eye Protection:SAFETY GOGGLES.
