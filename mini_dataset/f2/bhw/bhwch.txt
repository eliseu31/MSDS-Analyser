    BREATHING. KEEP WARM AND QUIET. SKIN CONTACT: WASH AFFECTED AREA
    THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP AND WATER. REMOVE  CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND
    LAUNDER BEFORE  RE-USE. IF EYE CONTACT: FLUSH EYES WITHLARGE
    AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
