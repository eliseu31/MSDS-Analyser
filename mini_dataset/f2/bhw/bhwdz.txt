=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE:FLUSH EYES WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES;
    SKIN:WASH SKIN THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP AND WATER,IF DRENCHED,REMOVE
    AND WASH CLOTHING; INGEST:INDUCE VOMITING AT ONCE BY GIVING TWO
    GLASSES OF	WATER TO DRINK AND PLACE FINGER AT BACK OF THROAT.  
    OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION.
