=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH EYES WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES HOLDING LIDS OPEN.RINSE CONTINUOUSLY WHILE ON WAY TO
    MEDICAL ATTENTION.SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING/SHOES.WIPE
    SKIN,FLUSH WITH WATER  FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES,THEN SOAP &
    WATER.GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.INGESTION:DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.GIVE
    WATER UNLESS VICTIM IS DROWSY,CONVULSING OR UNCONSCIOUS.GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION (SEE SUPP DATA)

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DANGER.CORROSIVE.STORE IN COOL,DRY
    PLACE WITH ADEQUATE VENTILATION.KEEP AWAY FROM OPEN FLAMES & HIGH
    TEMPERATURES.MINIMIZE ALL CONTACT.SENSITIZER.
Other Precautions:EMPTY CONTAINERS MAY CONTAIN HAZARDOUS
    RESIDUES.LAUNDER CONTAMINATED CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE.CONTAMINATED
    LEATHER ARTICLES,INCLUDING SHOES,CANNOT BE DECONTAMINATED AND
    SHOULD BE DESTROYED.AVOID CONTACT  WITH EYES.
