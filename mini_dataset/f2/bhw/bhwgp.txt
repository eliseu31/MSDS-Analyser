    CLOTHING AND WASH SKIN WITH SOAP AND WATER.INGESTION:DRINK 1 OR 2
    GLASSES OF WATER TO DILUTE.CALL A PHYSICIAN
    IMMEDIATELY.INHALATION:IF OVERCOME	BY VAPOR,REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE
    AND CALLPHYSICIAN.IF BREATHING IS IRREGULAR OR STOPPED,START
    RESUSCITATION,ADMINISTER OXYGEN.
