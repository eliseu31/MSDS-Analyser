=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE:FLUSH W/ WATER 15 MIN HOLD LIDS OPEN. SEE A DOCTOR.
    SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND LAUNDER BEFORE  REUSE.  WASH
    WITH SOAP & WATER, ALCOHOL, OR HAND CLEANER. INHALATION:REMOVE FROM
    EXPOSURE.  RESTORE BREATHING AS NECESSARY. INGESTED:UNLIKELY, BUT
    IF OCCURRING,DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING, GET IMMEDIATE MEDICAL CARE. 
    IF IRRITATION PERSISTS,OR IS SEVERE,SEE A DOCTOR.
