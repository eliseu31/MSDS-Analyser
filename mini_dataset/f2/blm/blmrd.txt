=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: CLEAN EYES WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES.
    SKIN: CLEAN WITH SOAP AND WATER. INHALED: MOVE AFFECTED PERSON TO
    FRESH AIR. APPLY ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION IF NECESSARY. SEEK MEDICAL
    ATTEN TION IMMEDIATELY. INGEST: DRINK WATER TO DILUTE. DO NOT
    INDUCE VOMITING. CALL PHYSICIAN.
