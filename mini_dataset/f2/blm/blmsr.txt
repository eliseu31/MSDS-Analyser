=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN; GET IMMED MED
    ASSISTANCE. SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND WASH WITH SOAP
    AND WATER. INHAL: MOVE TO FRESH AIR AND GIVE ARTF RESP IF BREATHING
    HAS STOP PED. INGEST: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE MILK OR WATER IF
    CONSCIOUS. GET IMMED MED ATTN. GET MED ASSISTANCE FOR ALL CASES OF
    OVEREXPOSURE.
