=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. RINSE LIBERALLY WITH SOAP
    AND WATER FOR A CONSIDERABLE PERIOD. INGEST: RINSE THE MOUTH
    SEVERAL TIMES WITH WATER, PATIENT MAY NOT SWALLOW THE WATER. ALWAYS
    CALL A DO CTOR. EYES: RINSE LIBERALLY WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MIN. SEND OR BRING THE PATIENT TO A DOCTOR OR HOSPITAL. INHAL: TAKE
    THE PATIENT INTO THE FRESH AIR & ALLLOW TO REST. ALWAYS CALL A
    DOCTOR.
