=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.PROVIDE
    OXYGEN IF NEEDED.EYE:FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES WHILE HOLDING
    EYELIDS OPEN.SKIN:REMOVE EXCESS WITH CLOTH OR PAPER.WASH WITH SOAP
    & WAT ER.INGESTION:DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.IF VOMITING OCCURS,KEEP
    HEAD BELOW HIPS TO PREVENT ASPIRATION OF LIQUID INTO THE LUNG.GET
    MEDICAL CARE IMMEDIATELY.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:COC
Flash Point:225F,107C
Extinguishing Media:USE WATER FOG, CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, OR DRY
    CHEMICAL. DO NOT USE DIRECT STREAM OF WATER;MATERIAL MAY FLOAT &
    REIGNITE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE A POSITIVE PRESSURE NIOSH-APPROVED
    SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS. EVACUATE AREA. COOL FIRE
    EXPOSED CONTAINERS WITH WATER SPRAY.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:COMBUSTION OR HEAT OF FIRE MAY PRODUCE
    HAZARDOUS DECOMPOSITION PRODUCTS AND VAPORS.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WEAR RESPIRATOR & PROTECTIVE CLOTHING.SHUT OFF
    SOURCE OF LEAK IF SAFE TO DO SO.DIKE & CONTAIN.REMOVE WITH VACUUM
    TRUCK/PUMP TO SALVAGE VESSELS.SOAK UP RESIDUE/SMALL SPILL WITH
    CLAY,SAND... FLUSH AREA  WITH WATER TO REMOVE TRACE RESIDUE.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORAGE-KEEP CONTAINER IN COOL,DRY
    PLACE AWAY FROM EXCESSIVE HEAT,HOT SURFACES,FLAMES & STRONG
    OXIDIZERS.
Other Precautions:AVOID CONTACT WITH SKIN, EYES, AND CLOTHING. WASH
    THOROUGHLY AFTER HANDLING. LAUNDER CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE. AVOID
    INHALING VAPORS OR MISTS. EMPTY CONTAINERS OF THIS PRODUCT CAN BE
    HAZARDOUS DUE TO THE  RETENTION OF RESIDUES AND VAPORS.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:IF EXPOSURE MAY OR DOES EXCEED OCCUPATIONAL
    EXPOSURE LIMITS, WEAR A NIOSH-APPROVED RESPIRATOR. IN ACCORD WITH
    29 CFR 1910.134, USE EITHER AN ATMOSPHERE-SUPPLYING RESPIRATOR OR
    AN AIR-PURIFYING RESPIRA TOR FOR ORGANIC VAPORS & PARTICULATES.
