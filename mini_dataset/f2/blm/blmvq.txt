=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:IF ANY IRRITATION PERSISTS OR SEVERE, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    EYE:FLUSH W/WATER FOR 15 MIN. SKIN:WASH W/SOAP & WATER. REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING & LAUNDER BEFORE REUSE. INHALED:REMOVE FROM
    EXPOSURE.  AID OR RESTORE BREATHING AS NEEDED. INGESTED:IMMEDIATELY
    DRINK 2 GLASSES OF WATER & INDUCE VOMITING BY IPECAC OR FINGER IN
    BACK OF THROAT. (NOTHING BY MOUTH IF UNCONSCIOUS!) GET IMMEDIATE
    MEDICAL ATT.
