=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.INGESTION:DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING.IF CONSCIOUS,DRINK A GLASS OF MILK/WATER.INHALATION:REMOVE
    FROM EXPOSURE.GIVE CPR/OXYGEN IF NEEDED.EYE:FLUSH WITH LUKE WARM
    WATER (LOW PRESSURE)FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES,OCCASIONALLY LIFTING
    THE EYELIDS.SKIN:WASH WITH SOAP & PLENTY OF WATER.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:>45F,>7C
Extinguishing Media:CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, OR DRY CHEMICAL.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR FIRE FIGHTING PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT AND A
    FULL FACED SELF CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS. EVACUATE AREA. COOL
    FIRE EXPOSED CONTAINERS WITH WATER SPRAY.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:CLOSED CONTAINERS MAY EXPLODE WHEN
    EXPOSED TO EXTREME HEAT OR BURST WHEN CONTAMINATED WITH WATER (CO2
    EVOLVED). VAPORS TRAVEL TO IGNITION SOURCES & FLASH BACK.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:REMOVE ALL SOURCES OF IGNITION.VENTILATE THE
    AREA.DIKE/IMPOUND SPILLED MATERIAL & CONTROL FURTHER SPILLAGE.COVER
    SPILL WITH SAW DUST,VERMICULITE,FULLER'S EARTH.POUR LIQUID
    DECONTAMINANT OVER SPILLAGE  TO REACT.TRANSFER TO OPEN CONTAINERS
    FOR DISPOSAL.
Neutralizing Agent:AMMONIUM HYDROXIDE (0-10%), DETERGENT (2-5%) AND
    BALANCE WATER/TERGITOL TMN-10

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORAGE-STORE BETWEEN 32F & 122F IN
    CLOSED CONTAINERS AWAY FROM HEAT,SPARKS & FLAMES.
Other Precautions:CLOSED CONTAINERS MAY EXPLODE WHEN EXPOSED TO EXTREME
    HEAT. DO NOT HEAT OR CUT EMPTY CONTAINER WITH ELECTRIC/GAS TORCH.
    AVOID CONTACT WITH EYES, SKIN AND CLOTHING. DO NOT BREATHE MIST OR
    VAPOR. WASH T HOROUGHLY AFTER HANDLING.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:WEAR A NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED POSITIVE PRESSURE
    AIR-SUPPLIED RESPIRATOR. IF THE TLV FOR ISOCYANATE MONOMER ARE
    BELOW 0.05 PPM, AT LEAST AN AIR PURIFYING (ORGANIC VAPOR)
    RESPIRATOR IS REQUIRED.
Ventilation:USE LOCAL EXHAUST OR GENERAL DILUTION VENTILATION. EXHAUST
    AIR MAY NEED TO BE CLEANED BY SCRUBBERS OR FILTERS.
Protective Gloves:NEOPRENE RUBBER GLOVES
Eye Protection:GOGGLES OR FULL FACE SHIELD
Other Protective Equipment:EYE WASH STATION & SAFETY SHOWERS SHOULD BE
    AVAILABLE IN THE IMMEDIATE VICINITY OF ANY POTENTIAL EXPOSURE.
Work Hygienic Practices:DO NOT WEAR CONTACT LENSES. WASH CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE.
Supplemental Safety and Health
