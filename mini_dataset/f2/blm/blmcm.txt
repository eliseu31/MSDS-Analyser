=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGEST: WASH OUT MOUTH WITH WATER PROVIDED PERSON IS
    CONSCIOUS. CALL MD. SKIN: FLUSH WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR
    AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND SHOES CALL
    MD. INHAL: REMOV E TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING BECOMES DIFFICULT,
    CALL MD. EYES: FLUSH WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES. ASSURE ADEQUATE FLUSHING BY SEPARATING LIDS W/FINGERS.
    CALL MD.
