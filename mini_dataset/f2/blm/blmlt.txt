=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:FLUSH W/LG AMTS OF H2O FOR @ LST 15 MIN. GET MD.
    SKIN:REMOVE CONTAM CLTHG; WASH CNTCT AREA W/MILD SOAP & H2O. IF
    SWELLING OR IRRIT DEVELOPS, GET MD. INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF
    BRTHG HAS STOPPE D, ADMIN ARTF RESP & GET MD. INGEST:DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMIT. IF CONSCIOUS, DILUTE BY ADMINISTERING H2O OR MILK. GET MD.
