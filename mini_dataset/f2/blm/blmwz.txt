=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH IMMED WITH PLENTY OF COOL RUNNING WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MIN. REMOVE CONTACT LENSES. SKIN: FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF
    COOL RUNNING WATER. WASH THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP AND WATER. INGEST:
    RINSE MOUTH;  THEN DRINK 1 OR 2 LARGE GLASSES OF WATER. DO NOT
    INDUCE VOMITING. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCON PERSON.
    IF IRRITATION OR DISCOMFORT PERSISTS. CALL PHYS.
