=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING, FLOOD SKIN WITH LARGE
    AMOUNTS OF WATER. IF IRRITATION PERSISTS SEEK MD. EYE: IMMEDIATELY
    FLUSH EYES, INCLUDING UNDER LIDS WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 1 5 MINUTES. CALL MD. INHAL: NO SPECIFIC INFORMATION
    AVAILABLE, ONE SHOULD OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION. INGEST: CALL MD
    IMMEDIATELY .
