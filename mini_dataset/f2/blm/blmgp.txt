    EARTH & TRANSFER LIQUID & SOLID DIKING MATERIAL TO SEGREGATED
    CONTAINERS FOR RECOVERY OR DISPOSAL. REMOVE CONTAM CLTHG & WASH
    BEFORE REUSE. KEEP SPILLS A WAY FROM SEWERS OR OPEN BODIES OF
    WATER.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:FREEZES @ 41F (5C), BECOMES "GEL LIKE"
    AT TEMPS OF ABOUT 10C(50F). MAY REACT ON SURFACES TO FORM A CRUSTED
    LIKE COATING AT TEMPS >100C.
Other Precautions:HUMANS: NEAT 0-60 PATCH TEST ON BACKS OF 200 SUBJECTS
    FOR A 5 DAY PERIOD; RPTD 48 HRS PATCH TEST AFTER 3 WEEK INTERVAL.
    RSLTS: NO REACTION ON ANY SUBJECT (NEGATIVE RESPONSE).
