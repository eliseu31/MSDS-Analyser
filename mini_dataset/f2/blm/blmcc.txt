=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE:DO NOT RUB. FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES WHILE HOLDING EYELIDS APART. IF IRRITATION, BLINKING OR
    TEARING OCCUR AND PERSIST, CALL MD. INGEST:CALL MD IF SYMPTOMS
    DEVELOP. ADVER SE HEALTH EFFECTS ARE NOT EXPECTED. INHAL:GET TO
    FRESH AIR. IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST, CALL MD. SKIN:WASH WITH WATER.
    APPLY CREAMS OR LOTIONS TO PREVENT DRYING.
