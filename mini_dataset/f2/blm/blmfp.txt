=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: FLUSH WITH WATER. SKIN: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST
    15 MIN. SEEK MED ATTN. INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR AND GIVE ARTF
    RESP IF BREATHING HAS STOPPED. CALL PHYS. INGEST: IF SWALLOWED GIVE
    LARGE AM OUNTS OF WATER OR MILK IF CONSCIOUS, THEN INDUCE VOMITING
    AND SEEK IMMED MED ATTN.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC

Lower Limits:6%
Upper Limits:37%
Extinguishing Media:ALCOHOL OR POLYMER FOAM FOR LARGE FIRES, CO2, DRY

Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED SCBA AND FULL

    SALVAGE TANKS. NOTIFY AUTHORITIES FOR MAJOR SPILLS.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.
