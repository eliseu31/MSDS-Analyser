=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION IF
    NEEDED, PREFERABLY MOUTH-TO-MOUTH. IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT GIVE
    OXYGEN. MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN: TREAT FOR FROSTBITE IF NECESSARY.
    EYES: FL USH WITH PLENTY OF WATER. MEDICAL ATTENTION.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NONE
Extinguishing Media:AS APPROPRIATE FOR COMBUSTABLES IN AREA.
Fire Fighting Procedures:SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS, IS
    REQUIRED IF CYLINDERS RUPTURE OR RELEASE UNDER FIRE CONDITIONS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:WATER FOG/COOL CONTAINRS. CYLINDERS ARE
    EQUIPPED W/TEMPERATURE AND PRESSURE RELIEF DEVICES BUT MAY STILL
    RUPTURE UNDER FIRE CONDITIONS. DECOMPOSITION MAY OCCUR.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:VENTILATE AREA-ESPECIALLY LOW PLACES WHERE
    HEAVY VAPORS MIGHT COLLECT. WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS
    FOR LARGE SPILLS. REMOVE OPEN FLAME.
