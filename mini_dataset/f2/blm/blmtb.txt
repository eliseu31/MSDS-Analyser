=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH WITH CLEAR WATER FOR 15 MIN. OR UNTIL IRRITATION
    SUBSIDES.  SKIN: REMOVE ANY CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND WASH SKIN
    THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP AND WATER.  INHALATION: VAPOR PRESSURE IS VERY
    LOW. VA POR INHALATION UNDER AMBIENT CONDITIONSIS NORMALLY NOT A
    PROBLEM.  INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. CALL A PHYSICIAN
    IMMEDIATELY.
