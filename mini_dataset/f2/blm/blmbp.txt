======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:SHOULD BE HANDLED AND STORED IN A COOL
    (50F - 80F), DARK PLACE. TEMPS OVER 120F SHOULD BE AVOIDED FOR MAX
    SHELF LIFE.
Other Precautions:SLOW POLYMERIZATION, WITH POSSIBLE HEAT GENERATION,
    WILL OCCUR IN DIRECT SUNLIGHT AND AT TEMPERATURES APPROXIMATELY
    130F OR HIGHER. AVOID SKIN CONTACT. DO NOT WEAR CONTACT LENSES.
