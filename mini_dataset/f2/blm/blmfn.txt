=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL:MOVE TO FRESH AIR, AID BREATHING, CALL PHYS. EYE: IMMED
    FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN AND CALL PHYS. SKIN: REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING, WASH AREA IMMED WITH SOAP AND WATER. SEE
    PHYS IF I RRITATION PERSISTS. INGEST: DRINK 1 OR 2 GLASSES OF WATER
    TO DILUTE. DO NOT NOT INDUCE VOMIT. CONSULT PHYS OR POISON CONTROL
    CENTER IMMED.
