First Aid:INHAL:PROVIDE FRESH AIR. IF BRTHG LABORED, GIVE O2/ARTF RESP.
    SKIN:WASH THORO W/SOAP & WATER. EYES:FLUSH IMMED W/ PLENTY OF WATER

    TO	DILUTE. DO NOT INDUCE VOMIT. CALL MD ORPOISON CONTROL CENTER
    IMMED. TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY.
