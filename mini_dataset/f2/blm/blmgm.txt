=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE, RESTORE BRTHG & NOTIFY MD. EYES:
    FLUSH IMMED W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR A MINIMUM OF 15 MIN.
    NOTIFY AN MD. SKIN: WASH AFFECTED AREA W/SOAP & WATER, REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED C LOTHING & WASH BEFORE REUSE. INGEST: DONOT INDUCE
    VOMITING. KEEP PERSON WARM/QUIET & GET MED ATTN. ASPIRATION OF THIS
    MATL INTO THE LUNGS CAN CAUSE CHEMICAL PNEUMONITIS WHICH CAN BE
    FATAL.
