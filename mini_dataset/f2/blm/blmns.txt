=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: MOVE TO FRESH AIR AT ONCE. IF BREATHING CONTINUES TO
    BE DIFFICULT, ADMINISTER OXYGEN UNTIL MEDICAL ASSISTANCE CAN BE
    RENDERED. EYES: IMMED FLUSH W/POTABLE WATER FOR A MINIMUM OF 15
    MIN, SEEK AS SISTANCE FROM MD . SKIN: FLUSH W/COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF
    WATER. CALL MD . INGEST: CALL MD IMMEDIATELY .

    PAPER TOWELS. LARGE QUANTITIES, USE AN ABSORBENT MATERIAL AFTER
    RETAINING BY DIKING.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP PRODUCT CONTAINERS COOL, DRY AND
    AWAY FROM SOURCES OF IGNITION. USE AND STORE THIS PRODUCT
    W/ADEQUATE VENTILATION.
Other Precautions:THIS PRODUCT SHLD NOT BE ALLOWED TO ENTER A CONFINED
