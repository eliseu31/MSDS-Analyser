=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO RISK-FREE AREA. RESTORE BREATHING.
    ASTHMA-TYPE SYMPTOMS MAY DEVELOP IMMED/DELAYED. MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    SKIN: REMOVE CLOTHING. WASH W/SOAP/WATER. WASH CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHING BEFORE  RE-USE. EYES: FLUSH W/WATER LIFTING LIDS-15
    MINUTES. MEDICAL ATTENTION. INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.
    NOTHING BY MOUTH TO UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. MEDICAL ATTENTION.
