    SHOULD BE ADMINISTERED BY QUALIFIED PERS.EYE:FLUSH AFFECTED EYES
    W/WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN.IF IRRIT DEVELOPS,SEEK MD.SKIN: REMOVE
    CONTAM  CLTHG & WIPE MATL FROM SKIN. THORO WASHW/SOAP & WATER. USE
    WATERLESS SKIN CLEANSER, IF NEC. IF IRRIT DEVELOPS & PERSISTS, SEEK
    MD. INHAL:MOVE AWAY FROM SOURCE OF EXPOS TO MISTS/FUMES & IN (SUPP
    DATA)

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:COC
Flash Point:>400F,>204C
Extinguishing Media:CO2, DRY CHEMICAL, FOAM, AND WATER SPRAY. WATER
    SPRAY OR FOAM MAY CAUSE FROTHING.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR NIOSH/MSHA APPRVD SCBA & FULL PROT
    EQUIP.USE WATER SPRAY TO COOL FIRE-EXPSD CNTNRS,DILUTE & DISPERSE
    VAPS,PROT PERS & TO FLUSH UNIGNIT MATLS AWAY.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:THIS MATL CAN IGNITE & BURN WHEN HEATED
    >ITS FLASH POINT. EMPTY TANKS/CONTAINERS RETAIN MATL RESIDUE AND
    MAY, IF HEATED, GENERATE VAPS WHICH CAN IGNITE/EXPLODE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WEAR REC PROT CLTHG & EQUIP & PROVIDE ADEQ
    VENT.DO NOT ALLOW SPILLS TO ENTER SEWERS, STREAMS/SURF WATERS.DIKE
    & CONTAIN SPILLS.PICK UP LRG SPILLS FOR LATER RECLAMATION &
    DISP.USE INERT ABSORB TO PICK  UP RESIDUE/SM SPILLS & COLLECT FOR
    LATER DISP.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN CLOSED CONTAINERS IN A COOL,
    DRY, WELL-VENT AREA AWAY FROM HEAT AND STRONG OXIDIZING AGENTS.
    PROTECT CONTAINERS FROM PHYSICAL DAMAGE.
Other Precautions:ROUTING OPER: USE IN WELL-VENT AREA & WEAR REC PROT
    CLTHG & EQUIP. AVOID BRTHG MISTS/FUMES GENERATED WHEN THIS MATL IS
    HEATED/ BURNED. AVOID PRLNGD SKIN CONT. CLEAN/LAUNDER CONTAM SHOES
    & CLTHG BEFORE  REUSE. LABEL ALL UNATTENDED (SEE ING 6)
