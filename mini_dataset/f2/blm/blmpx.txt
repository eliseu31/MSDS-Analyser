=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. SKIN: WASH WITH SOAP AND
    WATER. EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES.
    INGESTION: DRINK LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER. DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.
    REFER T O PHYSICIAN IMMEDIATELY.
