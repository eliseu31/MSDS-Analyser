=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH CLEAN WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES. CONTACT PHYSICIAN. SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING.
    FLUSH SKIN WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER. INHALATION: MOVE VICTIM TO
    FRESH AIR. I F BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, QUALIFIED PERSON SHOULD
    ADMINISTER ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. CONTACT PHYSICIAN. INGEST:
    CONTACT PHYSICIAN IMMEDIATELY. NOTES TO PHYSICIAN: IF SWALLOWED DO
    NOT INDUCE VOMITING.
