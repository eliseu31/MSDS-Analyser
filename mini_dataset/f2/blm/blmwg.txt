First Aid:GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES OF OVEREXPOSURE.SKIN:WASH
    WITH SOAP AND WATER.INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR & PROVIDE
    OXYGEN/CPR IF NECESSARY.EYE:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15
    MINUTES WHILE  HOLDING EYELIDS OPEN.INGESTION:CALL A PHYSICIAN.
