=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGEST: WASH OUT MOUTH WITH WATER PROVIDED PERSON IS
    CONSCIOUS. CALL MD. SKIN: FLUSH WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR
    AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND SHOES AND
    CALL MD. INHAL: R EMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING BECOMES
    DIFFICULT, CALL MD. EYE: FLUSH WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MINUTES. ASSURE ADEQUATE FLUSHING BY SEPARATING THE LIDS
    WITH FINGERS. CALL MD.
