=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: IRRIGATE EYES WITH WATER 15 MIN. MED. ATTEN. SKIN: WASH
    IN FLOWING WATER OR SHOWER. INGESTION: INDUCE VOMITING IMMEDIATELY
    BY GIVING 2 GLASSES OF WATER AND STICKING FINGER DOWN THROAT.
    NOTHING B Y MOUTH TO UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. MED ATTEN. INHALATION:
    REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. MED ATTEN.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:NON-COMBUSTIBLE
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR POSITIVE PRESSURE SELF-CONTAINED
    BREATHING APPARATUS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:NONE

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID EYE/SKIN CONTACT. COOL WATER
    (>80F) TO DISSOLVE CALCIUM CHLORIDE. HIGH HEAT DURING
    DISOLVING/MIXING. EXPOSED TO ATMOSPHERE WILL FORM SOLUTION.
Other Precautions:LEATHER CLOTHING AND SHOES WILL BE DAMAGED BY CALCIUM
    CHLORIDE.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:ATMOSPHERIC LEVELS SHOULD BE MAINTAINED BELOW
    THE EXPOSURE GUIDLINES. APPROVED AIR-PURIFYING RESPIRATOR. IN DUSTY
    ATMOSPHERES USE APPROVED DUST RESPIRATOR.
Ventilation:PROVIDE GENERAL AND/OR LOCAL EXHAUST VENTILATION TO CONTROL
    AIRBORNE LEVELS BELOW EXPOSURE GUIDLINES.
Protective Gloves:YES, DEPENDING ON OPERATION.
Eye Protection:SAFETY GLASSES, CHEMICAL GOGGLES
Other Protective Equipment:BODY COVERING PROTECTIVE CLOTHING IMPERVIOUS
    TO MATERIAL. BOOTS, APRONS, OR FULL-BODY SUIT DEPEND ON OPERATION.
Supplemental Safety and Health
EYE WASH SHOULD BE LOCATED IN IMMEDIATE WORK AREA.
