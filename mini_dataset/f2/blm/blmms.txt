=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE:FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. OBTAIN MEDICAL
    ATTENTION. SKIN:FUSH WITH WATER. INHAL:REMOVE PERSON TO FRESH AIR.
    IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST, OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION. INGEST: INDUCE
    VOMITING.  OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION.
