=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: CLEAN WITH SOAP AND WATER. INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING. CONSULT PHYSICIAN. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. EYE:
    FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. CONSULT PHYSICIAN.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:TOOLS TO OPEN ANY CNTNR MUST BE NON-
    SPARKING. PRIOR TO OPENING CNTNR, ELECTROSTATIC BONDING BETWEEN
    DISPENSING & RECEIVING CNTNR MUST BE ACCOMPLISHED
Other Precautions:IN ADDITION, ELECTROSTATIC GROUNDING OF CONTAINERS
    MUST BE PROVIDED. AVOID PROLONGED BREATHING OF VAPORS. AVOID
    PROLONGED OR REPEATED SKIN CONTACT. AVOID ALL SOURCES OF HEAT, OPEN
    FLAMES, SPARKS AND S TATIC ELECTRICITY.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:WHEN SPRAYING MATL USE A NIOSH/MSHA APPRVD
    CARTRIDGE RESP/GAS MASK SUITABLE TO KEEP AIRBORNE MISTS AND VAPOR
    CONCENTRATIONS BELOW TIME WEIGHTED THRESHOLD LIMIT VALUES. WHEN
