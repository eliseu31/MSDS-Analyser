=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: WASH MATL OFF SKIN W/PLENTY OF SOAP & H2O. IF REDNESS,
    ITCHING OR A BURNING SENSATION DEVELOPS, GET MED ATTN. EYES: IMMED
    FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN. IF REDNESS, ITCHING
    OR B URNING SENS DEVELOPS, HAVE EYES EXMINEDAND TREATED BY MED
    PERSONNEL. INGEST: GIVE 1 OR 2 GLASSES OF H2O. IF GI SYMPTOMS
    DEVELOP, CONSULT MEDICAL PERSONNEL. (NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH
    (SUPP DATA)

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:NOT APPLICABLE. USE MEDIA SUITABLE FOR SURROUNDING
    FIRE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED SCBA AND FULL
    PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT  IF INVOLVED IN A FIRE OF OTHER MATERIALS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:PRODUCT WILL EMIT TOXIC FUMES AT HIGH

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SWEEP UP AND RECOVER OR MIX MATERIAL WITH
    MOIST ABSORBENT AND SHOVEL INTO WASTE CONTAINER. WASH DOWN SPILL
    AREA WITH HOT WATER CONTAINING DETERGENT AND FLUSH TO A SEWER
    SERVICED BY A WASTEWATER TREATM ENT FACILITY.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:ENFORCE NO SMOKING RULE IN AREAS WHERE
    PTFE IS HNDLD/STORED. WASH HANDS & FACE AFTER HNDLG TO AVOID
    TRANSFER OF PTFE ONTO CIGARETTES & TOBACCO.
Other Precautions:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:NOT NORMALLY REQUIRED IF CONTROLS ARE ADEQ.
    WHERE POLYMER TEMP IS < 500F, USE NIOSH/MSHA APPRVD RESP FOR
    DUST/MIST/FUMES W/A TLV > 0.05 MG/M3, IF NEEDED. WHERE POLYMER TEMP
    IS > 500F, USE NIOSH/MSHA A PPRVD POSITIVE PRESS SUPPLIED AIR RESP.
Ventilation:PROVIDE LOCAL EXHAUST IF POLYMER IS HEATED ABOVE 500 F.
Protective Gloves:IMPERVIOUS GLOVES .
Eye Protection:CHEMICAL WORKERS GOGGLES .
Other Protective Equipment:EYEWASH STATION IN WORK AREA.
Work Hygienic Practices:MINIMIZE EXPOSURE IN ACCORDANCE WITH GOOD
    HYGIENE PRACTICE.
Supplemental Safety and Health
FIRST AID PROC: TO AN UNCON PERSON. INHAL: REMOVE VICTIM TO FRESH AIR.
    IF COUGH OR OTHER RESPIRATORY SYMPTOMS DEVELOP, CONSULT MED
    PERSONNEL. NOTE TO MD: INHAL FUMES OF DECOMP PROD CAN INDUCE
    TEMPORAR Y INFLUENZA-LIKE SYMPTOMS WHICH ARE DESCRIBED AS "POLYMER
