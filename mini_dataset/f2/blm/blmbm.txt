=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. REPORT TO
    MEDICAL. SKIN: WASH WITH SOAP AND WATER. INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH
    AIR. INGEST: REPORT TO MD.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NOT FLAMMABLE
Extinguishing Media:WATER
Fire Fighting Procedures:MATERIAL WILL GENERATE ACID FUMES. WEAR
    NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED SCBA AND FULL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT. AVOID
    VAPORS AND COMBUSTION PRODUCTS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:IF UNDILUTED MATERIAL IS HEATED TO 170F
    OR ABOVE, IT WILL FLASH IF IGNITED.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:PROTECT FROM SKIN CONTACT. PICK UP WITH
    ABSORBENT MATERIALS AND CONTAIN FOR DISPOSAL. LABEL ACCURATELY.
Neutralizing Agent:SODA ASH OR LIME.
