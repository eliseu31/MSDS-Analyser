=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL:REMOVE PERSON TO FRESH & SEE MD. INGEST:DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMIT. CALL POISON CONTROL CENTER, HOSPITAL EMER ROOM OR MD IMMED.
    EYE:FLUSH IMMED & THORO W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES.  IF IRRIT PERSISTS, CALL MD. SKIN:WASH THOROUGHLY W/WARM
    WATER & SOAP.
