First Aid:EYES: FLUSH IMMED WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN.
    SKIN: WASH OFF SKIN WITH WATER. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND
    CLEAN BEFORE REUSE. INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE O2 OR APPLY
    ARTF RESP  IF NEEDED. INGEST: IF SWALLOWED, CONSULT PHYS IMMED. IF
    ANY SYMPTOMS PERSIST, CONSULT PHYS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

    OPEN FIRE OR FLAME.

    NEUTRALIZE SPILLS.
