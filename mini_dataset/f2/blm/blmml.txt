=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE FROM HAZARD AREA, MAINTAIN BREATHING, CALL MD.
    SKIN: REMOVE WITH SOAP AND WATER. EYES: FLUSH IMMEDIATELY WITH
    LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. CALL MD. INGEST:
    DRINK 1 OR	2 GLASSES OF WATER TO DILUTE. DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.
    CALL MD.
