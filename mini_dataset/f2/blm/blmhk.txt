=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES.
    SKIN: WIPE OFF AND FLUSH WITH WATER. GET MD IF A REACTION DEVELOPS.
    INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GET MD IF ILL EFFECTS PERSIST. INGEST:
    GET M D IF LARGE AMOUNTS SWALLOWED. DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.
    COMMENT: IF SPONTANEOUS VOMITING OCCURS, KEEP HEAD LOWER THAN HIPS
    TO PREVENT ASPIRATION OF LIQUID INTO LUNGS.
