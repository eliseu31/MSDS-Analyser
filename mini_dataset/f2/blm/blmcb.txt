=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE:DO NOT RUB. FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES WHILE HOLDING EYELIDS APART. IF IRRITATION, BLINKING OR
    TEARING OCCUR AND PERSIST, CONSULT MD. INGEST:ADVERSE HEALTH
    EFFECTS ARE NOT  EXPECTED. CALL MD IF SYMPTOMS DEVELOP. INHAL:GET
    FRESH AIR. IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST, CALL MD. SKIN:FLUSH WITH COPIOUS
    AMOUNTS OF WATER. CALL MD .
