=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: RMEOVE PERSON TO FRESH AIR; CALL PHYSICIAN. SKIN: WASH
    WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER. EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES; PERSISTENT PAIN REFER TO OPHTHALMOLOGIST. INGEST: DRINK
    MILK.  DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. REFER TO PHYSICIAN IMMEDIATELY.
