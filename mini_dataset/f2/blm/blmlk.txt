=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN OR UNTIL
    IRRITATION SUBSIDES. HOLD EYELIDS OPEN WHILE FLUSHING. IF
    IRRITATION PERSISTS, GET MED ATTN. SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHING AND WASH SKIN THORO  WITH SOAP AND WATER. INGEST: DO NOT
    INDUCE VOMIT. SEEK MED AID. INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GET MED
    ATTN IF DISCOMFORT PERSISTS.
