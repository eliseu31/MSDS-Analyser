=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH COOL RUNNING WATER FOR AT LEAST
    15 MINUTES WHILE HOLDING EYELIDS APART. IF IRRITATION PERSISTS
    OBTAIN MD ATTENTION. SKIN:WASH WITH WATER. INHAL:NOT APPLICABLE.
    REMOVE TO FR ESH AIR. SUPPORT BREATHING (GIVE O2/ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRATION) . INGEST:DRINK SEVERAL GLASSES OF WATER. INDUCE
    VOMITING. IMMEDIATELY CALL MD.
