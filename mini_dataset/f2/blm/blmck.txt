=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGEST: WASH OUT MOUTH WITH WATER PROVIDED PERSON IS
    CONSCIOUS. CALL MD. SKIN: FLUSH WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR
    AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. REMOVE CONTAM CLOTHING AND SHOES AND CALL MD.
    INHAL: REMOVE  TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING BECOMES DIFFICULT. CALL
    MD. EYES: FLUSH WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES. ASSURE ADEQ FLUSHING BY SEPARATING EYELIDS W/FINGERS. CALL
    MD.
