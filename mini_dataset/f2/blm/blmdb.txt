=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE, RESTORE BREATHING. KEEP WARM AND
    QUIET. NOTIFY A PHYSICIAN. EYES: FLUSH IMMEDIATELY WITH LARGE
    AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN. SEE PHYSICIAN. SKIN: WASH
    AFFECTED ARE AS WITH WATER. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. INGEST:
    DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. CONSULT A PHYSICIAN OR POISON CONTROL
    CENTER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DO NOT STORE ABOVE 120 F. STORE LARGE
    QUANTITIES IN BLDGS DISIGNED AND PROTECTED FOR STORAGE OF NFPA
    CLASS LIQUIDS. GROUND CNTNRS WHEN POURING.
Other Precautions:AVOID FREE FALL OF LIQUID IN EXCESS OF A FEW INCHES.
    DO NOT FLAME CUT, BRAZE, OR WELD.
