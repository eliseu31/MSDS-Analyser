First Aid:INHAL:REMOVE TO CLEAN, FRESH AIR. SKIN:WASH WITH LARGE
    VOLUMES OF COLD WATER AND SOAP. EYE:WASH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES AND CALL PHYSICIAN. INGEST:CALL A PHYSICIAN.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================
