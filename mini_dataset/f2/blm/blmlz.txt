=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH IMMEDIATELY W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR A
    MINIMUM OF 15 MINUTES. TAKE TO MD FOR TREATMENT. INHAL: REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR, RESTORE BRTHG. CONSULT MD. SKIN: WASH AREAS W/SOAP &
    WATER. REMOVE C ONTAMINATED CLOTHING. CONSULT MD. INGEST: DILUTE BY
    DRINKING 2 GLASSES OF WATER. DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. CONSULT MD.

    PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT .
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:IF WATER IS USED, FOG NOZZLES ARE

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ISOLATE, ABSORB AND VENTILATE (OPEN ALL
    DOORS). REMOVE ALL IGNITION SOURCES, FLAMES, HOT SURFACES. AVOID
    BREATHING OF VAPORS. USE RESPIRATORY PROTECTIVE DEVICE.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID HIGH TEMPERATURES. DO NOT STORE
    OR USE NEAR HEAT, SPARKS OR FLAME. PREVENT PROLONGED/REPEATED
    BREATHING OF VAPOR OR SPRAY MIST.
Other Precautions:DO NOT TAKE INTERNALLY. AVOID BREATHING OF VAPORS OR
    DUST. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN. AVOID PROLONGED CONTACT WITH
