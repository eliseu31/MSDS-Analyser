=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: MOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION IF
    NECESSARY. SKIN: WASH WITH SOAP & WATER. EYE: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR
    AT LEAST 15 MINUTES, CONSULT MD. INGEST: DRINK ONE OR TWO GLASSES
    OF WATER T O DILUTE. DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. CONSULT MD OR POISON
    CONTROL CENTER IMMEDIATELY, TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY.
