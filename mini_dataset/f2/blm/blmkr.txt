=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE AFFECTED PERSONNEL FROM DUSTY AREA TO CLEAN
    AIR. INGEST: CALL MD IMMEDIATELY . SKIN: FLUSH W/ COPIOUS AMOUNTS
    OF WATER. CALL MD. . EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH W/ POTABLE WATER FO R
    A MINIMUM OF 15 MINUTES, SEEK ASSISTANCE FROM MD .
