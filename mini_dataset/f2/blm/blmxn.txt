=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALE-THIS MATERIAL IS NOT EXPECTED TO BE AN INHALATION
    HAZARD AT ROOM TEMP. IF VAPOR IS GENERATED WHEN HEATED, REMOVE FROM
    EXPOSURE. EYE-IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MIN. IF MATERIAL
    IS HOT,  TREAT FOR THERMAL BURNS & GO TO A HOSPITAL. SKIN-REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. WASH AREA WITH SOAP/WATER. IF IRRITATION
    OCCURS, SEE DOCTOR. INGEST-DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. SEEK A
    PHYSICIAN.

    RESPIRATOR WITH A DUST A ND MIST FILTER.
Ventilation:IF VAPOR OR MIST IS GENERATED, USE ADEQUATE VENTILATION IN
    ACCORDANCE WITH GOOD ENGINEERING PRACTICES.
Protective Gloves:RECOMMENDED.
Eye Protection:PLASTIC FACE SHIELD/SAFETY GLASSES.
Other Protective Equipment:EYE WASH STATION AND SAFETY SHOWER.
