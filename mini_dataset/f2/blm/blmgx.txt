=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:WHEN EXPOSED - ALWAYS GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. EYES: FLUSH WITH
    WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. SKIN: WASH WITH SOAP AND WATER. REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND CLEAN BEFORE REUSE. INHAL: REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR . PR OVIDE OXYGEN IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT.USE
    ARTIFICIAL RESP IF NOT BREATHING. GET MED ATTN. INGEST: DO NOT
    INDUCE VOMITING! ALWAYS GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
