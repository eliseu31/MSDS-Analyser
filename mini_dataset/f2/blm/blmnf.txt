=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:COC
Flash Point:>400F,>204C
Extinguishing Media:CO2, DRY CHEMICAL, FOAM, AND WATER SPRAY. WATER
    SPRAY OR FOAM MAY CAUSE FROTHING.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR NIOSH/MSHA APPRVD SCBA & FULL PROT EQUIP
    .USE WATER SPRAY TO COOL FIRE-EXPSD CNTNRS,DILUTE & DISPERSE
    VAPS,PROT PERS & TO FLUSH UNIGNIT MATL AWAY.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:THIS MATL CAN IGNITE & BURN WHEN HEATED
    ABOVE FLASH POINT. EMPTY TANKS OR CNTNRS RETAIN MATL RESIDUE & MAY,
    IF HEATED, GENERATE VAPS WHICH CAN IGNITE/EXPLODE.

    CONTAIN SPILLS,PICK UP LRG SPILLS FOR LATER RECLAMATION & DISP.USE
