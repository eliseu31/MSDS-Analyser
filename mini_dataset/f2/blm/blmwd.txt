=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH THOROUGHLY WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN. GET
    MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN: FLUSH THOROUGHLY WITH WATER. IF IRRITATION
    PERSITS, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF
    SYMPTOMS P ERSITS, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. INGEST: DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING. DRINK COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF MILK. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:FOR LARGE SPILLS, USE AMMONIUM CARBONATE TO
    "NEUTRALIZE" GLUTARALDEHYDE ODOR. COLLECT LIQUID & DISCARD IT. FOR
    SMALL SPILLS, WIPE WITH SPONGE OR MOP DOWN AREA WITH AN EQUAL
    MIXTURE OF HOUSEHOLD AMMONI A & WATER. FLUSH WITH LARGE
    QUANTITIEESOF H*2O.
Neutralizing Agent:AMMONIUM CARBONATE FOR ODOR.
