=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. IMMEDIATE MEDICAL
    ATTENTION. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION
    IF NECESSARY. WARM/QUIET. IMMEDIATE MEDICAL ATTENTION. EYES: FLUSH
    W/LARGE AMOUN TS WATER AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. WASH W/SOAP/WATER. MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IF IRRITATION PERSISTS.
