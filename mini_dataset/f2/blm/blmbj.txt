=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:WASH THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP & WATER. EYE: FLUSH WITH LARGE
    AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. INGEST:DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING, KEEP WARM & QUIET AND GET MD. INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    SUPPORT  BREATHING (GIVE O2/ARTF RESP) .
