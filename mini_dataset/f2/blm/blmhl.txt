=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: FLUSH W/WATER. IF IRRIT OCCURS, GET MD. SKIN: REMOVE
    CONTAM CLTHG/SHOES & WIPE EXCESS FROM SKIN. FLUSH W/WATER. FOLLOW
    BY WASHING W/SOAP & WATER. IF IRRIT OCCURS. GET MD. INHAL: REMOVE
    VICTIM TO  FRESH AIR & PROVIDE OXYGEN IF BRTHG DFCLT. GET MD.
    INGEST: DO NOT INDUCE VOMIT. IF VOMIT OCCURS SPONTANEOUSLY. KEEP
    HEAD BELOW HIPS TO PREVENT ASPIR OF LIQ IN LUNGS. GET MD. NOTE TO
    MD: IF (SUPP DATA)
