=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.INGESTION:DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING.DO NOT GIVE LIQUID.INHALATION:REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE.GIVE
    CPR/OXYGEN IF NEEDED.EYE:FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15
    MINUTES,OCCASIONALLY LIF TING THE EYELIDS.SKIN:WASH WITH SOAP &
    PLENTY OF WATER.REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND SHOES. DO NOT
    REUSE UNTIL CLEAN.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:CC
Flash Point:45F,7C TOLUENE
Lower Limits:1.3
Upper Limits:6.7
Extinguishing Media:CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, OR DRY CHEMICAL.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR FIRE FIGHTING PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT AND A
    FULL FACED SELF CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS. EVACUATE AREA. COOL
    FIRE EXPOSED CONTAINERS WITH WATER SPRAY.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:CLOSED CONTAINERS MAY EXPLODE WHEN
    EXPOSED TO EXTREME HEAT. VAPORS TRAVEL TO IGNITION SOURCES & FLASH
    BACK.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:REMOVE ALL SOURCES OF IGNITION.VENTILATE THE
    AREA.WEAR PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENTS.SHUT OFF SOURCE OF LEAK.DIKE &
    CONTAIN LARGE SPILLS & REMOVE WITH VACUUM TRUCKS OR PUMP.SOAK UP
    RESIDUE WITH SAND/CLAY.PICK  UP SMALL SPILLS WITH SAND/CLAY & PUT
    IN CONTAINER

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORAGE-STORE IN A COOL,DRY,VENTILATED
    AREA AWAY FROM HEAT,SPARKS & STRONG OXIDIZERS.
Other Precautions:DO NOT WELD,HEAT/DRILL ON OR NEAR CONTAINER;EVEN
    EMPTY CONTAINERS CAN CONTAIN EXPLOSIVE VAPORS. GROUND EQUIPMENT TO
    PREVENT ACCUMULATION OF STATIC CHARGE. DO NOT BREATHE MIST OR
    VAPOR. DO NOT GET IN E YES, ON SKIN OR ON CLOTHING.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

    RESPIRATOR FOR O RGANIC VAPORS.(REFER TO 29 CFR 1910.134)
