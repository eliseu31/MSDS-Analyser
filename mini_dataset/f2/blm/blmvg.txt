=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MIN. SKIN: WASH WITH PLENTY OF WATER. INGEST: GIVE TWO GLASSES OF
    WATER AND INDUCE VOMITING. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCON
    PERSON.  INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT,
    GIVE O*2.
