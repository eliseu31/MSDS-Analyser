First Aid:EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN; GET IMMED MED
    ASSISTANCE. SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND WASH WITH SOAP

    ASSISTANCE FOR ALL CASES OF OVEREXPOSURE.
