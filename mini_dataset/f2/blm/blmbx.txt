=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE PERSON FROM EXPOSURE AREA. IF BREATHING HAS
    STOPPED, USE MOUTH-TO-MOUTH RESUSCITATION AND GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION. EYE: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN. SKIN: WASH
    WITH SOAP AND WATER . INGEST: GET MD IMMEDIATELY .
