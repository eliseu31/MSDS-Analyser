=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH PROMPTLY WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MINUTES. SEEK MED ATTN IMMED. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY W/SOAP
    AND WATER. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTH AND SEEK MED ATTN. INHAL:
    REMOVE VICT IM TO A WLL VENT AREA. USE O2/ARTF RESPAS REQUIRED.
    SEEK MED ATTN IMMED. INGEST: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. SEEK MED ATTN
    IMMED.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE BELOW 90F IN COOL DRY PLACE,

    EQUIPMENTS.

    PRESSURE. USE WITH ADEQUATE VENTILATION. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF
