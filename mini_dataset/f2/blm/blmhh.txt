First Aid:EYE: FLUSH PROMPTLY WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MIN. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP AND WATER. REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED CLOTH AND SEEK MED ATTN. INHAL: REMOVE TO WELL
    VENTILATED AREA. US E O2 OR ARTF RESP AS REQUIRED. SEEK MEDATTN
    IMMED. INGEST: DO NOT INDUCE VOMIT. SEEK MED ATTN IMMED.
