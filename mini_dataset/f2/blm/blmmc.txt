=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE, RESTORE BREATHING AND NOTIFY
    PHYS. EYE: FLUSH IMMED WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MIN. NOTIFY PHYS. SKIN: WASH AFFECTED AREA WITH SOAP AND WATER.
    REMOVE CONT AMD CLOTHING AND WASH BEFORE REUSE. INGEST: DO NOT
    INDUCE VOMITING. KEEP PERSON WARM, QUIET AND GET MED ATTN.
    ASPIRATION OF MATERIAL INTO LUNGS CAN CAUSE CHEMICAL PNEUMONITIS
    WHICH CAN BE FATAL.
