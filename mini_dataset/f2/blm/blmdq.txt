=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: PROMPT WASHING WITH 99% ISOPROPYL ALCOHOL FOLLOWED BY
    WASHING WITH SOAP AND WATER. EYE: IRRIGATE PROMPTLY WITH CLEAN
    WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES AND CALL A PHYSICIAN. INHALATION:
    TREAT SYMPTOM ATICALLY; VASO-DILATORS, FRESH AIR, OXYGEN. CALL A
    PHYSICIAN. INGESTION: CALL A PHYSICIAN AT ONCE. INDUCE VOMITING ON
    PHYSICIAN'S ADVICE.

    IN AN OPEN CONTAINER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A COOL, DRY AREA IN TIGHTLY
    CLOSED CONTAINERS. DO NOT BREATHE VAPORS.
Other Precautions:AVOID CONTACT WITH SKIN OR CLOTHING. CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHING MUST BE LAUNDERED BEFORE WEARING AGAIN. CONTAMINATED SHOES
    SHOULD BE THOROUGHLY CLEANED OR DISCARDED.
