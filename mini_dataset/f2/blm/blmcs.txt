    LIFTING EYELIDS. SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING, THEN FLUSH
    WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. WASH CLOTHNG THOROUGHLY BEFORE
    REUSE. IN HAL: MOVE TO FRESH AIR. INGEST: CALL MDIMMEDIATELY. IN
    ALL CASES, IF IRRITATION CONTINUES, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION
    IMMEDIATELY.
