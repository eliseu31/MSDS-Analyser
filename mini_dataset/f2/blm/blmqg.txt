=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

LD50 LC50 Mixture:LD50:(ORAL,RAT) 1870 MG/KG
Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:(ACUTE) CAUSES EYE & SKIN IRRIT. MATL
    IS IRRIT TO MUC MEMB & UPPER RESP TRACT. ABSORP IN BODY LEADS TO
    FORMATION OF METHEMOGLOBIN WHICH IN SUFFICIENT CONC CAUSES
    CYANOSIS. ONSET MAY BE DELAYED 2 TO 4  HRS/LONGER. (CHRONIC)
    ANEMIA.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:NOT RELEVANT
Effects of Overexposure:SYMPTOMS OF OVEREXP TO POTASSIUM CHLORATE
    INCLUDE NAUSEA, VOMITING, ABDOMINAL PAIN, DIARRHEA, ANEMIA
    (HEMOLYTIC) AND HEMORRHAGE. METHEMOGLOBIN FORMATION RESULTS FROM
    CATALYTIC OXIDATION OF THE BLOOD.  ANURIA, DMG TO THE LIVER,
    CONVULSIONS DUE TO CENTRAL NERVOUS SYSTEM EFTS AND COMA MAY ALSO
    OCCUR.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES & SKIN: IMMED FLUSH W/COPIOUS AMTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST
    15 MIN WHILE REMOVING CONTAM CLTHG & SHOES. WASH CONTAM CLTHG
    BEFORE REUSE. INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BRTHG, GIVE ARTF
    RESP. IF B RTHG DFCLT, GIVE OXYGEN. INGEST: WASH OUT MOUTH W/WATER
    PROVIDED PERSON IS CONSCIOUS. CALL MD.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:WATER SPRAY.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED SCBA & FULL
    PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT . PREVENT CONTACT WITH SKIN AND EYES.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:CONTACT WITH OTHER MATERIAL MAY CAUSE
    FIRE. EMITS TOXIC FUMES UNDER FIRE CONDITIONS.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:EVACUATE AREA. WEAR NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED SCBA,
    RUBBER BOOTS & HEAVY RUBBER GLOVES. COVER WITH DRY LIME OR SODA
    ASH, PICK UP, KEEP IN CLSD CNTNR & HOLD FOR WASTE DISP. VENT AREA
    AND WASH SPILL SITE AFTE R MATERIAL PICKUP IS COMPLETE.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:IRRITANT. KEEP TIGHTLY CLOSED. KEEP
    AWAY FROM COMBUST MATLS, HEAT, SPARKS, AND OPEN FLAME. STORE IN A
    COOL DRY PLACE. AVOID BRTHG DUST.
Other Precautions:DO NOT GET IN EYES, ON SKIN, ON CLTHG. AVOID
    PRLNGD/RPTD EXPOS. MIXT OF POTASSIUM CHLORATE WITH: HYDROCARBONS,
    METAL PHOSPHIDES (ZN, AG, AL, CU, HG, MG, ETC), METAL THIOCYANATES,
    METAL SULFIDES, ARSEN IC, CARBON, PHOSPHOROUS, (SEE ING 2)
