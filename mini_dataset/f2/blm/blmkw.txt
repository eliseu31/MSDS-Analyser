Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN COOL, DRY, VENTILATED AREA.

Other Precautions:READ ENTIRE LABEL BEFORE USING. NO SMOKING IN AREA OF
    USE. DO NOT USE IN THE GENERAL VICINITY OF ARC WELDING, OPEN FLAMES

    OF HCL AND/O R PHOSGENE .
