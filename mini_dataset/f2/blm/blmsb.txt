=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: SYMPTOMS RESULTING FROM INHALATION OVEREXPOSURE
    USUALLY DISAPPEAR WITHIN 24 HOURS. SYMPTOMATIC TREATMENT, SUCH AS
    BED REST, POSSIBLY ASPRIN, TO AFFORD RELIEF FROM FEVER AND CHILLS.
    EYE: FL USH EYES WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES. SKIN: FLUSH WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER . INGEST: CALL
    MD IMMEDIATELY . IN ALL CASES, CONSULT PHYS FOR MEDICAL ATTENTION.
