=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:CALL A PHYSICIAN.INHALATION:NOT A KNOWN
    PROBLEM.EYES:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES HOLDING
    EYELIDS OPEN.SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING.WASH SKIN WITH SOAP
    AND WATER.INGESTION:IF CONSC IOUS,DRINK PLENTY OF WATER.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NON-FLAMMABLE

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WEAR AIR-SUPPLIED RESPIRATOR FOR UNVENTALATED
    AREA. SWEEP/SCOOP UP INTO CONTAINERS. CLEAN UP RESIDUE WITH 
    1,1,1-TRICHLOROETHANE.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A COOL AREA AWAY FROM OPEN
    FLAMES. DO NOT GET IN EYES, SKIN OR CLOTHING.
Other Precautions:USE IN WELL VENTILATED AREA. AVOID INHALATION OF
    PROPELLANTS. AVOID CONTACT WITH EYES OR SKIN.
