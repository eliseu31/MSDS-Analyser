=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. SKIN: WASH AREA WITH WARM WATER
    AND MILD SOAP. EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN. INGEST:
    IF SWALLOWED, DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. SEE PHYS AS SOON AS POSSIBLE
    AFTER  EXPOSURE.
