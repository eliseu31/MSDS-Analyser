=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:WASH W.SOAP & H*2O. EYES:FLUSH W. H*2O. INGESTED:DON'T
    INDUCE VOMITING. INHALED:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE CPR/OXYGEN IF
    NEED. KEEP WARM & QUIET. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. NEVER GIVE
    ANYTHING  BY MOUTH TO UNCONSCIOUS PERSON.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:414F/213C
Extinguishing Media:CO*2, FOAM, DRY CHEM & H*2O FOG.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED SCBA IN AN ENCLOSED
    AREA IN FIRES
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:WATER MAY CAUSE FROTHING.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:USE FLOOR DRY ABSORBENT FOR SMALL SPILLS.MOP
    OR PUMP LARGE SPILLS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN STEEL DRUM OR TANK BELOW
    120F.
Other Precautions:AVOID CONTACT WITH EYES AND SKIN:DO NOT BREATHE
    VAPORS/MIST.WASH THOROUGHLY AFTER EACH USE.DO NOT APPLY HIGH HEAT
    OR FLAME TO CONTAINER.THE DOT HAZARD LABEL IS NOT REQUIRED.
