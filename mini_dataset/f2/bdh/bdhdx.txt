======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN COOL,DRY AND WELL VENTILATED
    AREA.THESE LUBRICANTS BECOME VERY VISCOUS WHEN STORED AT LOW
    TEMPERATURES.THEY CONGEAL AT -12 TO -32 C.

    VAPORS/MIST;WASH THOROUGHLY AFTER EACH USE.
