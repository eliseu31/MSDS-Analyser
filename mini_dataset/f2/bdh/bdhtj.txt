=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING & WIPE EXCESS OFF. WASH WITH
    SOAP & WATER. LAUNDER CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE. IF IRRITATION
    PERSISTS, GET ME DICAL ATTENTION. INHALATION: REMOVE VICTIM TO
    FRESH AIR & PROVIDE OXYGEN IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT. GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION. INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. SET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION/ADVICE.
