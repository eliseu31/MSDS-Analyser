First Aid:SHOULD EYE CONTACT OCCUR,FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER;SKIN
    CONTACT:WASH W.SOAP & WATER,OIL SOAKED CLOTH SHOULD BE CLEANED
    BEFORE REUSE.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:COC
Flash Point:450F(232C)
Extinguishing Media:WTR SPRAY,DRY CHEM,FOAM,CO*2-WTR,FOAM MAY CAUSE
    FROTHING.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE WTR TO COOL FIRE EXPOSED CONTAINERS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:IF LEAK HAS NOT IGN,USE WTR SPRAY TO
    DISPERSE THE VAPORS AND TO PROVIDE PROTECTION FOR PERSONS.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:REMOVE IGNITION SOURCES,VENTILATE AREA
    WELL.ABSORB WITH VERMICULITE OR OTHER MATERIALS, SUCH AS
    SAWDUST,RAGS,PAPER & PLACE IN CLOSED CONTAINER. USE NON SPARKING
    TOOLS. WEAR PROPER PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT  DURING CLEAN UP.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:MINIMUM FEASIBLE HANDLING TEMPS.SHOULD
    BE MAINTAINED.PERIODS OF EXPOSURE TO HIGH TEMPS SHOULD BE
    MINIMIZED.WATER CONTAMINATION SHOULD BE AVOIDED.
Other Precautions:PRODUCT HAS BEEN EVALUATED FOR RCRA CHARACTERISTICS
    AND DOES NOT MEET CRITERIA OF A HAZARDOUS WASTE IF DISCARDED IN ITS
    PURCHASED FORM.
