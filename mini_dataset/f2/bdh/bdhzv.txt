=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EXCESSIVE INHALATION CALLS FOR REMOVAL TO UNCONTAMINATED
    AIR,USE OF OXYGEN OR MOUTH TO MOUTH BREATHING IF NECESSARY.EYE
    CONTACT CALLS FOR FLUSHING WITH WATER TO REMOVE MATERIAL.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:85F TOC
Extinguishing Media:CARBON DIOXIDE OR DRY CHEMICAL POWDER
Fire Fighting Procedures:SAME AS FOR ANY FLAMMABLE LIQUID.ADHESIVE IS
    CLASS I-C FLAMM
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:NONE

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:AVOID BREATHING VAPORS,REMOVE SOURCES OF
    IGNITION.GOOD GENERAL VENTILATION SHOULD MAINTAIN CONCENTRATION
    BELOW 200 PPM.PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT,SUCH AS AIR PURIFYING OR
    AIR-SUPPLYING RESPIRATORS CAN BE US ED IF ADEQUATE VENTILATION IS
    NOT AVAILABLE.
