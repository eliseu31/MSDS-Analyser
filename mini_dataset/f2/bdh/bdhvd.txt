=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES WHILE HOLDING EYELIDS
    OPEN. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN: WASH WITH SOAP & WATER/WATERLESS
    CLEANER. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND LAUNDER BEFORE REUSE. IF
    IRRITA TION PERSISTS SEE A DOCTOR. INHALED:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    GIVE OXYGEN IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT AND GET MEDICAL CARE.
    INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. IN GENERAL, NO TREATMENT 
    REQUIRED. SEE DOCTOR.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:COC
Flash Point:340F,171C
Extinguishing Media:USE WATER FOG, CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, DRY CHEMICAL,
    SAND OR EARTH.
