=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:IF SWALLOWED, DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING, CALL A PHYSICIAN
    IMMEDIATELY. IF SPLASHED INTO THE EYES, PURGE WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS
    OF WATER. IN CASE OF SKIN CONTACT, CLEANSE WITH WATERLESS SOAP
    FOLLOWED BY THO ROUGH WASHING WITH SOAP & WATER.
