=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES WHILE HOLDING EYELIDS
    OPEN. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN: WASH WITH SOAP & WATER/WATERLESS
    CLEANER. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. IF IRRITATION PERSISTS, GET
    MEDICA L ATTENTION. INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. GET MEDICAL
    ADVICE.
