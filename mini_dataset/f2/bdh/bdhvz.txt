=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:FLUSH WITH WATER 15 MIN.SKIN:FLUSH WITH
    WATER.INTERNAL:GIVE LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER OR MILK,FOLLOW WITH
    MILK OF MAGNESIA.GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:USE MEDIUM SUITABLE FOR SURROUNDING FIRE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APP.PROTECTIVE
    CLOTHING
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:NONE

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ELIMINATE IGNITION SOURCES.NEUTRALIZE WITH
    SODA ASH.FLOOD WITH WATER.FLUSH TO SEWER IN ACCORDANCE WITH
    FEDERAL,STATE AND LOCAL REGULATIONS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN COOL,DRY,WELL-VENTED
    AREA.KEEP AWAY FROM ALKALI.KEEP CONTAINERS CLOSED.PROTECT FROM
    PHYSICAL DAMAGE.
