=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN AND EYES- FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER. IF IRRITATION
    DEVELOPS, CALL A PHYSICIAN.  INHALATION- REMOVE TO FRESH AIR AND
    SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION.  INGESTION- PROCEDURES NORMALLY NOT NEEDED.
    IF LARGE Q UANTITIES ARE INGESTED, SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION.
