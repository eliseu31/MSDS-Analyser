=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Effects of Overexposure:LOW TOXICITY MAY CAUSE MILD SKIN IRRIT ON
    REPEATED/PROLONGED CNTCT. MAY CAUSE EYE BURNS.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:IN CASE OF SKIN CNTCT, WASH THOROUGHLY W/SOAP & WARM WATER.
    IF SPLASHED INTO EYES, FLUSH W/CLEAR WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN OR
    UNTIL IRRIT SUBSIDES. CALL MD.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:440F COC
Lower Limits:1
Upper Limits:6
Extinguishing Media:FOAM, DRY CHEM, CO*2, WATER SPRAY, FOG
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE AIR-SUPP EQUIP IN ENCL AREA. WATER-COOL
    EXPOSED CNTNRS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:AVOID BRTHNG VAPOR OR FUMES. DO NOT MIX
    OR STORE W/STRONG OXIDANTS LIKE LIQ CL OR CONC OXYGEN.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:RECOVER FREE LIQUID AND ABSORBENT TO SPILL
    AREA, KEEP PRODUCT OUT OF SWERS AND WATER COURSES BY DIKING OR
    IMPOUNDING. ADVISE AUTHORITIES IF PRODUCT HAS OR MAY ENTER SEWERS,
    WATER COURSES, SENSITIVE LA ND AREAS

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP CNTNRS CLOSED WHEN NOT IN USE. DO
    NOT HANDLE OR STORE NEAR HEAT, SPARKS, FLAME OR STRONG OXIDANTS.
Other Precautions:AVOID BRTHNG OIL MIST. REMOVE OIL-SOAKED CLOTHING &
    LAUNDER BEFORE REUSE. DISCARD OIL-SOAKED SHOES. WASH SKIN
    THOROUGHLY AFTER CNTCT.
