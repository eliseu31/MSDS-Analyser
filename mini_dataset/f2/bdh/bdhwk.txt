=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH RUNNING WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES WHILE LIFTING EYELIDS OPEN. IF IRRITATION DEVELOPS OR
    PERSISTS, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN: WASH WITH SOAP AND PLENTY OF
    WATER. IF I RRITATION DEVELOPS OR PERSISTS, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    VAPOR INHALATION UNLIKELY: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IF REQUIRED. INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. GET
    MEDICAL ATTENTION.
