=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:USE FIRST AID. CALL MEDICAL EMERGENCY CARE. REMOVE/ISOLATE
    COMTAMINATED CLOTHING. SHOWER VICTIM WITH SOAP AND WATER. DETAIN
    PERSONNEL/EQUIPMENT EXPOSED. ADVISE MEDICAL PERSONNEL THAT VICTIM
    MAY BE CON TAMINATED.
