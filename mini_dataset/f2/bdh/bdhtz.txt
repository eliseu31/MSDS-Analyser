=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:331F;166C (PMCC)
Extinguishing Media:WATER FOG,FOAM,DRY CHEM,CO*2
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE PROTECTIVE CLOTHING & SCBA. COOL CANS W/
    WATER.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:DO NOT USE A DIRECT STREAM OF WATER.
    PRODUCT WILL FLOAT AND CAN BE REIGNITED ON SURFACE OF WATER.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SCOOP UP EXCESS GREASE. CLEAN AREA W/
    APPROPRIATE CLEANER. KEEP OUT OF SURFACE WATERS & ANY WATER COURSES
    OR SEWERS ENTERING OR LEADING TO SURFACE WATERS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A COOL,DRY PLACE W/ ADEQUATE
    VENT. KEEP FROM OPEN FLAMES & HI TEMP. WASH BEFORE
    SMOKING,EATING,ETC.
Other Precautions:FIRST AID PROCEDURES: SKIN- IF IRRIT PERSIST,CALL MD.
    EYES- FLUSH W/ H*2O FOR 15 MIN HOLDING EYELIDS APART. SEE MD. HAZ
    DECOMPOSITION PROD: PHOSPHOROUS OXIDES & UNIDENTIFIED ORGANIC
    MATERIALS.
