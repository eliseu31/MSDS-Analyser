=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Effects of Overexposure:AFFECT CENTRAL NERVOUS SYSTEM & CAUSE
    RESPIRATORY IRRITATION.PARALYSIS BY HIGH CONCENTRATIONS.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN CONTACT:WASH WITH SOAP & WATER.AN EMOLIENT CREAM OR
    LOTION IS BENEFICIAL.EYE CONTACT:WASH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR 5
    MINUTES.INHALATION:PROVIDE FRESH AIR & REST.USE ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRATION IF NEED ED.INGESTION:DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.CALLA
    DOCTOR

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:25F TCC.
Lower Limits:1.0
Upper Limits:6.8
Extinguishing Media:MECHANICAL FOAM,DRY CHEMICAL,WATER FOG,CO*2
Fire Fighting Procedures:A STRAIGHT WATER STREAM WOULD SPREAD OIL FIRES
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:A VAOR ACCUMULATION WOULD FLASH &/OR
    EXPLODE IF IGNITED.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:AVOID OPEN FLAME & OTHER POSSIBLE IGNITION
    SOURCES.PROVIDE ADEQUATE VENTILATION.COLLECT IN APPROVED STORAGE
    CONTAINERS & DISPOSE OF ALL SPILLED LIQUID.DO NOT FLUSH TO SEWERS
    OR CLOSED DRAINS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP CONTAINERS SUCH AS CAN OR BOTTLE
    CLOSED.STORE IN COOL,DARK,WELL VENTILATED PLACE.AVOID EVERY SOURCE
    OF IGNITION.PROTECT CONTAINERS AGAIST DAMAGE.
Other Precautions:WEAR RUBBER GLOVES,FACE PROTECTING BOARD,ALL PURPOSE
    GAS MASK OR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS & OVERALLS.
