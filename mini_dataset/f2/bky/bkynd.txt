    CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:SCC
Flash Point:>110F
Extinguishing Media:SMALL FIRES: CO2 & DRY CHEMICAL FOAM. LARGE FIRES:
    FOG SPRAY OR FOAM.
Fire Fighting Procedures:KEEP CONTAINERS COOL W/WATER SPRAY. USE AIR
    MASKS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:MATERIAL IS A STRONG OXIDIZING AGENT; MAY
    INCREASE FIRE HAZARD OF ORGANIC MATERIALS. EXPOSURE TO TEMPS >120F
    MAY CAUSE DELAYED COMPOSITION AND FIRES.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ABSORB W/NON-COMBUSTIBLE MATERIAL LIKE
    VERMICULITE OR PERLITE. SWEEP UP W/NON-SPARKING TOOLS & THEN REMOVE
    THE MIXTURE TO A SAFE OPEN AREA FOR DISPOSAL BY BURNING. AREA OF
    SPILL RESIDUE SHOULD BE THOR OUGHLY SCRUBBED W/DETERGENT WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE AWAY FROM HAZARDOUS MATERIALS &
    SOURCES OF IGNITION & HEAT. STORE BETWEEN 60-65F TO MAINTAIN ACTIVE
    OXYGEN CONTACT. KEEP AWAY FROM FOOD/DRINKS.
Other Precautions:KEEP COOL & AWAY FROM DIRECT SUNLIGHT. AVOID
    INHALATION OF FUMES.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:USE AIR MASKS.
Ventilation:USE LOCAL EXHAUST VENTILATION TO KEEP 120F, HEAT &
    SOURCES OF IGNITION

====================  Disposal Considerations  ====================

Waste Disposal Methods:DISPOSE IN COMPLIANCE W/LOCAL, STATE & FEDERAL
    REGULATIONS. IGNITE W/LONG (6 FEET TORCH). SALVAGE OF SPILLED
    PEROXIDES SHOULDN'T BE ATTEMPTED.
