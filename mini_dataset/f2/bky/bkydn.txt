=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: WASH W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER. SKIN: WASH AREA W/SOAP
    OR MILD DETERGENT FOR 5 MINS. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR,
    ADMINISTER OXYGEN IF RESPIRATION IS DIFFICULT. IF NOT BREATHING
    BEGIN ARTIFIC IAL RESPIRATION. IF PULSE NOT DETECTED BEGIN EXTERNAL
    HEART MESSAGE. INGESTION: OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
