=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN/EYES: FLUSH W/FLOWING WATER FOR 15 MINS. SKIN: WASH
    W/SOAP & WATER. INGESTION: DRINK LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER OR MILK.
    DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. OBTAIN
    MEDICAL ATTENTI ON IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:105F
Lower Limits:1.7%
Upper Limits:11%
Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, CO2, ALCOHOL & WATER SPRAY.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WATER SHOULD BE USED TO COOL FIRE EXPOSED
    CONTAINERS & TO DISPERSE UNIGNITED VAPORS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:EXPLOSION HAZARD FOR ISOBUTYL ALCOHOL IF
    EXPOSED TO EXTREME HEAT OR FLAME. RELEASE OF HYDROGEN GAS WHICH
    COULD BE EXPLOSIVE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WEAR PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT. REMOVE ALL SOURCES
    OF IGNITION. ABSORB W/INERT MATERIAL. SHOVEL OR SWEEP UP, PLACE IN
    A DOT APPROVED CONTAINER & SEAL. VENTILATE AREA.
