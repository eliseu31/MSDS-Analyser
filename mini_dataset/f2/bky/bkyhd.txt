=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS.
    SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING, GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. IF
    BREATHING  IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN. INGESTION: OBTAIN MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:THE HYDROLYSIS/NEUTRALIZATION
    REACTIONS MAY GENERATE HEAT/FUMES WHICH CAN BE CONTROLLED BY THE
    RATE OF ADDITION. STORE IN A COOL DRY PLACE.
Other Precautions:KEEP TIGHTLY CLOSED. AVOID CONTACT W/EYES, SKIN,
    CLOTHING & INHALATION OF DUST. HYGROSCOPIC. AVOID RAISING DUST.
