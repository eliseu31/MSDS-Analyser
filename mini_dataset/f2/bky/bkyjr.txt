=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/WATER OR NORMAL SALINE FOR 15-20 MIN. SKIN:
    WASH W/SOAP & WATER OR DETERGENT W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR 15-20 MIN.
    INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION IF
    BREATHING ST OPS. MAINTAIN AIRWAY/BLOOD PRESSURE & ADMINISTER
    OXYGEN. KEEP WARM & A REST. INGESTION: GASTRIC LAVAGE W/CUFFED
    ENDOTRACHEAL TUBE IN PLACE TO PREVENT ASPIRATION, SHOULD BE DONE
    WITHIN 15 MIN. SEE SUPP

    FLARES IN HAZARD AREA. KEEP U NNECESSARY PEOPLE AWAY.
