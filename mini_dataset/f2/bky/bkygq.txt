=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR, GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION
    IF BREATHING HAS STOPPED. EYES: FLUSH W/COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER.
    SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INGESTION: DON'T INUDCE VOMITING. OBTAIN
    MEDIC AL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:120F
Extinguishing Media:USE FOAM, DRY CHEMICAL, CO2, WATER FOG
Fire Fighting Procedures:SCBA RECOMMENDED. WATER MAY BE UNSUITABLE AS
    AN EXTINGUISHING MEDIUM. USE WATER TO COOL CINTAINERS/SURFACES.
    NEVER USE A DIRECT STREAM OF WATER.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:VAPORS ARE >AIR & TRAVEL ALONG GROUND TO
    AN IGNITION SOURCE/MAY ACCUMULATE IN LOW AREAS OF POOR VENTILATION.
    CLOSED CONTAINERS CAN BUILDUP WHEN EXPOSED TO HEAT.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:REMOVE SOURCES OF IGNITION, INCLUDING HOT
    METAL SURFACES. CLEAN UP SPILL IMMEDIATELY BY SOAKING UP
    W/ABSORBANT SUCH AS CLAY, SAND, DIATOMACEOUS EARTH, ETC.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP COOL, DRY, & AWAY FROM IGNITION
    SOURCES. STORE IN WELL VENTILATED AREA. AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN &
    EYES. USE W/ADEQUATE VENTILATION.
Other Precautions:DON'T INHALATION VAPORS. GROUND DRUMS WHEN POURING OR
    OTHERWISE DISPENSING. DON'T WELD, DRILL, CUT OR HEAT FULL, PARTIAL,
    OR EVEN EMPTY CONTAINERS.
