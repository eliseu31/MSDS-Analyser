Spill Release Procedures:SWEEP UP & PLACE IN APPROPRIATE DISPOSAL

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN CLOSED, PROPERLY LABELED

Other Precautions:DON'T TRANSFER TO UNMARKED CONTAINERS.
