=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: WASH W/WATER/NORMAL SALINE. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP/MILD
    DETERGENT/PLENTY OF WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF
    BREATHING HAS STOPPED, GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. KEEP WARM & AT
    REST. INGESTI ON: IF CONSCIOUS, REMOVE BY GASTRIC LAVAGE/INDUCE
    EMESTS W/IPECAC SYRUP. KEEP HEAD BELOW HIPS TO PREVENT ASPIRATION.
    TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY/SUPPORTIVELY. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES. SEE SUPP
