=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR 15 MIN. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER.
    INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING.
    NOTE: DECISION OF WHETHER TO INDUCE VOMITING OR NOT SHOULD BE MADE
    BY PHYS ICIAN DUE TO POSSIBILITY THAT MATERIAL MAY BE ASPIRATED
    CONSISTS SYSTEMIC EFFECTS. IF LAVAGE IS PERFORMED, ENDOTRACHEAL &
    ESOPHAGEAL CONTROL IS SUGGESTED. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES.
