    BY A PHYSICIAN. NOTE TO PHYSICIAN: DECISION TO INDUCE VOMITING OR

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SMALL SPILLS: FLUSH AWAY W/WATER. FLOOR MAY

    STOP SPILL AT SOURCE.
