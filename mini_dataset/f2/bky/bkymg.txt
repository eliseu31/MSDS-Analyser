=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. SKIN:
    WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE CPR OR
    OXYGEN IF NEEDED. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. NEVER GIVE
    ANYTHING BY M OUTH TO AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. OBTAIN MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC

Fire Fighting Procedures:DON'T USE WATER OR HALOGENATED EXTINGUISHING

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:USE NON_ SPARKING TOOLS. SHOVEL & PLACE IN
    CLOSED CONTAINER. ADD ABSORBENT TO SPILL. EVACUATE AREA.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

    MIST.
