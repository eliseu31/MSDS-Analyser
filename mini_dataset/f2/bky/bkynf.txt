=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION
    OR OXYGEN IF NEEDED. GET MEDICAL HELP FROM ANESTHESIOLOGIST. SKIN:
    WASH W/SOAP & WATER. EYES: WASH W/COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER.
    INGESTION: I NDUCE VOMITING. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NONE
Extinguishing Media:USE METHODS SUITABLE FOR SURROUNDINGS FIRES.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:VAPORS WILL COLLECT IN LOW AREAS.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ALLOW SMALL SPILLS TO DISSIPATE W/GOOD
    VENTILATION. FOR LARGE SPILLS, WEAR PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT & ABSORB
    ON VERMICULITE, & THEN PLACE IN CLOSED CONTAINER.
