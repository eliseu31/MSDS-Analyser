=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. RESTORE BREATHING. TREAT
    SYMPTOMATICALLY. EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINS. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INGESTION: DRINK 1 TO 2 GLASSES OF
    WATER TO DI LUTE. DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY.
    OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE AWAY FROM EXTREME HEAT/FIRE/OPEN
    FLAME/E/SPARKS/ELECTRICAL EQUIP. STORE IN WELL VENTILATED AREAS.
    AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN OR BREATHING VAPORS.
Other Precautions:KEEP CLOSURE TIGHT & CONTAINER UPRIGHT TO PREVENT
    LEAKAGE. DRUMS SHOULD BE GROUNDED & BONDED WHEN POURING. WASH OUT
    OR USE CONTAINERS FOR OTHER PURPOSES. DON'T TAKE INTERNALLY OR USE
    CUTTING TORCH/WEL DING ON OR NEAR DRUM. DON'T SMOKE.
