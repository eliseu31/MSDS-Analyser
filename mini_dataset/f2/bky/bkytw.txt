=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR 15 MIN. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP &
    WATER. INGESTION/INHALATION: OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:WATER, CO2 & DRY CHEMICAL
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS & FULL
    PROTECTIVE CLOTHING.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SCOOP UP TABLETS & PLACE IN SUITABLE
    CONTAINER. IF TABLETS ARE CRUSHED, CLEAN UP LARGER PIECES, CLEAN
    REMAINING POWDER W/DAMP CLOTH TOWELS TO SUPPRESS DUST. CLEAN
    W/DETERGENT & WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE AT ROOM TEMPERATURE. WHEN STORED
    AT ROOM TEMPERTURE IN UNOPENED CONTAINER, MATERIAL IS STABLE TO
    EXPIRATION DATE INDICATED ON LABEL OF PRODUCT.
Other Precautions:AVOID CUTS FROM GLASS IF BOTTLE BREAKS.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============
