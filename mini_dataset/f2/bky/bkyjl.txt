=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. EYES: FLUSH THOROUGHLY W/WATER.
    INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. INGESTION: DRINK 1 OR 2 GLASSES OF
    WATER. IF CONSCIOUS, INDUCE VOMITING. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN
    ALL CAS ES. NOTE TO PHYSICIAN: IN ACUTE BARIUM POISONING, A
    CONSTANT FINDING IS A RAPID DECREASE IN SERUM-POTASSIUM LEVEL
    (HYPOKALEMIA). INFUSION OF POTASSIUM IS INDICATED.
