=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: WASH W/SOAP/WATER. EYE: FLUSH W/WATER FOR 15 MINS.
    INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. APPLY CPR/OXYGEN IF REQUIRED.
    INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. FORCE FLUIDS/GIVE ACTIVATED
    CHARCOAL TABLETS.  OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
    PHYSICIAN'S NOTE: USE GASTRIC LAVAGE ONLY AFTER ENDOTRACHEAL
    INTUBATION IN VIEW OF ASPIRATION RISK FOR WHICH
    ANTIBIOTIC/CORTICOSTERIOD THERAPY MAY BE INDICATED.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:COC
Flash Point:356F
Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, FOAM, WATER SPRAY, OR CO2
Fire Fighting Procedures:ADDITION OF WATER/FOAM CAUSES FROTHING. WEAR
    SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS. DON'T POINT SOLID WATER STREAM
    DIRECTLY INTO BURNING TO AVOID SPREADING.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:AUTOIGNITION TEMPERATURE: 455F. USE WATER
    SPRAY TO COOL FIRE-EXPOSED CONTAINERS & AS A PROTECTIVE SCREEN.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:CONTAIN SPILL. ABSORB W/INERT ABSORBENT SUCH
    AS DRY CLAY, SAND, OR DIATOMACEOUS EARTH, COMMERCIAL SORBENTS, OR
    RECOVER USING PUMPS. SCOOP UP USED ABSORBENT & RAGS INTO DRUMS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T CUT, DRILL, OR WELD EMPTY
    CONTAINERS. AVOID PROLONGED CONTACT W/SKIN. STORE IN COOL,
    WELL-VENTILATED AREA. AVOID INHALATION.
Other Precautions:AVOID SPLASHING, HIGH-PRESSURE SYSTEMS, & FORMATION
    OF OIL MIST.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:IF MIST IS GENERATED BY HEATING OR SPRAYING,
    WEAR APPROVED ORGANIC VAPOR RESPIRATOR SUITABLE FOR OIL MIST IN
    AREAS W/SUFFICIENT OXYGEN.
Ventilation:GENERAL VENTILATION
Protective Gloves:NITRILE OR NEOPRENE
Eye Protection:CHEMICAL GOGGLES
Other Protective Equipment:OIL-RESISTANT CLOTHING
Work Hygienic Practices:REMOVE/LAUNDER CONTAMINATED CLOTHING BEFORE
    REUSE. DISCARD SATURATED LEATHER ARTICLES. WASH HANDS BEFORE
    EATING.
