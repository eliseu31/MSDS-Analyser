=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF
    WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INGESTION:
    DRINK 1 OR 2 GLASSES OF WATER TO DILUTE. DON'T INDUCE VOMITING.
    OBTAIN MEDICA L ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NONE
Extinguishing Media:CO2, DRY CHEMICAL, FOAM, OR OTHER NATIONAL FIRE
    PROTECTION ASSOCIATION APPROVED METHOD FOR TREATING A CLASS B FIRE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE FULL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT & SCBA. WATER
    SPRAY MAY BE INEFFECTIVE. FOG NOZZLES PREFERABLE. USE WATER TO COOL

Spill Release Procedures:WASH W/WATER. CONTAIN SPILLED MATERIAL &
    REMOVE W/INERT ABSORBENT. STORE IN CLOSED CONTAINER UNTIL PROPERLY
    DISPOSED OF.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE <110F & KEEP FROM FREEZING. KEEP
    CONTAINER CLOSED WHEN NOT IN USE. DON'T REUSE EMPTY CONTAINERS.
    KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.
Other Precautions:DON'T BREATH VAPORS, SPRAY MIST, OR SANDING DUST.
    AVOID CONTACT W/EYES. USE ADEQUATE VENTILATION TO KEEP AIR
    CONTAMINANT CONCENTRATION LOW IN THE MIXING/APPLYING/CURING AREAS.
