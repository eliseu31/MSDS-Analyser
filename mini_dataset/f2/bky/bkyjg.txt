=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN/EYES: FLUSH W/WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION/OXYGEN IF NECESSARY. INGESTION: DON'T
    INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE PLENTY OF WATER & GLASSES OF MILK. NEVER GIVE
    ANYTHIN G BY MOUTH TO UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. KEEP AIRWAY CLEAR. OBTAIN
    MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES. PHYSICIAN NOTE: NO SPECIFIC
    ANTIDOTE. GIVE SUPPORTIVE CARE. BASE TREATMENT ON PATIENT RESPONSE.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NON-FLAMMABLE
Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, CO2. AVOID USING WATER OR FOAM TO
    PREVENT SPREADING OF MATERIAL.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE WATER TO KEEP FIRE-EXPOSED CONTAINERS
    COOL. WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS IN PRESSURE-DEMAND IN
    CONFINED AREAS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:AUTOIGNITION TEMPERATURE: 850F.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:STOP LEAK/CONTAIN SPILL. REMOVE CONTAMINATED
    SOIL. DON'T USE VACUUM TRUCK. SOAK UP REMAINING TRACE ON INERT
    ABSORBENT MATERIAL (RAG/SAND/CLAY/SAWDUST) PLACE IN CLOSED,
    LABELED, SOUND CONTAINER OUTDOOR S. WASH AREA W/WATER. WEAR
    PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN, EYES, OR
    CLOTHING. AVOID INGESTION & INHALATION OF SPRAY MIST. STAY OUT OF
    SMOKE WHEN BURNING EMPTY CONTAINERS.
Other Precautions:STORE IN DRY LOCATION AWAY FROM CHILDREN, ANIMALS,
    FOODS, FEEDS, SEEDS, OR OTHER AGRICULTURAL CHEMICALS. DON'T USE,
    POUR, SPILL, OR STORE NEAR HEAT OR OPEN FLAME. DON'T APPLY WHEN
    WEATHER CONDITIONS F AVOR DRIFT FROM TARGET AREA.
