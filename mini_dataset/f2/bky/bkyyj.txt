=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:WATER, CO2, OR DRY CHEMICAL.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS &
    PROTECTIVE CLOTHING.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SMALL: WIPE LIQUIDS W/ABSORBENT GAUZE PADS.
    WIPE SOLIDS W/WET ABSORBENT GAUZE. USE DETERGENT & WATER TO CLEAN.
    LARGE: COVER LIQUID W/ABSROBENT SHEETS, PADS OR PILLOWS. COVER
    POWDER W/DAMP CLOTH OR TOW ELS. USE DETERGENTS & WATER TO CLEAN.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE AT OR <77F. AVOID CUTS FROM
    GLASS IF VIALS BREAK.
Other Precautions:FEDERAL LAW PROHIBITS DISPENSING W/OUT PRESCRIPTION.
    USE DOUBLE GLOVING FOR CLEANING UP SPILLS. CHANGE GLOVES REGULARLY
    & REMOVE CONTAMINATED GLOVES IMMEDIATELY.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:USE NIOSH APPROVED DUST RESPIRATOR, HIGH
    EFFICIENCY DUST FILTER, OR POWDERED AIR-PURIFYING RESPIRATOR IF
    BIOLOGICAL SAFETY CABINET, OR SUITABLE VENTILATION IS NOT
    AVAILABLE.
Ventilation:PROVIDE BIOLOGICAL SAFETY CABINET FOR PREPARATION, OR AS
    DETERMINED APPROPRIATE BY INDUSTRIAL HYGIENIST.
Protective Gloves:DISPOSABLE SURGICAL LATEX
Eye Protection:SAFETY GLASSES, FACE SHIELD, GOGGLES
Other Protective Equipment:PROTECTIVE DISPOSABLE GOWN OF LOW
    PERMEABILITY FABRIC W/CLOSED FRONT, LONG SLEEVES, & ELASTIC CUFFS.
Work Hygienic Practices:WASH BEFORE EATING/SMOKING. DON'T
    EAT/DRINK/SMOKE/CHEW GUM/APPLY COSMETICS/STORE FOOD NEAR PRODUCT.
