=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF LOW PRESSURE WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINS. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR. IF CASE OF RESPIRATORY DISTRESS, GIVE OXYGEN OR CPR.
    INGESTION:	DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. ASPIRATION INTO THE LUNGS WILL
    CAUSE SEVERE CHEMICAL PNEUMONIA. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:-45F
Lower Limits:1.3%
Upper Limits:8.2%
Extinguishing Media:FOAM, DRY CHEMICAL, & CO2
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT & SELF-CONTAINED
    BREATHING APPARATUS IN ENCLOSED SPACES. COOL TANKS & CONTAINERS
    EXPOSED TO FIRE W/WATER FOG/SPRAY.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:RELEASES INVISIBLE VAPORS >AIR AT NORMAL

    EXPLODE. HEATED CONTAINERS MAY RUPTURE. FLAMMABLE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:STOP FLOW OF MATERIAL. REMOVE ALL SOURCES OF
    IGNITION. CONTAIN SPILL USING SAND/CLAY/DIATOMACEOUS EARTH/OR
    ABSORBENT MATERIALS. EVACUATE AREA. RUN-OFF MAY CREATE AN EXPLOSION
    HAZARD. FLUSH CONTAMINATE D AREA W/WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP AWAY FROM SPARKS, OPEN FLAME, &
    HEAT. KEEP CONTAINERS CLOSED. USE ONLY W/ADEQUATE VENTILATION.
Other Precautions:INSTALL ALL ELECTRICAL EQUIPMENT IN STORAGE/USE AREAS
    IN ACCORDANCE W/THE NATIONAL ELECTRIC CODE. AVOID CONTACT W/EYES &
    SKIN. AVOID SPLASHING. ASPIRATION WILL CAUSE CHEMICAL PNEUMONIA.
