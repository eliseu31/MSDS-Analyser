=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER.
    INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE OXYGEN IF NEEDED. INGESTION:
    DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
