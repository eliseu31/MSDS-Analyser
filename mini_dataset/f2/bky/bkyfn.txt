=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS.
    SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:FOAM, DRY CHEMICAL, OR CO2
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS & FULL
    PROTECTIVE CLOTHING. USE WATER TO COOL EXPOSED CONTAINERS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:FLASHBACK MAY OCCUR ALONG VAPOR TRAIL.
    CONTAINER MAY EXPLODE IF EXPOSED TO EXTREME HEAT. AEROSOL
    CONTAINER. VAPORS >AIR & MAY COLLECT IN LOW AREAS.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:USE ABSORBENT MATERIALS OR SCRAPE UP DRIED
    MATERIAL & PLACE INTO CONTAINERS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP FROM HEAT OR FLAMES. CONTENTS
    UNDER PRESSURE. DON'T EXPOSE TO TEMPERATURES >120F. AVOID CONTACT
    W/OXIDIZING MATERIALS.
Other Precautions:AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN & EYES.
