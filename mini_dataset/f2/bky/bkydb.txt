=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR 15 MIN. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP &
    WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR & RESTORE BREATHING. KEEP
    WARM & QUIET. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE SEVERAL
    GLASSES OF W ATER. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCONSCIOUS
    PERSON. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:PMCC
Flash Point:22F
Lower Limits:0.9%
Extinguishing Media:CO2, DRY CHEMICAL OR FOAM
Fire Fighting Procedures:WATER SPRAY MAY BE INEFFECTIVE. IF WATER IS

    & REMOVE W/INERT ABSORBENT.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP CONTAINER TIGHTLY CLOSED WHEN NOT
    IN USE. USE W/ADEQUATE VENTILATION. AVOID BREATHING VAPOR/SPRAY
    MIST & CONTACT W/SKIN/EYES. DON'T INGEST.
Other Precautions:USE APPROVED BONDING/GROUNDING PROCEDURES. KEEP AWAY
    FROM CHILDREN. TRANSFER ONLY TO APPROVED CONTAINERS W/APPROPRIATE
    LABELING. DON'T SMOKE. INTENTIONAL MISUSE BY DELIBERATELY
    CONCENTRATING & INHALIN G THE CONTENTS CAN BE HARMFUL/FATAL.
