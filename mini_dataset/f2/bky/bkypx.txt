=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:EYES: IRRITATION. SKIN: DEFATTING,
    REDNESS, CRACKING, BLISTERING, IRRITATION, & SYSTEMIC EFFECTS.
    INHALATION: RESPIRATORY TRACT IRRITATION, CNS DEPRESSION, HEADACHE,
    WEAKNESS, DIZZINESS, INCOORDINATIO N, COLLAPSE, & LUNG CHANGES.
    INGESTION:GASTROINTESTINAL DISTURBANCES SUCH AS NAUSEA, VOMITING, &
    DIARRHEA.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:NONE
Effects of Overexposure:INGESTION: ASPIRATION OF THIS PRODUCT INTO THE
    LUNG MAY CAUSE CHEMICAL PNEUMONITIS WHICH CAN BE FATAL/HARMFUL.
    SKIN: REDNESS, ITCHING, BURNING.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:DISEASE IN OR A HISTORY OF AILMENTS
    INVOLVING THE SKIN, EYE, RESPIRATORY TRACT, LIVER, KIDNEY, CENTRAL
    NERVOUS SYSTEM ARE AT A GREATER THAN NORMAL RISK OF DEVELOPING
    ADVERSE EFFECTS.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR 15 MINS. SKIN: WASH
    THOROUGHLY W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE 1 GLASS OF MILK OR 1 TO 2 OZ
    (30 TO 60G)  OF ACTIVIATED CHARCOAL IN WATER TO VICTIM AS
    TOLERATED. DON'T ATTEMPT TO GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN
    UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. NOTE TO PHYS: THERE IS NO SPECIFIC ANTIDOTES
    FOR EFFECTS FROM OVEREXPOSURE.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:23F
Extinguishing Media:USE CO2, DRY CHEMICAL, WATER SPRAY, ALCOHOL OR
    UNIVERSAL TYPE FOAMS.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SCBA & FULL PROTECTIVE CLOTHING. USE
    WATER TO COOL FIRE-EXPOSED CONTAINER, STRUCTURE/PROTECT PERSONNEL.
    CLOSED CONTAINERS MAY EXPLODE WHEN EXPOSED TO HEAT.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:VAPORS FROM FLAMMABLE/EXPLOSIVE MIXTURES
    IN AIR. VAPORS ARE >AIR, MAY TRAVEL THE (GROUND, PITS, SEWER) TO
    IGNITION SOURCE-FLASH. FLAMMABLE LIQUID.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:STOP SPILL & LEAD IF NO RISK IS INVOLVED.
    ELIMINATE ALL SOURVES OF IGNITION. VENTILATE AREA. AVOID CONTACT
    W/HOT LIQUID & FUMES. USE AN INERT ABSORBENT TO COMPLETE A
    CLEAN-UP. TAKE UP CAREFULLY TO AVO ID HEAT & SPARKS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN, EYES, &
    BREATHING OF VAPORS, MISTS/FUMES. KEEP IN A CLOSED, LABELED
    CONTAINER WITHIN A COOL (WELL SHADED), DRY-VENTILATED AREA.
Other Precautions:PROTECT FROM PHYSICAL DAMAGE. KEEP CONTAINERS CLOSED
    WHWHEN NOT IN USE. GROUND/BOND DRUMS. DON'T USE PRESSURE TO EMPTY
    DRUMS. EMPTY CONTAINERS MAY RETAIN PRODUCT RESIDUE INCLUDING
    FLAMMABLE/EXPLOSIVE  VAPORS. AVOID CONTACT W/HOT LIQUID/FUME.
