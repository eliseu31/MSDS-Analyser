=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN. SKIN: WASH
    THOROUGHLY W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. RESTORE
    USING APPROVED CPR TECHNIQUES IF BREATHING STOPS. INGESTION: DON'T
    IND UCE VOMITING. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:SCC
Flash Point:-156F
Lower Limits:12%
Extinguishing Media:CO2, DRY CHEMICAL, FOAM, OR OTHER CLASS B
    EXTINGUISHING MEDIA
Fire Fighting Procedures:WATER SPRAY MAY BE INEFFECTIVE. IF WATER IS
    USED, FOG NOZZLES ARE PREFERABLE. FULL PROTECTIVE CLOTHING & SCBA.
    USE WATER TO KEEP FIRE-EXPOSED CONTAINERS COOL.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:HEAT EXPOSED CONTAINERS MAY BUILD UP
    PRESSURE, AUTOIGNITE OR EXPLODE. FLAMMABLE LIQUID. VAPORS MAY
    IGNITE EXPLOSIVELY OR FLASHBACK FROM LONG DISTANCES.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:REMOVE ALL SOURCES OF IGNITION (FLAMES, HOT
    SURFACES & ELECTRICAL STATIC OR FRICTIONAL SPARKS). VENTILATE AREA.
    CONTAIN SPILLED MATERIAL & REMOVE W/INERT ABSORBENT USING
    NON-SPARKING TOOLS. STORE IN C LOSED CONTAINERS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T USE WELDING/CUTTING TORCH ON OR
    NEAR CONTAINER. DON'T BREATH VAPORS, SPRAY MIST, SANDING DUST. KEEP
    OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN. DON'T SMOKE.
Other Precautions:CONTENTS UNDER PRESSURE. EXPOSURE TO HEAT OR
    PROLONGED EXPOSURE TO SUN MAY CAUSE BURSTING. DON'T
    PUNCTURE/INCINERATE/STORE >120F/PLACE IN GARBAGE COMPACTORS OR
    REUSE EMPTY CONTAINERS. KEEP CONTAINER C LOSED WHEN NOT IN USE.
