=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:FOR SULFUR OXIDE FUMES DISCONNECT BATTERIES, EVACUATE &
    VENTILATE. EXTERNAL:FLUSH AREAS CONTAMINATED BY SULFURIC ACID
    ELECTROLYTE W/WATER. INTERNAL:DRNK LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER OR
    MILK, FOLLOWED BY  MILK OR MAGNESIA, BEATEN EGGS OR VEGETABLE OIL.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:SEE EXT MEDIA
Extinguishing Media:WATER, FOAM, DRY. DIRECT FLAME TO BATTERY CASE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:PER MSDS NOT APPLICABLE; HOWEVER USE
    APPROPRIATE PROTECTIVE CLOTHING/EQPMT AS CONDITIONS WARRANT.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:KEEP LIGHTED CIGARETTES/SPARKS/FLAMES
    AWAY.EXPLO CAN RESULT FRM IMPROPER CHARGING/IGNITION OF CHARGING
    GASES.EXPLO CAN RESULT IF CHARGED IN GAS TIGHT ENCLOSURES

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:AVOID CONTACT W/SULFURIC ACID ELECTROLYTE FROM
    BATTERY. FLUSH W/WATER. NEUTRALIZE WITH SOLUTION OF BAKING SODA &
    WATER.
Neutralizing Agent:SOLUTION OF BAKING SODA & WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:BATTERIES W/RELEASED ELECTROLYTE SHALL
    BE SEALED IN POLYEHTYLENE BAGS. KEEP BATTERIES AWAY FROM CHILDREN.
Other Precautions:DO NOT CRACK BATTERY CASES. DO NOT OVERCHARGE. DO NOT
    SHORT CIRCUIT BATTERY TERMINALS. KEEP LIGHTED CIGARETTES, SPARKS,
    FLAMES AWASY FROM CHARGING BATTERIES.
