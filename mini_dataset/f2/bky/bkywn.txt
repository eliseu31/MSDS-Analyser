=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:FOR DRY, UNRECONSTITUTED POWDER, USE FIRE
    EXTINGUISHER FOR DRY CHEMICAL.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS & FULL
    PROTECTIVE CLOTHING.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:PURE SODIUM AZIDE IS EXPLOSIVE WHEN

    HIGHLY EXPLOSIVE METAL AZIDES.

    AFFECTED AREA W/WATER. VENTILATED AFFECTED AREA.
