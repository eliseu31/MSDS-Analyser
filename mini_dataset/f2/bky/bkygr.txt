=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGESTION: INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE 2 GLASSES OF WATER & STICK
    FINGER DOWN THROAT. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCONSCIOUS
    PERSON. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRATION, MOU TH-TO-MOUTH OR GIVE OXYGEN. SKIN: FLUSHW/WATER
    THEN WASH W/SOAP/MILD DETERGENT & WATER. EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR 15
    MINS. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES. NOTE TO PHYSICIAN: SEE
    SUPP.
