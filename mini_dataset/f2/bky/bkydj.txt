======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE <100F. KEEP AWAY FROM HEAT,
    SPARKS, & OPEN FLAME. USE ONLY W/ADEQUATE VENTILATION. DON'T TAKE
    INTERNALLY. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.
Other Precautions:AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN & EYES & BREATHING VAPORS. KEEP
    CONTAINERS TIGHTLY CLOSED & UPRIGHT WHEN NOT IN USE. EMPTY
    CONTAINERS MAY CONTAIN HAZARDOUS RESIDUES.
