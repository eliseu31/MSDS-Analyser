=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER FOR AT LEAST 5 MINS. EYES: FLUSH
    W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. INGESTION: INDUCE VOMITING.
    INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE OXYGEN IF NEEDED. OBTAIN
    MEDICAL AT TENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:COC
Flash Point:415F
Extinguishing Media:CO2, DRY CHEMICAL, FOAM, WATER, OR FOG.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS &
    PROTECTIVE CLOTHING.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:CONTAINERS CONTAMINATED W/WATER MAY
    EXPLODE DUE TO PRESSURE BUILD-UP.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ABSORB IN ABSORBENT SWEEPING/CLEANING COMPOUND
    SUCH AS OIL DRY. REMOVE FROM AREA IN OPEN DRUMS & TREAT W/WATER
    CONTAINING 1% AMMONIA BEFORE DISPOSAL.
Neutralizing Agent:1% AMMONIA/WATER SOLUTION

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:PROTECT FROM WATER BY A DRY ATMOSPHERE
    & MAINTAIN AT 65-75F. VENT WATER CONTAMINATED CONTAINERS TO AVOID
    PRESSURE BUILD UP.
Other Precautions:AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN & EYES. MATERIAL HAS A SHELF
    LIFE OF 6 MONTHS, IF KEPT IN A CLOSED ORIGINAL CONTAINER & STORED
    PROPERLY.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:USE A CANISTER TYPE GAS MASK EQUIPPED FOR
    ORGANIC VAPORS, MASKS SUPPLIED FROM AN EXTERNAL AIR SOURCE, OR
    SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING EQUIPMENT.
