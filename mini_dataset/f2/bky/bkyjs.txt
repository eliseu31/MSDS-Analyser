=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. PROVIDE OXYGEN THERAPY OR
    RESUSCITATION. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. HIGH PRESSURE SKIN
    INJECTIONS ARE SERIOUS MEDICAL EMERGENCIES. EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR
    AT LEAST 15  MINS. INGESTION: RINSE MOUTH W/WATER. KEEP CALM &
    WARM. DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. AVOID ASPIRATION INTO THE LUNGS, WHICH
    MAY CAUSE CHEMICAL PNEUMONIA. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:>100F
Lower Limits:0.7%
Upper Limits:5%
Extinguishing Media:ANY APPROVED EXTINGUISHING AGENT FOR CLASS B FIRES
    SUCH AS DRY CHEMICAL, FOAM, CO2, OR HALON
Fire Fighting Procedures:WATER IS INEFFECTIVE, BUT WATER FOG IS USED
    FOR INTENSITY CONTROL/COOLING EXPOSED AREAS. WEAR SCBA IN POSITIVE
    PRESSURE IN ENCLOSED AREAS. APPROACH FROM UPWIND.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:VAPORS IN EMPTY PRODUCT
    CONTAINERS/VESSELS READILY FORM EXPLOSIVE MIXTURES IN AIR/MIGRATE
    TO DISTANT IGNITION SOURCE/FLASHBACK. RUNOFF TO SEWERS ARE A
    HAZARD.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:CONTAIN SPILLS. ABSORB FREE LIQUID W/ABSORBENT
    MATERIAL/PADS/SAND/EARTH. REMOVE IGNITION SOURCE/SAFELY STOP FLOW.
    AVOID ENTRANCE INTO ALL BODIES OF WATER. EVACUATE AREA/WEAR
    PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT. SPIL LS ARE SLIPPERY. OPEN SPILLS EMIT
    FLAMMABLE VAPOR.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN/EYES/INHALATION
    OF VAPOR/MIST. KEEP CONTAINERS TIGHTLY CLOSED. DON'T SMOKE. DON'T
    CUT/GRIND/DRILL/WELD/HEAT/PRESSURIZE.
Other Precautions:STORE IN COOL/DRY/ISOLATED/WELL-VENTILATED AREA.
    BOND/GROUND TRANSFER LINES TO PREVENT STATIC ELECTRICITY
    ACCUMULATION. EMPTY CONTAINERS CONTAIN TOXIC/FLAMMABLE/COMBUSTIBLE
    RESIDUE/VAPORS. DON'T REUSE  CONTAINER. DON'T WEAR CONTACT LENSES.
