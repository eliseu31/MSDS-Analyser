=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:NO
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:YES    IARC:YES	  OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:EYES/SKIN: IRRITATION. INHALATION:
    RESPIRATORY IRRITATION. MAY CAUSE DIZZINESS, NAUSEA, VOMITING, OR
    NARCOSIS. VAPOR EXPOSURE MAY RESULT IN IRRITATION/BURNING OF
    EYES/SKI	      N, HEADACHE, DIZZINE SS, THROAT IRRITATION, LOSS
    OF APPETITE, NAUSEA, & VOMITING.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:SEE INGREDIENTS.
Effects of Overexposure:SKIN: DEFATTING & DERMATITIS. INHALATION:
    NEURAL DYSFUNCTION, KIDNEY & LIVER DAMAGE.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:ALLERGIES, EYE, SKIN, & RESPIRATORY
    DISORDERS.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION,
    IF NOT BREATHING. EYES: FLUSH W/MUCH WATER. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP &
    WATER. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. IF VOMITING OCCURS
    SPONTANEOUSLY,  KEEP VICTIM'S HEAD BELOW HIS HIPS TO PREVENT
    BREATHING VOMITUS INTO LUNGS. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TOC
Flash Point:35F/2C
Lower Limits:1.4%
Upper Limits:17.4%
Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, CO2, FOAM
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE METHODS FOR FLAMMABLE LIQUIDS.  NOT
    MISCIBLE W/WATER, BUT MAY BE USED TO COOL FIRE EXPOSED CONTAINERS.
    WEAR SCBA & PROTECTIVE CLOTHING.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:FLAMMABLE LIQUID.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:REMOVE IGNITION SOURCES. WEAR PROTECTION.
    CONTAIN SPILL W/DIKES OF ABSORBENT MATERIAL & SOAK UP THE SPILL
    W/ABSORBENT MATERIAL. CLEAN UP THE ABSORBENT & CONTAIN SECURELY.
    SWEEP & WET MOP THE AFFECTED  AREA W/SOAP & WATER. VENTILATE AREA
    ADEQUATELY.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T SMOKE IN AREA. ONLY PROPERLY
    TRAINED & PROTECTED PERSONNEL SHOULD BE IN THE IMMEDIATE SPRAY
    AREA.
Other Precautions:DON'T WEAR CONTACT LENSES WHEN USING THIS PRODUCT AS
    CONTACT LENSES CONTRIBUTE TO THE SEVERITY OF AN EYE INJURY.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:IF CONDITIONS GENERATE VAPORS/MISTS, WEAR NIOSH
    APPROVED RESPIRATOR APPROPRIATE FOR THOSE EMISSION LEVELS AT POINT
    OF USE (FULL FACEPIECE/PURIFYING CARTRIDGE RESPIRATOR EQUIPPED FOR
    ORGANIC VAPORS/MIS TSD, SCBA IN PRESSURE DEMAND MODE,(SUPP)
Ventilation:SPRAY BOOTH FOR SPRAYING. EXHAUST: SUFFICIENT TO KEEP
    AIRBORNE CONCENTRATIONS LOW. DESIGNED TO DRAW AIR PAST (SEE SUPP)
Protective Gloves:SOLVENT RESISTANT
Eye Protection:SAFETY GOGGLES
Other Protective Equipment:SOLVENT RESISTANT APRON & BOOTS, EYEWASH &
    SAFETY SHOWER, & FULL PERMEATION RESISTANT EQUIPMENT.
Work Hygienic Practices:WASH AFTER HANDLING THE PRODUCT. REMOVE/LAUNDER
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE.
Supplemental Safety and Health
CON'T WASTE: WHEN FLOUROELASTOMER SOLUTION & CURING AGENT ARE MIXED AND
    SOLVENT IS ALLOWED TO EVAPORATE, THE SOLID PRODUCT IS A
    NON-HAZARDOUS LANDFILL ITEM. CON'T RESPIRATORY: A POSITIVE PRESSURE
    AIR  SUPPLIED RESPIRATOR. CON'T VENT: OPERATOR, THEN PAST THE
    SOURCE, AND THEN TO THE EXHAUST, NOT THE REVERSE.
