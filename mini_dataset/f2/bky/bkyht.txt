=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. RESTORE BREATHING. SKIN:
    WASH THOROUGHLY W/SOAP & WATER. EYES: FLUSH W/CLEAN LUKEWARM WATER
    FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. DON'T GIVE
    ANYTHING  BY MOUTH TO AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. VOMITING MAY CAUSE
    ASPIRATION RESULTING IN CHEMICAL PNEUMONITIS. OBTAIN MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
