=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:YES    IARC:YES	  OSHA:YES
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:INHALATION: ANESTHETIC. DEATH BY
    ASPHYXIATION IS POSSIBLE. SKIN: MAY CAUSE IRRITATION. PROLONGED
    EXPOSURE MAY CAUSE LIVER, KIDNEY & HEAT DAMAGE.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:METHYLENE CHLORIDE & ANTIMONY TRIOXIDE
    (ONLY BY ACGIH) ARE A2 SUSPECTED HUMAN CARCINOGENS.
Effects of Overexposure:ASPHYXIATION, NARCOSIS, IRRITATION

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE
    VOMITING. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN
    ALL CASES.
