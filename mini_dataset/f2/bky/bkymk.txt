=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING HAS STOPPED,
    GIVE CPR. IF CHEST TIGHTNESS, SHORTNESS OF BREATH, CHEST PAIN, OR
    COUGH PERSIST FOR MORE THAN 5 MINS, OBTAIN MED ATTN. EYES/SKIN:
    FLUSH W/PLE NTY OF WATER FOR 15 MINS. DON'T RUB. NOTE: WATER WILL
    PRODUCE A BURNING SENSATION ON SKIN, LASTING 5 MINS. INGESTION:
    GASTRIC LAVAGE. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:386F
Extinguishing Media:WATER, FOG, FOAM, OR CO2.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR FULL PROTECTIVE CLOTHING & SELF-CONTAINED
    BREATHING APPARATUS W/FULL FACEPIECE OPERATED IN PRESSURE-DEMAND OR
    OTHER POSITIVE PRESSURE MODE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:AUTOIGNITION TEMPERATURE: 1202-1292F.
    REMOVE & DECONTAMINATE CLOTHING IMMEDIATELY.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:USE EQUAL PARTS METHANOL & WATER W/18% SODIUM
    HYDROXIDE/COMMERCIAL LYE OR AN AQUEOUS SOLUTION OF 10%
    MONOETHANOLAMINE ANIONIC DETERGENT. ALLOW TO STAND FOR 10 MINS.
    ABSORB W/VERMICULITE/DIATOMACEOUS E ARTH/CLAY/FINE SAND &
    PLACE/LABEL HEAD DRUM.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:USE ONLY IN WELL-VENTILATED AREAS.
    AVOID CONTACT W/EYES/SKIN/CLOTHING. DON'T USE CHLORINE/OXYGEN
    BLEACH OR DETERGENTS CONTAINING BLEACH TO CLEAN UP CS
Other Precautions:MONOETHANOLAMINE & CAUSTIC SOLUTIONS CAN HAVE A
    HARMFUL EFFECT ON SURFACES SUCH AS PAINTS, SPECIFIC PLASTICS, &
    METALS, ESPECIALLY ALUMINUM. TEST SURFACES BEFORE USING
    DECONTAMINATING SOLUTIONS SO THA T PROPER SOLUTION BE READY FOR A
    SPILL.
