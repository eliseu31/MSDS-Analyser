=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES/SKIN: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES.
    INGESTION: IF PATIENT IS CONSCIOUS, GIVE LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER.
    DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
