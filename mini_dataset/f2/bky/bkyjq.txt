=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE CPR, MOUTH-TO-MOUTH OR
    GIVE OXYGEN IF NEEDED. EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST
    15 MINS. SKIN: FLUSH W/SOAP & WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS.
    INGESTION: GIVE  PLENTY OF WATER. DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. DON'T GIVE
    ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. IF UNCONSCIOUS OR IN
    CONVULSIONS. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES. NOTE TO
    PHYSICIAN: SEE SUPP

    REMOVED, WET VACUUM THE AREA. IF AR EA IS POROUS, REMOVE & PLACE IN
    CLOSED CONTAINER.
