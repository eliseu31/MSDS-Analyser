=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP &
    WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. ADMINISTER ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRATION IF NECESSARY. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. OBTAIN
    MEDICAL A TTENTION IN ALL CASES. NOTE TO PHYSICIAN: ADMINISTER
    ATROPINE SULFATE. DON'T GIVE MORPHINE.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:CC
Flash Point:122F
Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, CO2, FOAM, OR WATER SPRAY
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE SELF-CONTAINED AIR SUPPLY. PREVENT SPREAD
    OF CONTAMINATED RUNOFF. FIGHT FIRE FROM UPWIND POSITION. USE WATER
    SPRAY TO COOL FIRE-EXPOSED CONTAINERS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:COMBUSTIBLE LIQUID. KEEP CONTAINERS COOL
    TO AVOID EXPLOSIVE IGNITION.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ELIMINATE IGNITION SOURCE. WEAR PROTECTIVE
    CLOTHING. SOAK UP W/ABSORBENT MATERIAL/PLACE IN RECOVERY DRUM. DIKE
    AREA TO PREVENT RUNOFF INTO NEARBY WATERWAY. WASH CONTAMINATED AREA
    W/DETERGENT/WATER. CO LLECT RINSE FOR DISPOSAL. DON'T DRIVE OVER
    SPILL.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:PERIODICALLY INSPECT STORED MATERIALS.
    DON'T CONTAMINATE WATER SUPPLIES. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.
    DON'T APPLY DIRECTLY TO WATER.
Other Precautions:DON'T STORE NEAR FOOD/FEED. TOXIC TO FISH, BIRD &
    OTHER WILDLIFE. AVOID STORAGE AT HIGH TEMPERATURES. AVOID
    CONTAMINATION W/WATER, ACIDS & ALKALIES. USE IN WELL VENTILATED
    AREA. AVOID INHALATION OF VA PORS, CONTACT W/SKIN & EYES.
