=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR 15 MIN. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP &
    WATER. INGESTION/INHALATION: OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:WATER, CO2 & DRY CHEMICAL
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS & FULL
    PROTECTIVE CLOTHING.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SCOOP UP TABLETS & PLACE IN SUITABLE
    CONTAINER. IF TABLETS ARE CRUSHED, CLEAN UP LARGER PIECES, CLEAN
    REMAINING POWDER W/DAMP CLOTH TOWELS TO SUPPRESS DUST. CLEAN
    W/DETERGENT & WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE AT TEMPERATURES 77F & BELOW.
    AVOID CUTS FROM GLASS IF BOTTLES BREAK.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:USE NIOSH APPROVED DUST RESPIRATOR IF >TLV.
Ventilation:LOCAL EXHAUST TO MAINTAIN 77F
Hazardous Decomposition Products:TOXIC FUMES OF CHLORINE, OXIDES OF
    NITROGEN & PHOSPHORUS
Conditions to Avoid Polymerization:TEMPERATURES >120F.

====================  Disposal Considerations  ====================

Waste Disposal Methods:DISPOSAL BY INCINERATION IN COMPLIANCE W/LOCAL,
    STATE, & FEDERAL REGULATIONS. U058 (HAZARDOUS WASTE).
