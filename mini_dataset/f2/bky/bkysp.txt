=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: WASH W/COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR 15 MINS. SKIN: WASH
    AFFECTED AREA W/SOAP & WATER. INGESTION/INHALATION: OBTAIN MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:CO2, DRY CHEMICAL, WATER
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS & FULL
    PROTECTIVE CLOTHING.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SMALL SPILLS - WIPE LIQUIDS W/GAUZE PADS. WIPE
    SOLIDS W/WET ABSORBENT GAUZE. CLEAN USING DETERGENT & WATER. LARGE
    SPILLS - COVER LIQUID W/ABSORBENT SHEETS, SPILL-CONTROL PADS OR
    PILLOWS. COVER POWDER  W/DAMP CLOTHING OR TOWELS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:BIOLOGICAL SAFETY CABINET FOR
    PREPARATION, OR AS DETERMINED APPROPRIATE BY INDUSTRIAL HYGIENIST.
    SHIP & STORE UNDER REFRIGERATION.
Other Precautions:EATING, DRINKING, SMOKING, CHEWING GUM, APPLYING
    COSMETICS OR STORING FOOD NEAR PREPARATION AREA SHOULD BE
    FORBIDDEN. IF HEATED TO 87F, THE DRUG WILL APPEAR AS AN OIL FILM IN
    THE VIAL.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:NIOSH APPROVED DUST RESPIRATOR, W/HIGH
    EFFICIENCY DUST FILTER, OR POWDERED AIR-PURIFYING RESPIRATOR IF
    BIOLOGICAL SAFETY CABINET, OR SUITABLE VENTILATION ISN'T AVAILABLE.
Protective Gloves:DISPOSABLE SURGICAL LATEX
Eye Protection:SAFETY GLASSES, FACESHIELD, GOGGLES
Other Protective Equipment:PROTECTIVE DISPOSABLE GOWN OF LOW
    PERMEABILITY FABRIC W/CLOSE FRONT, LONG SLEEVES/ELASTIC CUFFS.
Work Hygienic Practices:WASH THOROUGHLY BEFORE EATING OR SMOKING.
Supplemental Safety and Health
NOTE: ETHYL ALCOHOL IS USED AS A DILUENT FOR THIS DRUG & IS PACKAGED IN
    A SEPARATE VIAL.
