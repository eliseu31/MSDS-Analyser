=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. SKIN:
    WASH THOROUGHLY W/SOAP & WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. INHALATION:
    REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE CPR OR OXYGEN IF NEEDED. INGESTION: DRINK
    2 OR 4 GL ASSES OF MILK/WATER. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES.
