=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGESTION: DILUTE STOMACH CONTENTS W/PLENTY OF WATER. EYES:
    FLUSH W/WATER. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
