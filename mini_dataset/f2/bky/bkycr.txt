=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING HAS STOPPED,
    ADMINISTER CPR. EYES: FLUSH W/CLEAR WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS.
    SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE 2
    GLASSES O F WATER. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES. NOTE TO
    PHYSICIAN: DON'T USE ADRENALIN OR ANY OTHER SIMILAR CARDIAC
    STIMULANTS TO TREAT EFFECTS OF OVEREXPOSURE FROM THIS PRODUCT.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:>116F
Extinguishing Media:WATER, FOAM, DRY CHEMICAL OR CO2.
Fire Fighting Procedures:FIRE FIGHTERS SHOULD WEAR SELF-CONTAINED
    POSITIVE-PRESSURE BREATHING APPARATUS.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:EVACUATE/VENTILATE AREA. PICK UP LIQUID ON
    SUITABLE ABSORBENT & STORE IN SEALED CONTAINERS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP AEROSOL CANS IN COOL PLACE OUT OF
    DIRECT SUNLIGHT. FOR INDUSTRIAL USE ONLY. DON'T USE IN CLOSED
    SPACE.
Other Precautions:KEEP THIS & ALL CHEMICALS OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.
    AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN & BREATHING VAPORS. VAPOR CONCENTRATION WILL
    BE HIGHEST ALONG FLOOR & IN LOW LYING AREAS.
