=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:INGESTION: CAN CAUSE BURNS TO MOUTH,
    ESOPHAGUS, AND STOMACH. EYE: GIVES IRRITATION AND POSSIBLE ALKALINE
    BURNS. SKIN: PROLONGED CONTACT CAN BE IRRITATING AND ALSO DRIES
    SKIN. INHALATION: CAN BE IRRITA TING TO UPPER RESPIRATORY TRACT AND
    CAUSE DISCOMFORT.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE:FLUSH W/WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES, INCLUDING UNDER
    EYELIDS. GET MED AID. SKIN: FLUSH WITH WATER & WASH WITH SOAP. GET
    MED AID, IF IRRITATION PERSISTS. INHALATION: REMOVE VICTIM TO FRESH
    AIR. G ET MED AID, IF IRRITATION PERSISTS. INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMIT. GIVE DILUTED VINEGAR OR CITRUS JUICE FOLLOWED BY LRG QTY
    MILK OR WATER TO DRINK.NOTHING BY MOUTH TO UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. GET
    MED AID.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Fire Fighting Procedures:FIRE FIGHTERS SHOULD USE SELF-CONTAINED
    BREATHING APPARATUS AND FULL PROTECTIVE CLOTHING. USE WATER SPRAY
    TO COOL FIRE EXPOSED CONTAINERS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:THIS MATERIAL IS CLASSIFIED NONFLAMMABLE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:NOTIFY SAFETY PERSONNEL OF LEAK/SPILL.CONTAIN
    SPILL.COLLECT LIQ W/INERT CLAY.SHOVEL UP DRY MAT'L.PLACE IN LABELED
    CLOSED CONTAINERS.NEUTRALIZE RESIDUE W/DILUTE ACID/SODIUM
    BICARBONATE.DON'T FLUSH TO S EWER,STREAM,WATERWAYS.FLUSH RESIDUE TO
    CHEM SEWERS
Neutralizing Agent:NEUTRALIZATION IS REQUIRED FOR ALKALINE MATERIALS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:WHEN MAKING SOLUTIONS ADD SLOWLY TO
    SURFACE OF WATER WHILE STIRRING. USE CAUTION WHEN ADDING ALKALINE
    MAT'LS TO HOT WATER.
Other Precautions:AVOID OUTDOOR STORAGE, HEAT, & SUNLIGHT. STORE IN
    CLOSED, PROPERLY LABELED CONTAINERS. PROTECT CONTAINERS FROM
    PHYSICAL DAMAGE. EMPTY CONTAINERS SHOULD BE WATER FLUSHED AND
    CLEANED (FREE OF RESIDUE) P RIOR TO DISCARD PROCEDURES.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:PROVIDE RESPIRATORY PROTECTION WHERE DUST OR
    MISTING CONDITIONS OCCUR.
Ventilation:WHERE DUSTY, MISTING OR VAPOR CONDITIONS EXIST PROVIDE
    LOCAL EXHAUST VENTILATION (10-100 FPM CAPTURE VELOCITY).
Protective Gloves:PROVIDE RUBBER GLOVES
Eye Protection:SAFETY GOGGLES & FULL FACE SHIELD
Other Protective Equipment:PROVIDE RUBBER APRONS. AVOID WEARING
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. LAUNDER CLOTHING PRIOR TO REUSE. EYEWASH
