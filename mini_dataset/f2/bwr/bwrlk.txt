=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:GET MEDICAL HELP IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST. INHALED:REMOVE TO FRESH
    AIR. PROVIDE CPR/OXYGEN IF NEEDED. EYES:FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15
    MINUTES, HOLDING EYELIDS OPEN. SKIN:WASH WITH SOAP & WATER.
    ORAL:FOR SMALL  QUANTITIES, DRINK WATER. FOR LARGE QUANTITIES,
    INDUCE VOMITING. GET IMMEDIATE MEDICAL ATTENTION.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

    SOURCES. VENTILATE AREA. PICK UP SPILL WITH NON-FLAMMABLE ABSORBENT
    SUCH AS SAND, EARTH. PREVENT LIQUID FROM ENTERING SEWERS. SMALL
    SPILL MAY BE FLUSHED T O SEWER WITH LARGE AMOUNT OF WATER IF
    PERMITTED.
Neutralizing Agent:NOT RELEVANT

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN COOL, VENTILATED AREA AWAY
    FROM SOURCES OF IGNITION. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.
Other Precautions:DO NOT GET IN EYES. DO NOT BREATHE VAPOR OR MIST.
    AVOID PROLONGED OR REPEATED SKIN CONTACT. WASH THOROUGHLY AFTER
    HANDLING AND BEFORE EATING OR DRINKING.
