=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING;WASH WITH SOAP AND WATER OR
    WITH A WATERLESS HAND CLEANER.EYES:FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15
    MINUTES.INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.GIVE OXYGEN OR ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRATION IF NEED ED.INGEST:GET PROMPT QUALIFIED MEDICAL
    ATTENTION.IF CONSCIOUS,GIVE MILK OR EGG WHITE,BEATEN WITH
    MILK,INDUCE VOMITING BY STICKING FINGER DOWN THROAT.CONTINUE UNTIL
    VOMITUS IS CLEAR.
