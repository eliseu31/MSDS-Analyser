First Aid:INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. SUPPORT BREATHING (GIVE O*2/ARTF
    RESP) . EYES:FLUSH IMMED W/PLENTY OF COOL RUNNING WATER. REMOVE

    COO L RUNNING WATER. WASH THORO W/SOAP & WATER. INGEST:RINSE MOUTH;
    THEN DRINK 1-2 LG GLASSES OF WATER. DO NOT INDUCE VOMIT. NEVER GIVE
    ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO UNCON PERSON. CALL POIS CTL CTR/PHYS IMMED.
