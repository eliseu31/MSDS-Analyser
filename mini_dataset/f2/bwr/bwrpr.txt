=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL:REMOVE FROM AREA TO FRESH AIR. SEEK MED ATTN IF RESP
    IRRIT DEVELOPS/IF BRTHG BECOMES DFCLT. EYE:IMMED RINSE W/WATER.
    REMOVE ANY CONT LENSES & CONTINUE FLUSHING W/RUNNING WATER FOR AT
    LST 15 MINS . HOLD EYELIDS APART TO ENSURE RINSING OF ENTIRE SURF
    OF EYES & LIDS W/WATER. GET IMMED MED ATTN. SKIN:WASH AFFECTED
    AREAS W/PLENTY OF SOAP & WATER FOR SEVERAL MINS. SEEK MED ATTN IF
    IRRIT (SUPP DATA)
