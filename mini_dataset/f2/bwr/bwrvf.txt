=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGEST:CALL MD IMMEDIATELY . EYES:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH W/COPIOUS
    AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. SKIN:IMMEDIATELY WASH
    W/SOAP & COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER. WASH CONTAMINATED CLOTHING
    BEFORE  REUSE. INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING GIVE
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN.
    CALL MD.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================
