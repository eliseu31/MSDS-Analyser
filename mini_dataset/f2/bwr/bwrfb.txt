=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES-FLUSH WITH LOTS OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IF SYMPTOMS OCCUR. SKIN-WASH WITH SOAP & WATER. GET
    MEDICAL ATTENTION IF SYMPTOMS OCCUR. INHALED-REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    GET MEDICAL AT TENTION IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST. INGESTED-IF CONSCIOUS,
    DRINK 1-2 GLASSES. SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION.
