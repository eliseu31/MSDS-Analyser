=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: IF INHALED, REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT
    BREATHING, GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION, PREFERABLY MOUTH TO MOUTH.
    IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN. CONTACT: IN CASE OF
    CONTACT, IMMEDIATELY  FLUSH EYES WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST
    15 MINUTES. CALL A PHYSICIAN.

    OCCURRED.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN COOL LOCATION (60-80F) AWAY
    FROM SUNLIGHT AND ANY SOURCE OF HEAT, SPARKS, FLAME. KEEP CONTAINER
    TIGHTLY CLOSED WHEN NOT BEING USED.
Other Precautions:USE WITH ADEQUATE VENTILATION, AVOID PROLONGED OR
    REPEATED BREATHING OF VAPORS. AVOID SKIN CONTACT, USE IN AN AREA
    FREE FROM FLAME AND SPARKS.
