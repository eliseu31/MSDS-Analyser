=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE AFFECTED PERSONS TO FRESH AIR AND TREAT
    SYMPTOMS. INGESTION: IF CONSCIOUS, GIVE WATER OR CITRUS JUICE TO
    DILUTE AND CONTACT PHYSICIAN. SKIN: WASH WITH SOAP AND WATER,
    REMOVE CONTAMI NATED CLOTHING AND WASH BEFORE REUSE. EYE: FLUSH
    WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES AND SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION.
