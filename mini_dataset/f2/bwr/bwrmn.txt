=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN-REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHES. WASH WITH SOAP & WATER. IF
    IRRITATION PERSISTS, SEE A DOCTOR. EYES-FLUSH WITH LOTS OF WATER
    FOR 15 MINUTES; LIFTING LIDS. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IF SYMPTOMS
    PERSISTS.
