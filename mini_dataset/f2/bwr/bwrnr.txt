=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:YES	 OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:MFR STATES THAT THE HEALTH HAZARDS ARE
    UNKNOWN FOR THIS PRODUCT. HMIS HAS FOUND IN THE LITERATURE(RTECS)
    THAT VINYLIDENE CHLORIDE IS VERY TOXIC VIA INHALATION AND INGESTION
    AND HAS MODERATE DERMAL ABS ORPTIVITY.IT MAY CAUSE LIVER DAMAGE
    AND,IT AND ISOBUTANE,CAN CAUSE CNS EFFECTS BY INHALATION.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:VINYLIDENE CHLORIDE IS LISTED BY IARC
    AND EPA AS HAVING LIMITED EVIDENCE OF CARCINOGENICITY.
Effects of Overexposure:INHAL:HMIS SUGGESTS HEADACHE,DIZZINESS.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:NOT REPORTED.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING;WASH WITH WATER.EYES:FLUSH
    WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES.INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.GIVE OXYGEN OR
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION IF NEEDED.INGEST:DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.GET
    PROMP T QUALIFIED MEDICAL ATTENTION.
