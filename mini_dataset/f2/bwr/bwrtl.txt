=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:FLUSH CONTAMINATED SKIN W/PLENTY OF WATER. CONSULT MD IF
    IRRIT DEVELOPS. EYES:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MINUTES, OCCASIONALLY LIFTING UPPER & LOWER LIDS. GET MED
    ATTN AT  ONCE. INHAL:MOVE EXPOSED PERSON TO FRESH AIR. IF
    IRRITATION PERSISTS, GET MED ATTN PROMPTLY. INGEST:DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMIT. IF VICTIM IS CONSCIOUS GIVE PLENTY OF WATER TO DRINK. GET
    MED ATTN AT ONCE.
