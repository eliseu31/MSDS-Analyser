=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGEST:WASH OUT MOUTH W/WATER PROVIDED PERS IS CONSCIOUS.
    CALL MD. SKIN:FLUSH W/COPIOUS AMTS OF WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINS.
    REMOVE CONTAM CLTHG & SHOES & CALL MD. INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    IF BRTHG BEC OMES DFCLT, CALL MD. EYES:FLUSH W/COPIOUS AMTS OF
    WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. ASSURE ADEQ FLUSHING BY SEPARATING
    EYELIDS W/FINGERS. CALL MD.
