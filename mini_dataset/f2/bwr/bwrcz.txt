    PROMPT MED ATTN. SKIN: WASH THORO W/SOAP & WATER. REMOVE & WASH
    CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE. INHAL: MOVE TO FRESH AIR. INGEST: DILUTE BY
    GIVING WA TER TO DRINK & CALL MD. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO
    AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. NOTE TO MD: IT MAY NOT BE ADVISABLE TO
    INDUCE VOMIT IF SEVERE DAMAGE TO DIGESTIVE TRACT HAS OCCURRED.
