    CONTAM CLTHG, THEN FLUSH SKIN W/WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINS. WASH
    CLTHG THORO &  SEPARATELY, BEFORE RE-USE. EYE:FLUSH W/WATER FOR AT
    LST 15 MINS, OCCAS LIFTING UPPER & LOWER EYELIDS. IF IRRITATION
