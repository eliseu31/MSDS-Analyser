=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. RESTORE BREATHING IF NEEDED.
    SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION. EYE: PROMPTLY FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15
    MINUTES WHILE HOLDING LIDS OPEN. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN: WASH
    WITH SOAP  AND WATER. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IF IRRITATION PERSISTS. INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. INJECTION: GET IMMEDIATE MEDICAL
    AID.
