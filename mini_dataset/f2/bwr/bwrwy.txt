=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: IMMED & CONTINUOUS IRRIGATION W/FLOWING WATER AT LEAST
    30 MINS. PROMPT MED AID ESSENTIAL. SKIN: IMMED FLUSH SKIN W/WATER
    AT LEAST 15 MINUTES, WHILE REMOVING CONTAM CLOTHING, PREFERABLY
    UNDER SAF ETY SHOWER. GET MED AID IMMED. AVOID PROLONGED/REPEATED
    CONTACT WITH SKIN. WASH THOROUGHLY AFTER HANDLING. INGEST: DO NOT
    INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE LARGE AMT WATER/MILK IF AVAILABLE & TRANSPORT
    TO **

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:OC
Flash Point:215F,102C
Extinguishing Media:USE WATER SPRAY, DRY CHEMICAL, "ALCOHOL" FOAM, OR
    CARBON DIOXIDE
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE WATER TO KEEP FIRE-EXPOSED CONTAINERS
    COOL. WATER SPRAY MAY BE USED TO FLUSH SPILLS AWAY FROM EXPOSURE &
    TO DILUTE SPILLS TO NONFLAMMABLE MIXTURES.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:CARE MUST BE TAKEN TO PREVENT IGNITION
    WHEN PROCESSING OR HANDLING AT TEMPERATURES ABOVE THE FLASH POINT.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================
