=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS OCCAS LIFTING
    UPPER/LOWER EYELIDS. SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMD CLTHG, THEN FLUSH W/WATER
    FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. WASH CLTHG THORO BEFORE REUSE. INHAL: MOVE TO
    FRESH AIR . INGEST: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. GET MED HELP IMMED.
    ONLY IF PATIENT IS CONSCIOUS, GIVE LGE QTYS OF WATER OR MILK.
