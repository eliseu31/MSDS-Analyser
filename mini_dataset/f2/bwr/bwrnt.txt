=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST. EYES:FLUSH WITH
    WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. HOLD EYELIDS OPEN. SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHING. IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER AND WASH WITH A
    NON-ALKALI NE (ACID) TYPE OF SKIN CLEANER. INHALATION:REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR. INGESTION:DRINK 1-2 GLASSES OF WATER. SEEK MEDICAL
    ATTENTION. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON.
