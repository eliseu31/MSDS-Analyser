=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES-WASH THOROUGHLY WITH WATER FOR 15 MIN. IF IRRITATION
    PERSISTS, OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN-FOR EXTERNAL USE ONLY. IN
    RARE CASES OF LOCAL SENSITIUVITY, DISCONTINUE USE OF PRODUCT.
    INHALATION-RE MOVE TO FRESH AIR AND CONTACT A PHYSICIAN.
    INGESTION-DRINK PLENTY OF WATER AND CONTACT A PHYSICIAN.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:PASSES OPEN AND CLOSED DRUM TEST FOR AEROSOLS.
Fire Fighting Procedures:IF PRESSURIZED CANS RUPTURE, TREAT AS AN
    ALCOHOL FIRE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:PRODUCT IS AN ALCOHOL-WATER MIXTURE IN AN
    AEROSOL CAN.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:PRODUCT IS AN AEROSOL IN A PRESSURIZED CAN.
    EXCESS QUANTITIES MAY BE FLUSHED WITH WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:THIS PRODUCT WILL NOT FREEZE. IT DOES
    NOT PERFORM PROPERLY IF USED OR RAPIDLY HEATED IMMEDIATELY AFTER
    REMOVAL FROM UNHEATED STORAGE.
Other Precautions:STORE AT AMBIENT ROOM TEMPERATURE FOLLOWING PROLONGED
    EXPOSURE TO COLD. DO NOT PUNCTURE, INCINERATE OR STORE ABOVE 100F.
    READ AND OBSERVE LABELED INSTRUCTIONS.
