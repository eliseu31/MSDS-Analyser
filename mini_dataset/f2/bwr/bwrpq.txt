=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE FROM AREA TO FRESH AIR. SEEK MED ATTN IF RESP
    IRRIT DEVELOPS OR IF BRTHG BECOMES DFCLT. EYE: RINSE WITH WATER.
    REMOVE ANY CONTACT LENSES AND CONTINUE FLUSHING WITH PLENTY OF
    WATER FOR AT  LEAST 15 MINS. SEEK MED ATTN IF IRRIT DEVELOPS. SKIN:
    WASH AFFECTED AREAS W/SOAP & WATER FOR SEVERAL MINS. SEEK MED ATTN
    IF IRRIT DEVELOPS. INGEST: GIVE 3-4 GLASSES OF WATER,BUT DO NOT
    INDUCE(SUPDAT)
