=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. GET IMMEDIATE MEDICAL
    ATTENTION. SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. WASH AFFECTED AREA
    WITH SOAP AND WATER. INHALATION: MOVE TO FARESH AIR OBTAIN MEDICAL
    ATTENT ION IF NEEDED. INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCEVOMTIING. GET
    IMMEDIATE MEDICAL ATTENTION. FOR INDUSTRIAL USE. KEEP AWAY FROM
    CHILDREN.
