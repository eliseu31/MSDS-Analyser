=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BRTHG BECOMES DFCLT, CALL MD.
    EYES:FLUSH W/COPIOUS AMTS OF WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINS. ASSURE ADEQ
    FLUSHING BY SEPARATING EYELIDS W/FINGERS. CALL MD. INGEST:WASH OUT
    MOUTH  W/WATER PROVIDED PERS IS CONSCIOUS. CALL MD. SKIN:FLUSH
    W/COPIOUS AMTS OF WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINS. REMOVE CONTAM CLTHG &
    SHOES. CALL MD.
