=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE & SKIN:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH EYS & SKIN WITH WATER FOR @
    LEAST 15 MINUTES. REMOVE CONTAMD CLOTHING. CALL PHYSICIAN.
    INGEST:DO NOT INDUCE VOMIT. GIVE 1-2 GLASSES OF WATER CALL PHYS
    IMMED. NEVER GIVE AN YTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCON PERSON.
    INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE ARTF RESP IF NECESSARY. CALL PHYS.
