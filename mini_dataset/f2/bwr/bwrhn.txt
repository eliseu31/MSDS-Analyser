=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: IF INHALED, REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING
    GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION, PREFERABLY MOUTH TO MOUTH. IF
    BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN. CALL A PHYSICIAN. CONTACT: IN
    CASE OF CONT ACT, IMMEDIATELY FLUSH EYES WITH PLENTYOF WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MINUTES. CALL A PHYSICIAN. IN CASE OF FIRE USE
    EXTINGUISHING AGENTS RECOMMENDED.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WARNING, EXTREMELY FLAMMABLE. ELIMINATE
    IGNITION SOURCES. SOAK UP LIQUID W/ABSORBENT MAT'L
    (SAND,EARTH,SWEEPING COMPOUND,OTHER ABSORBENT MAT'L). PACKAGE
    ABSORBENT MAT'L OR SOLID PRODUCT IN STEEL DRUMS  WHICH ARE IN GOOD
    CONDITION. CLEAN AREA OF SPILL.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN COOL LOCATION (60-80F)AWAY
    FROM SUNLIGHT AND ANY SOURCE OF HEAT,SPARKS,FLAME. KEEP CONTAINER
    TIGHTLY CLOSED WHEN NOT BEING USED.
Other Precautions:USE WITH ADEQUATE VENTILATION. AVOID PROLONGED OR
    REPEATED BREATHING OF VAPORS. AVOID SKIN CONTACT. USE IN AN AREA
    FREE FROM FLAMES AND SPARKS.
