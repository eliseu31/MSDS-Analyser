=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. CALL PHYSICIAN
    IF DISCOMFORT PERSISTS. SKIN: WASH WITH SOAP AND WATER. CALL
    PHYSICIAN IF DRYING PERSISTS. SKIN ABSORPTION: FLUSH WITH SOAP AND
    WATER. INH ALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF DISCOMFORT PERSISTS,
    CALL PHYSICIAN. INGESTION: CALL MD IMMEDIATELY .
