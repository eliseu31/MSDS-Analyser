    15 MINS, GET MED ATTN. SKIN: WASH EXPOSED AREAS WITH SOAP & WATER.
    INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, ADMIN O*2.
    IF BRTH G STOPS, GIVE ARTF RESP. GET MED ATTN. INGEST: DO NOT
    INDUCE VOMIT. GET MED ATTN IMMED. ASPIR OF MATL INTO LUNGS DUE TO
