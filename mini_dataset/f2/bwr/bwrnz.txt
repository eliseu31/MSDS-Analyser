=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. WASH FROM SKIN WITH SOAP
    AND WATER. LAUNDER CLOTHING PRIOR TO RE-USE. EYES:FLUSH WITH WATER
    FOR 15 MINUTES WHILE HOLDING EYELIDS OPEN. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    INHALAT ION:REMOVE VICTIM TO FRESH AIR. ADMINISTER OXYGEN IF
    NECESSARY. INGESTION:DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE LARGE DOSES OF
    WATER. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
