=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE IMMEDIATELY. CALL PHYSICIAN.
    IF BREATHING IS IRREGULAR OR STOPPED, START RESUSCITATION,
    ADMINISTER OXYGEN. SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. WASH SKIN.
    EYE: FLUSH WI TH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES/TILL IRRITATION
    STOPS. INGESTION: CALL PHYSICIAN, DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING OR GIVE
    ANYTHING BY MOUTH.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:>20F,>-6.6C
Upper Limits:0.7%
Extinguishing Media:CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, WATER FOG, DRY CHEMICAL. NFPA
    CLASS B FIRE-BURNING LIQUIDS-USE CLASS B FIRE EXTING/EXTING AGENT.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED SCBA AND FULL
    PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT . AVOID BREATHING VAPOR OR FUMES.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:KEEP CONTAINER TIGHTLY CLOSED. ISOLATE
    FROM HEAT, ELECTRICAL EQUIPMENT, SPARKS AND OPEN FLAME. CLOSED
    CONTAINERS MAY EXPLODE WHEN EXPOSED TO EXTREME HEAT.
