=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE & SKIN CONTACT:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH EYES W/WATER FOR 15 MIN.
    CALL PHYSICIAN. FLUSH SKIN W/PLENTY OF WATER.  INGESTED:INDUCE
    VOMITING BY STICKING FINGER DOWN THROAT. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY
    MOUTH TO AN  INCONSCIOUS PERSON. CALL PHYSICIAN. INHALED:REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR.
