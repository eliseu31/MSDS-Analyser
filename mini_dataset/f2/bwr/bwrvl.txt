======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DO NOT GET IN EYES, ON SKIN OR
    CLOTHING. DO NOT STORE NEAR COMBUSTIBLES. EMPTY CONTAINER MAY
    CONTAIN HAZARDOUS RESIDUES. KEEP CONTAINER CLOSED.
Other Precautions:KEEP AWAY FROM HEAT, SPARKS & FLAMES. DO NOT REUSE
    CONTAINER. STORE <90F (32C) TO MAINTAIN ACTIVE OXYGEN CONTENT.
