=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING;WASH WITH SOAP AND
    WATER.GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IF SYMPTOMS OCCUR.EYES:FLUSH WITH WATER
    FOR 15 MINUTES.GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.GIVE
    OXYGEN OR A RTIFICIAL RESPIRATION IF NEEDED.INGEST:DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING.GET PROMPT QUALIFIED MEDICAL ATTENTION.IF CONSCIOUS,GIVE
    1-2 GLASSES OF WATER.
