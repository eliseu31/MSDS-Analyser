=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR, RESTORE BREATHING. CONSULT
    PHYSICIAN. SKIN CONTACT: FLUSH WITH WATER. EYE CONTACT: FLUSH
    IMMEDIATELY WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER. CONSULT PHYSICIAN. FIRST
    AID: IF CONTACT,  IMMEDIATELY FLUSH EYES/SKIN WITH PLENTYOF WATER
    AT LEAST 15 MIN WHILE REMOVING CONTAMINATED CLOTHING/SHOES. GET
    IMMED MED AID. WASH CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE. DESTROY SHOES.
    INGEST:GET MEDICAL AID IMMED.
