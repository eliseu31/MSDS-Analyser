=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING;WASH WITH SOAP AND WATER.DO
    NOT ATTEMPT TO NEUTRALIZE.GET MEDICAL ASSISTANCE.EYES:REMOVE
    CONTACT LENSES.FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES.DO NOT ATTEMPT TO
    NEUTRALIZE.G ET MEDICAL ATTENTION.INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.GIVE
    OXYGEN OR ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION IF NEEDED.INGEST:DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING.GET PROMPT QUALIFIED MEDICAL ATTENTION.IF CONSCIOUS,GIVE
    WATER.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NONE
Extinguishing Media:WATER FOG,DRY CHEMICAL,CARBON DIOXIDE,FOAM.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE A SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS AND
    FULL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT.COOL FIRE EXPOSED CONTAINERS WITH WATER
    FOG.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:PEROXIDES AND THEIR DECOMPOSITION
    PRODUCTS ARE EXPLOSIVE.VAPORS ARE HEAVIER THAN AIR,CAN TRAVEL
    DISTANCES ALONG THE GROUND,AND FLASHBACK.DECOMP PRODS TOXIC.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:VENTILATE AREA.USE PROPER PROTECTIVE AND
    RESPIRATORY EQUIPMENT.SWEEP OR VACUUM;DO NOT CREATE DUST;PLACE IN A
    DRY CONTAINER FOR DISPOSAL.CLEAN SPILL AREA WITH WATER.
Neutralizing Agent:NOT REPORTED.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE BELOW 104F IN A
    DRY,WELL-VENTILATED PLACE.KEEP CONTAINER CLOSED WHEN NOT IN
    USE.KEEP AWAY FROM HEAT,SPARKS,FLAMES AND INCOMPATIBLE MATERIALS.
Other Precautions:GROUND CONTAINERS WHEN TRANSFERRING MATERIAL TO AVOID
    STATIC ELECTRICITY BUILDUP.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:WHERE ENVIRONMENTAL CONTROLS ARE LACKING OR IN
    ENCLOSED SPACES USE A NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED RESPIRATOR FOR ORGANIC
    VAPORS.

    MAINTAIN PEL/TLV.
