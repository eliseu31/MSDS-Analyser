    SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. FLUSH W/WATER. EYE:FLUSH W/WATER
    FOR 15 MIN. INHALED:NOT LIKELY TO OCCUR. INGESTED. FLUSH MOUTH OUT
    W/WAT ER IMMEDIATELY & GET IMMEDIATE MEDICAL ATTENTION.
