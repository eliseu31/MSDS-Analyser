=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. SUPPORT BREATHING (GIVE
    O*2/ARTF RESP) . INGEST:DRINK LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER. CONTACT A
    PHYSICIAN IMMEDIATELY. EYES:FLUSH IMMEDIATELY WITH PLENTY OF COOL
    WATER  FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. IF IRRITATIONPERSISTS, CONTACT A
    PHYSICIAN. SKIN:WASH PROMPTLY WITH PLENTY OF SOAP AND WATER. IF
    IRRITATION PERSISTS, CONTACT A PHYSICIAN.
