=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION- TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY, MOVE TO FRESH AIR.  EYES-
    WASH OUT IMMEDIATELY WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. GET
    MEDICAL ATTENTION.	SKIN- WASH WITH SOAP (NON-ALKALINE TYPE OF SKIN
    CLEANER) A ND WATER. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IF SYMPTOMS OCCUR.
    REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING.  INGESTION- INDUCE VOMITING AS
    DIRECTED BY MEDICAL PERSONNEL. CALL A PHYSICIAN OR POISON CONTROL
    CENTER IMMEDIATELY.

    CLEAN SURFACE THOROUGHLY TO REMOVE RESIDUAL CONTAMINATION.
