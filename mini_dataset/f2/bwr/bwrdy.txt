=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING;WASH WITH WATER.EYES:FLUSH
    WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES.INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.GIVE OXYGEN OR
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION IF NEEDED.INGEST:DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. GET
    PROM PT QUALIFIED MEDICAL ATTENTION.USE SUPPORTIVE CARE.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Autoignition Temp:Autoignition Temp Text:500F
Extinguishing Media:USE WATER FOG, CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, OR DRY
    CHEMICAL.
Fire Fighting Procedures:DO NOT FIGHT FIRE MANUALLY.WEAR FIRE FIGHTING
    PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT AND A FULL FACED SELF CONTAINED BREATHING
    APPARATUS. COOL FIRE EXPOSED CONTAINERS W/WATER FOG
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:EXPLOSIVE PRODUCT COMBUSTION OR HEAT OF
    FIRE MAY PRODUCE HAZARDOUS DECOMPOSITION PRODUCTS AND VAPORS.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:BROKEN UNITS ELIMINATE SOURCES OF
    IGNITION;EVACUATE AREA;SWEEP UP WITH A SOFT BRISTLE BRUSH INTO A
    CONDUCTIVE RUBBER PAN;PLACE IN CONDUCTIVE CONTAINER(USING PROPER
    GROUNDING); DESENSITIZE(SEE NEUTRALI ZER).CLEAN SPILL AREA.
Neutralizing Agent:5% FERRIC AMMOIUM NITRATE OR 10% SODIUM
    HYDOXIDE(BOTH AQUEOUS SOLNS - W/W).

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN APPROVED MAGAZINES;HANDLING
    MUST BE PERFORMED BY QUALIFIED PERSONNEL(IAW DOD4145.26M).KEEP
    PRODUCT COOL AND DRY.
Other Precautions:UNITS WILL ACTIVATE WHEN IMMERSED IN WATER OR MINERAL
