=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. IF IRRITATION PERSISTS,
    GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP AND WATER.
    IF IRRITATION PERSISTS, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. INHALATION: REMOVE
    VICTIM  TO FRESH AIR. IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST, GETMEDICAL ATTENTION.
    INGESTION: DRINK PLENTY OF WATER. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION
    IMMEDIATELY.

Flash Point:NOT TESTED
Lower Limits:NOT TESTED
Upper Limits:NOT TESTED
Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, HALON FIRE EXTINGUISHER, WATER SPRAY,
