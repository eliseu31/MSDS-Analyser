=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:GET MEDICAL HELP IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST. INHALED:REMOVE TO FRESH
    AIR. EYES:FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES, HOLDING EYELIDS OPEN.
    SKIN:WASH WITH SOAP & WATER. ORAL:DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. IF
    CONSCIOUS, D RINK 1-2 GLASSES OF WATER. SEEK MEDICALATTENTION.
    NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON.
