    WATER.EYES:FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES.GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION.INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.GIVE OXYGEN OR ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRATION IF NEEDED.INGEST:D O NOT INDUCE VOMITING.GET PROMPT

    CONTAINER FOR DISPOSAL;CLEAN SPILL AREA TO REMOVE

    OF WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================
