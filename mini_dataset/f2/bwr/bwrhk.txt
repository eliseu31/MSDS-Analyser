=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/LGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES.
    TAKE TO MD. SKIN: WASH AFFECTED AREAS W/SOAP & WATER. REMOVE
    CONTAMD CLTHG. IF IRRIT PERSISTS, CONSULT MD. INHAL: REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR. TREAT  SYMPTOMATICALLY. CONSULT MD. INGEST: DRINK 1 OR 2
    GLASSES OF WATER TO DILUTE. DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. CONSULT MD OR
    POISON CONTROL CENTER IMMEDIATELY. TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY.
