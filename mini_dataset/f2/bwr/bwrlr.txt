=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:65.0F,18.3C
Lower Limits:2.0
Upper Limits:12.7 @200F
Extinguishing Media:ALCOHOL OR UNIVERSAL FOAM APPLIED BY MFR'S
    RECOMMENDED TECHNIQUES FOR LARGE FIRE. CO2 OR DRY CHEMICAL FOR
    SMALL FIRE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE WATER SPRAY TO COOL FIRE EXPOSED
    CONTAINERS AND STRUCTURES AND TO DISPERSE VAPORS. WEAR SCBA &
    PROTECTIVE CLOTHING WHEN FIGHTING FIRE IN ENCLOSED AREA.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:VAPORS ARE HEAVIER THAN AIR AND MAY
    TRAVEL ALONG GROUND OR FLOOR, THEN 'FLASH BACK' FROM A DISTANT
    IGNITION SOURCE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:EXTINGUISH & DO NOT TURN ON ANY IGN SOURCES
    TIL AREA IS DETERMINED TO BE FREE FROM EXPLOSION/FIRE HAZARDS.
    COLLECT LG SPILLS FOR DISPOSAL. FLUSH SMALL SPILLS W/WATER.
Neutralizing Agent:NO INFORMATION GIVEN ON MSDS BY MFR.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:WARNING! FLAMMABLE! CAUSES EYE
    IRRITATION. KEEP AWAY FROM HEAT,SPARKS,& FLAME. AVOID CONTACT
    W/EYES. KEEP CONTAINER CLOSED. USE WITH ADEQUATE VENTILA.
Other Precautions:FOR INDUSTRIAL USE ONLY. TRANSFER HAZ:VAP MAY BE
    IGNITED BY STATIC SPARKS. USE PROPER BOND/GROUND DURING LIQ TRANSF
    PER DOC NFPA 77. THOROUGHLY EVALUATE PROD USAGE IN ELEVATED TEMP
    PROCESSES-REFER TO	TECH BULLETIN "IGN HAZ OF ORG CHEM VAP".
