=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST.
    EYES/SKIN:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. HOLD EYELIDS
    OPEN. INHALED:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. PROVIDE CPR/OXYGEN IF NECESSARY.
    ORAL:DO NOT INDUCE VO MITING EXCEPT ON ADVICE OF COMPETENT MEDICAL
    PERSONNEL. IF CONSCIOUS, DRINK PLENTY OF WATER OR MILK. NEVER GIVE
    ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================
