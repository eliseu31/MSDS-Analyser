=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:WASH EYES & SKIN W/COPIOUS QTYS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINS. CALL MD. SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMD CLTHG. WASH SKIN W/COPIOUS QTYS
    OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. CALL MD. INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    ADMI N APPRVD O*2 SUPPLY IF BRTHG IS DFCLT. ADMIN ARTF RESP/CPR IF
    BRTHG HAS CEASED. CALL MD. INGEST:GIVE LGE QTY OF WATER TO DILUTE.
    INDUCE VOMIT. CALL MD. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN
    UNCON(SUPDAT)

    UP IS COMPLETE.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN COOL, DRY AREA. STORE IN WELL
    VENT AREA. STORE AWAY FROM INCOMP MATLS. PROD IS HIGHLY
    HYGROSCOPIC. KEEP TIGHTLY CLSD.
Other Precautions:DO NOT ADD ANY OTHER MATL TO CNTNR. DO NOT WASH DOWN
    DRAIN. AVOID RAISING DUST. DO NOT GET IN EYES, ON SKIN/ON CLTHG.
    WASH WELL AFTER USE IN ACCORD/GOOD STOR & HNDLG PRACS. DO NOT ALLOW
    SMOKING & FOOD  COMSUMPTION WHILE HANDLING.
