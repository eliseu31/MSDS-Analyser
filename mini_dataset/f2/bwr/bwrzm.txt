First Aid:INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF RESP SYMPTOMS DEVELOP, SEEK MED
    ATTN AT ONCE. SKIN:WASH OFF QUICKLY W/PLENTY OF WATER, THEN SOAP &
    WATER. REMOVE & WASH CONTAMINATED CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE. EYE:FLUSH
    IMME D W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER, ESP UNDER LIDS, FOR AT LST 15

    IMMED.
