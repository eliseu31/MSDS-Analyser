=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL:MOVE PERS TO FRESH AIR. ADMIN OXYGEN IF PERSON
    EXPERIENCES DFCLTY IN BRTHG. INGEST:IMMED CONT LOCAL POISON CONTROL
    CENTER. SKIN:REMOVE CONTAM CLTHG, THEN FLUSH SKIN W/WATER FOR AT
    LST 15 MINS. W ASH CLTHG THORO & SEPARATELY BEFORE RE-USE.
    EYE:FLUSH W/WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINS, OCCAS LIFTING UPPER & LOWER
    EYELIDS. IF IRRIT CONTINUES, GET MED ATTN IMMED.
