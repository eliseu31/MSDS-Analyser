=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:NO  Ingestion:NO
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:YES    IARC:YES	  OSHA:YES
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:INHALATION OF DUST OR FUME FROM
    CADMIUM & ITS CMPDS MAY CAUSE IRRITATION OF THE NOSE &
    THROAT.INGESTION OF CADMIUM DUST MAY CAUSE GI TRACT IRRITATION.
    NICKEL & CERTAIN NICKEL CMPDS MAY CAUSE LOCAL DER MATITIS ON
    CONTACT.INHALATION MAY CAUSEUPPER RESPIRATORY IRRITATION.INGESTION
    MAY CAUSE GI TRACT IRRITATION.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:NICKEL & CADMIUM & THEIR COMPOUNDS ARE
    LISTED AS CARCINOGENS OR POTENTIAL CARCINOGENS.
Effects of Overexposure:HEALTH HAZARD CONT'D: CHRONIC: LONG TERM
    EXPOSURE MAY CAUSE EMPHYZEMA, KIDNEY DISFUNCTION, BONE LESIONS,
    LUNG CANCER. SYMPTOMS: INHALED-COUGHING, CHEST PAIN, SWEATING
    CHILLS, SHORTNESS OF BREATH, WEAK NESS. INGESTION-NAUSEA, VOMITING,
    DIARRHEA, ABDOMINAL CRAMPS. SKIN-RASH, ITCHING.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:RESPIRATORY SYSTEM DISORDERS,
    PROSTATE DISORDERS, LIVER & KIDNEY DISORDERS.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION-REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE. SEE PHYSICIAN. EYES-FLUSH
    WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. SEE PHYSICIAN IF SYMPTOMS DEVELOP.
    SKIN-FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. SEE DOCTOR IF SYMPTOMS
    DEVELOP. INGESTED- INDUCE VOMITING IF CONSCIOUS. SEE A PHYSICIAN.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, CARBON DIOXIDE, WATER.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS AND
    FULL PROTECTIVE GEAR IF INVOLVED IN FIRE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:NOT APPLICABLE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:CONTAIN THE SPILL. COLLECT THE SPILLED
    MATERIAL AND PLACE IN A PLASTIC-LINED CONTAINER. HEPA VACUUMING IS
    PREFERRED. FLUSH SPILL AREA WITH WATER.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP CONTAINER CLOSED. AVOID CONTACT
    WITH CLOTHING. AVOID GENERATING DUST.
Other Precautions:KEEP FOOD AND TOBACCO AWAY FROM WORK AREA. WEAR
    NISOH/MSHA APPROVED DUST MASKS OR RESPIRATORS; IMPERVIOUS GLOVES;
    SAFETY GLASSES OR GOGGLES.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:IF ENGINEERING CONTROLS FAIL OR NON-ROUTINE
    PROCEDURES OR AN EMERGENCY OCCURS; WEAR NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED
    RESPIRATOR WITH DUST CARTDIDGE OR SUPPLIED AIR RESPIRATOR OR SCBA,
    AS REQUIRED. USE IAW 29 CFR 1 910.134 & MANUFACTURER'S
    RECOMMENDATIONS
Ventilation:USE ADEQUATE MECHANICAL VENTILATION OR LOCAL EXHAUST TO
    MAINTAIN EXPOSURE BELOW TLV(S) OR PEL(S).
Protective Gloves:IMPERVIOUS-PVC.
Eye Protection:SAFETY GLASSES/CHEMICAL SPLASH GOGGLES.
Other Protective Equipment:DO NOT WEAR CONTAMINATED CLOTHES HOME.
    SHOWER & CHANGE OR WEAR COVERALLS.
Work Hygienic Practices:WASH HANDS AFTER HANDLING AND BEFORE EATING,
    DRINKING, OR SMOKING. LAUNDER CONTAMINATED CLOTHES BEFORE REUSE.
