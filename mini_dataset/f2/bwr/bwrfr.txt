=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGEST: CALL MD IMMEDIATELY . EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MINUTES WHILE LIFTING UPPER & LOWER EYELIDS. IF IRRITATION
    PERSISTS, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP A
    ND COOL WATER. INHAL: IF INHALED AND AFFECTED, REMOVE INDIVIDUAL TO
    FRESH AIR. IF IRRITATION PERSISTS, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
