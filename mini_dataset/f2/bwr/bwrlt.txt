=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:NO
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:YES    IARC:YES	  OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:TARGET ORGANS:EYES, SKIN, CNS, BRAIN.
    ACUTE- EXCESSIVE INHALATION IS HARMFUL/FATAL. MAY CAUSE CNS
    DEPRESSION. MAY CAUSE IRRITATION OF EYES, SKIN & RESPIRATORY TRACT.
    CAN BE ABSORBED THROUGH SKIN. HARM FUL IF SWALLOWED. CHRONIC-
    OVEREXPOSUREMAY CAUSE PERMANENT BRAIN & CNS DAMAGE. MAY CAUSE
    ADVERSE EFFECTS TO LIVER.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:CONTAINS CHROMIUM.
Effects of Overexposure:IRRITATION, HEADACHE, DIFFICULTY IN BREATHING,
    NAUSEA, DIZZINESS, LOSS OF COORDINATION
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:NONE GENERALLY RECOGNIZED.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:CALL A DOCTOR IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST. EYE:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH
    WATER FOR 15 MINUTES, HOLDING EYELIDS OPEN. SKIN:WASH WITH SOAP &
    WATER. INHALED:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. PROVIDE CPR/OXYGEN IF NEEDED.
    ORAL:I F CONSCIOUS, DRINK SEVERAL GLASSES OF WATER & INDUCE
    VOMITING. CALL A PHYSICIAN. NEVER GIVE FLUID OR INDUCE VOMITING IF
    PATIENT IS UNCONSCIOUS OR HAS CONVULSIONS. KEEP AIRWAY CLEAR WHILE
    VOMITING.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:FLAMMABL AEROSOL
Lower Limits:1.0
Upper Limits:6.0
Extinguishing Media:CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, SAND/DRY CHEMICAL. AVOID
    SPREADING ANY BURNING LIQUID WITH WATER SPRAY. WATER IS
    INEFFECTIVE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR FULL PROTECTIVE CLOTHING AND
    NIOSH-APPROVED SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS. COOL
    FIRE-EXPOSED CONTAINERS WITH WATER SPRAY.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:SPRAY CANS UNDER PRESSURE. EXTREMELY
    FLAMMABLE. EXPLOSION HAZARD EXISTS WHEN SPRAY CANS HEATED. DO NOT
    ENTER SPRAY CAN STORAGE AREA IF FIRE PRESENT.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:VENTILATE AREA. REMOVE IGNITION SOURCES. PLACE
    LEAKING CONTAINERS OUTDOORS, AWAY FROM SOURCES OF IGNITION. ALLOW
    PRESSURE TO DISSIPATE. ABSORB LIQUID WITH NON-FLAMMABLE ABSORBENT
    MATERIAL SUCH AS SAND  AND PLACE IN CONTAINER. KEEP AWAY
    FROMSEWERS.
Neutralizing Agent:NOT RELEVANT

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE SPRAY CANS IN COOL, WELL
    VENTILATED AREAS AWAY FROM RADIATORS, STOVES, HOT WATER, SPARKS,
    FLAMES, SUNLIGHT. DO NOT STORE ABOVE 120F.
Other Precautions:DANGER! EXTREMELY FLAMMABLE. VAPORS ACCUMULATE AND
    MAY IGNITE EXPLOSIVELY. CONTAINER UNDER PRESSURE. FOLLOW LABEL
    INSTRUCTIONS. AVOID EYE, SKIN CONTACT. DO NOT BREATHE VAPORS/MISTS.
    USE IN WELL VENTIL ATED AREA. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:IF TLV IS EXCEEDED, WEAR NIOSH-APPROVED ORGANIC
    VAPOR RESPIRATOR OR AIR-PURIFYING RESPIRATOR. IN EMERGENCY, WEAR A
    NIOSH-APPROVED POSITIVE-PRESSURE SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING
    APPARATUS.
Ventilation:MECHANICAL (GENERAL AND/OR LOCAL EXHAUST, EXPLOSION-PROOF)
    VENTILATION TO MAINTAIN EXPOSURE BELOW TLV(S).
Protective Gloves:NITRILE, VITON
Eye Protection:CHEMICAL SAFETY GOGGLES
Other Protective Equipment:EYE WASH STATION & SAFETY SHOWERS SHOULD BE
    AVAILABLE IN THE IMMEDIATE VICINITY OF ANY POTENTIAL EXPOSURE.
Work Hygienic Practices:OBSERVE GOOD INDUSTRIAL HYGIENE PRACTICES AND
