    ETC.), REMOVE FROM EXPOS & SEEK MED ATTN. SKIN: IF IRRIT/RASH
    OCCURS, THORO WASH AREA W/SOAP & WATER & ISOLATE FROM EXPOS. IF
    IRRIT/RASH PER SISTS, SEEK MED ATTN. EYES:IF IRRIT OCCURS, FLUSH
