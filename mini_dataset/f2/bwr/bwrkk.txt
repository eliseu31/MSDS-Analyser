=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES-FLUSH WITH LOTS OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. CONSULT MEDICAL
    PERSONNEL. SKIN-WASH OFF WITH FLOWING WATER OR SHOWER.
    INHALED-REMOVE TO FRESH AIR IF EFFECTS OCCUR. CONSULT A PHYSICIAN.
    INGESTION-IF CON SCIOUS, INDUCE VOMITING IMMEDIATELY AS DIRECTED BY
    MEDICAL PERSONNEL. DO NOT GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH IF UNCONSCIOUS.
    NOTE: IF BURN IS PRESENT, TREAT AS THERMAL BURN, AFTER
    DECONTAMINATION.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:ALWAYS USE COOL WATER (TEMPERATURE
    LESS THAN 80F/27C) WHEN DISSOLVING CALCIUM CHLORIDE. AVOID EYE AND
    PROLONGED SKIN CONTACT.
Other Precautions:HEAT DEVELOPED BY SOLUTIONS IS VERY HIGH DURING
    DISSOLVING AND MIXING. LEATHER CLOTHING AND SHOES WILL BE DAMAGED
    BY CALCIUM CHLORIDE.
