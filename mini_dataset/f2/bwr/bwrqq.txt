=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL:REMOVE PATIENT TO WELL-VENTILATED AREA, AWAY FROM
    EXPOSURE TO FUMES. SKIN:WASH AFFECTED AREAS THOROUGHLY W/SOAP &
    WATER. USE MOISTURIZING CREAMS FOR EXTREME DRYNESS. EYES:FLUSH
    THOROUGHLY W/WATE R FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. GET IMMEDIATE MEDICAL
    ATTENTION. INGEST:DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING, CALL MD.

    THOROUGHLY.
