=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH WITH TEPID WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES HOLDING
    EYELIDS APART, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN: WASH WITH SOAP AND
    WATER. IF IRRITATION OCCURS, GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. INHALATION:
    REMOVE FROM  EXPOSURE. SUPPORT BREATHING (GIVE ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRATION/OXYGEN) . INGESTION: CALL MD IMMEDIATELY .
