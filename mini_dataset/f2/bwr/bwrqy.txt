=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGEST:CALL MD IMMEDIATELY . EYES:IMMEDIATELY FLUSH W/POTABLE
    WATER FOR A MINIMUM OF 15 MINUTES, SEEK ASSISTANCE FROM MD .
    INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. SKIN:FLUSH AREAS OF CONTACT W/WATER. W
    IPE AWAY LIQUID ADHESIVE WHERE POSSIBLEW/RESIDUALS BEING REJECTED
    BY SOAKING IN WATER.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC

Extinguishing Media:FLUSH W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER OR DRY CHEMICAL
    EXTINGUISHER.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED SCBA & FULL PROTECTIVE
    EQUIPMENT .
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:NONE. COMBUSTIBLE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ABSORB W/PAPER TOWELS OR CLOTH. SWEEP INTO
    TRAY FOR LIQUID TO BE REACTED W/WATER FOR SOLID WASTE TREATMENT.
    AVOID OVEREXPOSURE TO FUMES W/LARGE SPILLS & POOR VENTILATION.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID MOISTURE, DIRECT UV-SUNLIGHT &
    DO NOT STORE >25C. KEEP CONTAINERS CLOSED TIGHTLY WHEN NOT IN USE.
    IDEAL STORAGE IS 5-10C.
Other Precautions:STORE IN A COOL, DRY PLACE FOR BEST PROPERTIES &
    SHELF LIFE. AVOID BREATHING VAPOR, CONTACT W/EYES & SKIN. DO NOT
    STORE AT <5C WHICH FREEZES PRODUCT TO USELESS STATE. PRODUCT NOT
    DAMAGED BY FREEZING.
