=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL:MOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF DIFFICULTY IN BREATHING,
    ADMINISTER OXYGEN & GET IMMEDIATE MEDICAL ATTENTION. INGEST:SEEK
    MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY. EYE:FLUSH W/FLOWING WATER FOR AT

    LAUNDER CONTAMINATED CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE.
