=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:IMMED FLUSH W/PLENTY OF COOL RUNNING WATER. REMOVE CONT
    LENSES. CONTINUE FLUSHING FOR AT LST 15 MINS, HOLDING EYELIDS APART
    TO ENSURE RINSING OF ENTIRE EYE. CALL MD IMMED. SKIN:IMMED FLUSH
    W/PLEN TY OF COOL RUNNING WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINS WHILE REMOVING
    CONTAM CLTHG & SHOES. WASH CLTHG BEFORE REUSE. INGEST:RINSE MOUTH
    AT ONCE; THEN DRINK 1-2 LG GLASSES OF WATER/MILK. DO NOT INDUCE
    (SUPDAT)
