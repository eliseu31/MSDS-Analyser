=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGEST:CALL MD IMMEDIATELY . EYES:FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MINUTES. SKIN:WASH THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP AND WATER. REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT
    BREATHI NG, GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION, PREFERABLY MOUTH-TO-MOUTH.
    IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN. CALL A PHYSICIAN.

    LINED OR GLASS CONTAINERS.

    PROC. AVOID SKIN CONT. AVOID BREATHING VAPS. DO NOT GET IN EYES.

    ONLY WITH ADEQUATE V ENTILATION.
