=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION:MOVE TO FRESH AIR. CONSULT PHYSICIAN IF SYMPTOMS
    PERSIST. INGEST:GIVE LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER THEN SEVERAL GLASSES
    OF MILK. INDUCE VOMITING.GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.
    EYES:FLUSH I MMEDIATELY WITH LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER FOR AT LEAST
    15 MINUTES, LIFTING EYELIDS OCCASIONALLY. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    SKIN:REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND WASH WITH PLENTY OF SOAP AND
    WATER.
