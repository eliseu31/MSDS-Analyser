=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION- IF SYMPTOMATIC, MOVE TO FRESH AIR.  EYES- WASH
    OUT IMMEDIATELY WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION.	SKIN- WASH WITH SOAP (NON-ALKALINE TYPE OF SKIN
    CLEANER) AND WATE R. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IF SYMPTOMS OCCUR. 
    INGESTION- DRINK 1-2 GLASSES OF WATER. SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:FLUSH TO SEWER WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER.
    MAY REQUIRE NEUTRALIZATION IF PH <3 OR >12. OTHERWISE, ABSORB SPILL
    WITH VERMICULITE OR OTHER INERT MATERIAL, THEN PLACE IN A CONTAINER
    FOR CHEMICAL WASTE.  CLEAN SURFACE THOROUGHLY TO REMOVE
    CONTAMINATION.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================
