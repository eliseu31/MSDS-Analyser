=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:WASH THORO W/SOAP & WATER. SEEK MED ATTN IF IRRIT
    PERSISTS. EYES:MOVE TO FRESH AIR & IRRIGATE W/WATER FOR AT LST 15
    MINS. IF IRRIT PERSISTS, SEEK MED ATTN. IF EYE IS STRUCK W/WIRE,
    SEEK MED ATTN.  INHAL:MOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BRTHG IS DFCLT, GIVE
    OXYGEN. IF NOT BRTHG, GIVE ARTF RESP. KEEP VICTIM QUIET & WARM.
    CALL POIS CTL CTR/MD IMMED. INGEST:DRINK WATER & CALL POIS CTL
    CTR/MD IMMED. (SUPDAT)
