======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP AWAY FROM HEAT, SPARKS AND FLAME.
    AVOID BREATHING VAPOR. AVOID PROLONGED/REPEATED SKIN CONTACT. AVOID
    CONTACT WITH EYES.
Other Precautions:USE WITH ADEQUATE VENTILATION. DO NOT ALLOW WATER OR
    OTHER LIQUIDS INTO CONTAINER. FOOD, BEVERAGE, TOBACCO PRODUCTS
    SHOULD NOT BE CARRIED OR STORED CONSUMED WHERE MATERIAL IS IN USE.
    STORE STREET CLOT H SEPARATELY FROM WORK CLOTHES/PPE.
