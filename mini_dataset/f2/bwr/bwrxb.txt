=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:USE RESPIRABLE FUME RESPIRATOR OR AIR SUPPLIED
    RESPIRATOR WHEN WELDING IN CONFINED SPACE OR WHERELOCAL EXHAUST OR
    VENTILATION DOES NOT KEEP EXPOSURE BELOW TLV.
Ventilation:USE VENTILATION, LOCAL EXHAUST OR BOTH TO KEEP FUMES &
    GASES BELOW THE TLV. TRAIN WELDER TO KEEP HIS HEAD OUT OF FUMES.
Protective Gloves:WELDER'S GLOVES
Eye Protection:HELMET OR FACE SHIELD W/FILTER LENS
Other Protective Equipment:SEE ANSI Z-49.1. WEAR PROTECTION NECESSARY
