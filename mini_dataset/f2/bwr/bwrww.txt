    PERSONNEL SHOULD BEGIN ARTIF RESP/CPR IMMED. CALL DR. EYE: FLUSH

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID HOT METAL SURFACES. USE IN WELL
    VENTILATED AREA. KEEP CONTAINER CLOSED WHEN NOT IN USE. KEEP AWAY
    FROM EXCESSIVE HEAT AND OPEN FLAME.
Other Precautions:CONTAINERS EVEN THOSE THAT HAVE BEEN EMPTIED WILL
    RETAIN PRODUCRT RESIDUE AND VAPORS. ALWAYS OBEY HAZARD WARNINGS AND
    HANDLE EMPTY CONTAINERS AS IF THEY WERE FULL.
