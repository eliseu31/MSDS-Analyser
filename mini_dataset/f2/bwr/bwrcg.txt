=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN:FLUSH W/COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER. CALL MD . EYE:FLUSH
    WITH WATER FOR @ LEAST 15 MINUTES. INGEST:DRINK GLASSFUL OF WATER &
    CALL PHYS. INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR IF BREATHING PROBLEMS DEVELOP.
     IF BREATHING PROBLEMS PERSIST, SEEK PROMPT MEDICAL ATTENTION.
