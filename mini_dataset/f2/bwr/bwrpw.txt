=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. DRINK WATER TO CLEAR THROAT
    AND BLOW NOSE TO EVACUATE DUST. SKIN: FLUSH WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF
    WATER . INGESTION: CALL MD IMMEDIATELY . EYES: FLUSH WITH L ARGE
    QUANTITIES OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. IF IRRITATION PERSISTS
    CONSULT PHYSICIAN.
