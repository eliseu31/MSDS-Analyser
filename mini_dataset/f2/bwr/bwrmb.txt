=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:FLUSH W/WATER FOR 15MINS. CALL PHYSICIAN. SKIN:RINSE
    W/WATER FOR 15MINS. REMOVE CONTAMIN CLOTHING. INGEST:DRINK MILK. DO
    NOT INDUCE VOMITING.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:CC
Flash Point:676F,358C
Extinguishing Media:CLASS ABC FIRE EXTINGUISHER, CO2, WATER.
Fire Fighting Procedures:COOL EXTERIOR OF BATTERY TO PREVENT
    RUPTURE.ACID MIST/VAP ARE CORROSIVE. PROTECIVE CLOTHING & RESP
    EQPMT ARE REQUIRED.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:HYDROGEN GAS IS PRODUCED WHILE CHARGING.
    VENTILATE CHARGING AREA & REMOVE OPEN FLAMES/SPARKS/OTHER IGNITION
    SOURCES THAT MAY CAUSE AN EXPLOSION.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:DILUTE SPILL CAUTIOUSLY W/5-6 VOLUMES OF
    WATER. NEUTRALIZE W/SODA ASH OR LIME. WHEN EXPOSURE LEVEL IS NOT
    KNONW WEAR NIOSH APPROVED POSITIVE PRESSURES SELF-CONTAINED
    RESPIRATOR.
Neutralizing Agent:SODA ASH OR LIME.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE AWAY FROM REACTIVE MATERIALS.
    KEEP AWAY FROM HEAT & FLAMES DURING & AFTER CHARGING.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:SULFURIC ACID MIST-HALF MASK W/DUST & ACID MIST
    FILTER.
Protective Gloves:RUBBER GLOVES
Eye Protection:GOGGLES OR FACE SHIELD.
Other Protective Equipment:WEAR SAFETY GLASSES & PROPER PROTECTIVE
    CLOTHING TO PREVENT ACID CONTACT W/EYES & SKIN.
Work Hygienic Practices:WASH HANDS AFTER HADNLING BATTERY & SULFURIC
    ACID.
