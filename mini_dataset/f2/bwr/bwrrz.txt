=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. SEVERE EXPOSURE MAY REQUIRE ARTF
    RESP. GET MED ATTN. SKIN: WASH SKIN THOROUGHLY W/SOAP & WATER. GET
    MED ATTN. EYE: FLUSH W/LGE VOLUME OF WATER WHILE HOLDING EYELIDS
    OPEN. G ET MED ATTN. INGEST: GIVE 1-2 LARGE GLASSES OF MILK OR
    WATER & INDUCE VOMITING. GET MED ATTN.
