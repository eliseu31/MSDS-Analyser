=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGEST: TAKE TO DOCTOR, IF ACCIDENTLY SWALLOWED, INDUCE
    VOMITING. SKIN: WASH OFF CONTACTED AREAS. EYES: FLUSH W/POTABLE
    WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. CALL MD. INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    SUPPOR T BREATHING(GIVE O*2/ARTF RESP).

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:WATER, CO*2.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED SCBA & FULL PROT
    EQUIP. KEEP DRUM COOL, DO NOT HIT W/DIRECT WATER STREAM, USE SPRAY.
    DUST MAY BE FIRE & EXPLOSIVE HAZARD.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:IF MIXED W/OXIDIZING MATERIALS IT MAY BE
    SENSITIVE TO HEAT & IMPACT. CAN BE IGNITED BY FLAME.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:PUT ON DUST MASK, SWEEP UP MATERIAL & PUT INTO
    CLOSED CONTAINER. WASH DOWN AREA WITH WATER TO SEWER.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.
