    RESPIRATION OR OXYGEN IF NEEDED. CALL A PHYSICIAN. INGESTION: DO
    NOT INDUCE VOMITING. CALL A PHYSICIAN. EYE: FLUSH EYES WITH WATER
    FOR 15 MINUTES. C ALL A PHYSICIAN. SKIN CONTACT: WASH SKIN WITH
    SOAP AND WATER. CALL A PHYSICIAN IF IRRITATION PERSISTS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================
