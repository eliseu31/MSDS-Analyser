First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE FROM EXPOS & CALL FOR MED AID. ADMIN
    RESPIRATION. IF NO DETECTABLE PULSE, BEGIN EXTERNAL HEART MASSAGE.
    EMPLOY FIRST AID TECHNIQUES RECOM BY AMERICAN RED CROSS. IN CASE OF
    ELEC SHOCK, TU RN OFF POWER PRIOR TO REMOVAL FROM EXPOS AREA &
    ADMIN AID. EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN. SKIN: REMOVE
    CONTAMD CLTHG. WASH SKIN W/SOAP & WATER. INGEST: DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMIT. OBTAIN MED ATTN.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:USE NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED OR EQUIVALENT FUME

    BELOW TLV.
