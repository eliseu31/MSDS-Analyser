=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: FLUSH W/COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER. SKIN: WASH W/PLENTY OF
    MILD SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. INGESTION: FOR
    SMALL AMOUNTS, GIVE MILK OR WATER TO DILUTE. FOR LARGE QUANTITIES,
    SEE  PHYSICIAN. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:121F
Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, CO2 OR HALON
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS. DON'T
    USE WATER.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:AVOID USE OF ANY IGNITION SOURCE NEAR SPILL.
    CONTAIN MATERIAL W/INERT MATERIAL OR ABSORB IN INERT MEDIA SUCH AS
    ACTIVATED CARBON OR KITTY LITTER. DON'T HOSE DOWN SPILLS. SMALL
    SPILLS MAY BE WIPED UP.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN
    GLASS/TIN/LINED/STAINLESS/STEEL/ EPOXY-LINED CONTAINERS TO PRESERVE
    QUALITY. DON'T STORE IN PLASTIC. STORE CONTAINERS AWAY FROM HEAT.
Other Precautions:PROTECT AGAINST PHYSICAL DAMAGE TO CONTAINERS. AVOID
    CONTACT W/SKIN/EYES & MUCOUS MEMBRANES.
