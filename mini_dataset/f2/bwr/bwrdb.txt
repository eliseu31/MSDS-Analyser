=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:IMMED FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR @ LEAST 15 MINS. GET
    MED ATTN. SKIN:IMMED WASH W/SOAP & PLENTY OF WATER. WASH CONTAMD
    CLTHG BEFORE RE-USE & DESTROY CONTAMD SHOES. INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH
    AIR. IF  NOT BRTHG, GIVE ARTF RESP, PREFERABLY MOUTH-TO-MOUTH. IF
    BRTHG IS DFCLT, GIVE OXYG. GET MED ATTN. INGEST:GET MED ATTN IMMED.
    INDUCE VOMIT ONLY UPON ADVICE OF PHYS.
