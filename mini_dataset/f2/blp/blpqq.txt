=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. RESTORE BREATHING. SKIN:
    WASH THOROUGHLY W/SOAP & WATER. EYES: FLUSH W/CLEAN LUKEWARM WATER
    (LOW PRESSURE) FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE
    VOMITING. DON' T GIVE ANYTHING TO AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. VOMITING
    MAY CAUSE ASPIRATION OF THE SOLVENT, RESULTING IN CHEMICAL
    PNEUMONITIS. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

    TOOLS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN & EYES. AVOID
    STORING NEAR HIGH TEMPERATURES/FIRE/OPEN FLAMES/SPARKS/OR OTHER
    IGNITION SOURCES. DON'T TAKE INTERNALLY.
Other Precautions:STORE IN WELL VENTILATED AREA. KEEP CONTAINERS
    TIGHTLY CLOSED/UPRIGHT TO PREVENT LEAKAGE. AVOID INHALATION OF
    VAPORS/SPRAY MISTS. DON'T HANDLE UNTIL SAFETY PRECAUTIONS HAVE BEEN
    READ & UNDERSTOOD. SMO KE IN SMOKING AREAS ONLY.
