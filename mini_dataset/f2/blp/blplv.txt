=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY W/SOAP & WATER. EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR
    30 MINS. INGESTION: TAKE LARGE QUANTITIES OF MILK OR WATER.
    INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:COC
Flash Point:>200F
Extinguishing Media:CO2, FOAM, WATER FOG W/LIGHT WATER ADDITIVES, OR
    DRY CHEMICAL.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WATER FROM SAFE DISTANCE, PREFERABLY W/FOG
    NOZZLE. IF DRY CHEMICAL IS USED THOROUGHLY WET AREA TO PREVENT
    REIGNITION.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:DRY CHEMICAL EXTINGUISHING AGENT MAY
    CATALYZE DECOMPOSITION. MEKP ADDED TO HOT SOLVENTS OR MONOMERS WILL
    CAUSE A VIOLENT REACTION.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:DIKE TO PREVENT RUNOFF FROM ENTERING
    DRAINS/SEWERS/STREAMS & TRANSFER INTO CONTAINER. SPILLED MATERIAL
    SHOULD BE SWEPT UP W/AN INERT/MOIST/DILUENT SUCH AS PERLITE,
    VERMICULITE, OR SAND, & PLACE IN A C LEAN POLYETHYLENE LINED
    DRUM/POLYETHYLENE DRUM.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID INHALATION OF VAPORS. AVOID
    SWALLOWING & CONTACT W/EYES & SKIN. KEEP IN ORIGINAL, CLOSED
    CONTAINER OUT OF DIRECT SUNLIGHT & AT TEMPS <80F.
Other Precautions:DON'T STORE OR USE NEAR FOOD OR DRINK. DON'T ADD MEKP
    TO HOT SOLVENTS/MONOMERS AS VIOLENT DECOMPOSITION/REACTION MAY
    RESULT. DON'T SPRAY RAW MEKP INTO CURING, RAW RESIN OR FLUES.
