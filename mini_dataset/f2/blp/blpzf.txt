=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY
    W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE OXYGEN IF
    NEEDED. INGESTION: OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:CC
Flash Point:250F
Extinguishing Media:CO2, WATER SPRAY, DRY CHEMICAL, OR CHEMICAL FOAM
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:KEEP CONTAINERS COOL TO PREVENT PRESSURE
    BUILD-UP & POSSIBLE RUPTURE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:CONTAIN SPILL. ABSORB ON LOOSE, ABSORBENT
    MATERIAL. PLACE IN OPEN CONTAINER. WASH AREA W/DETERGENT & WATER.
    WEAR PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE AT TEMPERATURES BETWEEN 60F &
    90F IN A DRY, COOL PLACE TO PROTECT QUALITY. KEEP CONTAINERS
    TIGHTLY CLOSED TO AVOID MOISTURE CONTAMINATION.
Other Precautions:SHELF LIFE IS 6 MONTHS FROM DATE OF MANUFACTURING IN
    SEALED DRUMS. AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN & INHALATION OF VAPORS. AVOID
    CONTACT W/ISOCYANATES UNLESS MIXED AT PROPER RATIO. ISOCYANATES
    ADDED TO CONTAINER S MAY CAUSE FOAMING.
