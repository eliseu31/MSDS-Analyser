=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR AT LEAST
    15 MINS. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE OXYGEN OR ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRATION, IF NEEDED. INGESTION: IF CONSCIOUS, GIVE PLENTY OF
    WATER TO  DRINK. DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN
    ALL CASES. REFER TO PHYSICIAN ABOUT SERIOUSNESS OF ANY INJURY.
