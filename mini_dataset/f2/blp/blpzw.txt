=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. SKIN:
    WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE CPR OR
    OXYGEN IF NEEDED. KEEP QUIET & WARM. INGESTION: DRINK PLENTY OF
    WATER. DON'T  INDUCE VOMITING. AVOID ASPIRATION OF MATERIAL INTO
    LUNGS. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. AVOID
    ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
