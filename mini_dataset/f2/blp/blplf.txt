=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE. RESTORE BREATHING. KEEP
    WARM & QUIET. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF
    WATER FOR 15 MIN. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
