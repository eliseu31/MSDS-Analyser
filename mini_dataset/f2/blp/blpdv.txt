Routes of Entry: Inhalation:NO	Skin:NO  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:RIFADIN (RIFAMPIN) IS A PRESCRIPTION
    DRUG USED IN TREATMENT OF PULMONARY TUBERCULOSIS AND ASYMPTOMATIC
    CARRIERS OF N. MENINGITIDIS. CONSULT 'PHYSICIAN'S DESK REFERENCE'
    FOR USEAGES AND WARNINGS.  CONT ACT WITH CAPSULES POSES NO PROBLEM.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:THE PRODUCT CONTAINS NO INGREDIENT THAT
    IS LISTED BY IARC, NTP OR OSHA AS A CARCINOGEN.
Effects of Overexposure:SYMPTOMS OF CONTACT WITH THE POWDER WITHIN A
    CAPSULE: EYE:IRRITATION, TEMPORARY CORNEAL DAMAGE,
    SKIN:DISCOLORATION.  INGESTED:G/I DISTURBANCES, FEVER, HEADACHE,
    FATIGUE, DIZZINESS,MENTAL CONFUSION,VIS UAL DISTURBANCES, ALLERGIC
    AND ASMATIC REACTIONS, BLOOD CHANGES, HEPATITIS, JAUNDCE, AND
    DISCOLORATION OF BODY FLUIDS.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:NONE GIVEN BY MANUFACTURER
    (SUPPLIER).

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE:FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MIN,SEE A PHYSICIAN.  SKIN:WASH
    WITH SOAP AND WATER IMMMEDIATELY.  INHALED/INGESTED:GET IMMEDIATE
    MEDICAL HELP.   IF ANY EFFECT IS NOTED, SEE A PHYSICIAN.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:USE WATER FOG, CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, OR DRY
    CHEMICAL.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR FIRE FIGHTING PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT AND A
    FULL FACED SELF CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS. EVACUATE AREA. COOL
    FIRE EXPOSED CONTAINERS WITH WATER SPRAY.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:NONE

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SWEEP UP POWDER OR CAPSULES AND PLACE IN
    DISPOSAL CONTAINER.  AVOID CAUSING DUST.  USING SKIN PROTECTION,
    WASH AREA WITH SOAP AND WATER.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE GIVEN BY MANUFACTURER (SUPPLIER).
