=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR 15 MINS. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER.
    INHALATION: MOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING STOPS, ADMINISTER
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INGESTION: IF
    CONSCIOUS, GI VE 2 GLASSES OF WATER. INDUCE VOMITING.OBTAIN MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ELIMINATE ALL IGNITION SOURCES. DIKE & PUMP
    LIQUID TO CONTAINERS. ABSORB REMAINING LIQUID W/SAND, CLAY, EARTH,
    ETC. SHOVEL INTO CONTAINERS & CLOSE.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP CLOSURE TIGHT & CONTAINER UPRIGHT
    TO PREVENT LEAKAGE. STORE DRUM OUT OF SUN, AWAY FROM HEAT, SPARKS
    OR OPEN FLAMES & IN WELL VENTILATED AREA.
Other Precautions:STORE BETWEEN 40-100F. AVOID INHALATION OF DUSTS &
    VAPORS. AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN & EYES. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.
    DON'T TAKE INTERNALLY.
