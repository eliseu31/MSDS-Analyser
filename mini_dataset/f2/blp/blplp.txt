=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. SKIN:
    WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE CPR OR
    OXYGEN IF NEEDED. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. AVOID
    ASPIRATION OF MATE RIAL INTO THE LUNGS. OBTAIN MED ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES. PHYSICIAN NOTE: NO SPECIFIC ANTIDOTE FOR OVEREXPOSURE
    EFFECTS. OVEREXPOSURE TREATMENT SHOULD BE DIRECTED AT SYMPTOMS
    CONTROL/CLINICAL CONDITION.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:87F
Lower Limits:1.1%
Upper Limits:7.1%
Extinguishing Media:FOAM, CO2 & DRY CHEMICAL
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE CLASS B APPROVED SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING
    APPARATUS. USE WATER TO COOL FIRE EXPOSED CONTAINERS AT A SAFE
    DISTANCE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:VAPORS ARE >AIR & TRAVEL ALONG GROUND TO
    AN IGNITION SOURCE & FLASH BACK. IF POLYMERIZATION OCCURS,
    EXPLOSIVE RUPTURING MAY OCCUR. FLAMMABLE LIQUID.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ELIMINATE ALL SOURCES OF IGNITION. VENTILATE
    AREA. ABSORB W/AN INERT MATERIAL. REMOVE & PLACE IN LABELED DRUM.
    FLUSH SPILL AREA W/DETERGENT & WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A CLOSED, PROPERLY LABELED
    CONTAINER IN A COOL VENTILATED AREA <86F. AVOID CONTAMINATION &
    CONTACT W/EYES & SKIN.
Other Precautions:DON'T SMOKE/EAT OR DRINK WHERE PRODUCT IS IN USE.
    AVOID BREATHING VAPORS, MISTS, FUMES & DUSTS CREATED BY SANDING,
    GRINDING, CHIPPING OF DIRED PRODUCT. GROUND METAL CONTAINERS TO
    AVOID STATIC CHARGE.  MIX IN PROPER RATIO W/MEKP.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:USE NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED AIR PURIFYING ORGANIC
    VAPOR FILTER RESPIRATOR IF >TLV.
Ventilation:MECHANICAL (GENERAL/LOCAL EXHAUST): TO KEEP 86F, FLAME, HOT SURFACES,
    SPARKS, STATIC, & OTHER SOURCES OF IGNITION.
Hazardous Decomposition Products:SMOKE, CO & CO2
Conditions to Avoid Polymerization:HEAT, PEROXIDES, OXIDIZING AGENTS &
