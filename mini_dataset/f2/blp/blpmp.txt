=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:NO  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:EYES: IRRITATION. INHALATION: NOSE &
    THROAT IRRITATION, METALLIC TASTE IN MOUTH, METAL FUME FEVER
    W/FLU-LIKE SYMPTOMS.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:NONE
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:SENSITIVITY, RESPIRATORY CONDITIONS
    SUCH AS EMPHYSEMA, LIGHT & SENSITIVE SKIN, LARGE AMOUNTS OF BODY
    HAIR, & SKIN DISORDERS

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING, BEGIN
    MOUTH-TO-MOUTH RESUSCITATION. EYES: FLUSH W/WATER TO REMOVE
    PARTICULATES. SKIN: BRUSH OFF EXCESS DUST. WASH HANDS W/MILD
    NON-ABRASIVE SOAP & WA TER. INGESTION: OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN
    ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:MATERIAL IN SOLID STATE DOESN'T PRESENT
    HAZARD. DUST HAZARDS EXIST UNDER CONDITIONS OF SMALL PARTICAL SIZE.
    DISPERSION IN AIR/IGNITION SOURCE CAUSES EXPLOSION.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:TREAT CHIPS OR DUST AS METAL ALLOYS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T WEAR CONTACT LENSES. DRY
    HONING/CUTTING/GRINDING/WELDING/BRAZING/SOLDERING/ABRADING/MELTING
    OF STONE PRODUCES DUST OR METAL FUMES.
Other Precautions:AVOID INHALATION & INGESTION. AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN &
    EYES.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:IF CONDITIONS OF USE SUCH AS DRY HONING,
    CUTTING, GRINDING, WELDING, BRAZING, OR SOLDERING OF STONE GIVE
    RISE TO DUST, USE NIOSH APPROVED RESPIRATOR RATED FOR METAL DUST TO
    KEEP BELOW TLV.
Ventilation:USE FORCED VENTILATION TO KEEP BELOW TLV IF CONDITIONS OF
    USE GIVE RISE TO METAL FUMES.
Protective Gloves:AS REQUIRED
Eye Protection:SAFETY GLASSES
Supplemental Safety and Health
WATER WILL CAUSE STONE HOLDER TO SLOWLY CORRODE.
