=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:CALL A DOCTOR IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST. EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH
    WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES, HOLDING EYELIDS OPEN. SKIN: WASH WITH
    SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. INGESTION: CALL A
    PHYSICIAN.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NONE
Extinguishing Media:USE WATER FOG, CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, OR DRY
    CHEMICAL FOR SURROUNDING FIRE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR FIRE FIGHTING PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT AND A
    FULL FACED SELF CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS. EVACUATE AREA. COOL
    FIRE EXPOSED CONTAINERS WITH WATER SPRAY.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:NONE

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:CLEAN BY VACUUM OR BROOM SWEEPING AND REMOVE
    TO DISPOSAL CONTAINER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORAGE- STORE IN CLOSED CONTAINER.
Other Precautions:WASH EMPTY CONTAINER, RINSE WITH WATER AND DISPOSE OF
    IN TRASH. WASH THOROUGHLY AFTER HANDLING. AVOID CONTACT WITH EYES,
    SKIN AND CLOTHING.
