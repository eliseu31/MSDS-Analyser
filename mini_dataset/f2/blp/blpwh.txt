=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER.
    INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING.
    OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NON-FLAMMABLE
Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, CO2, WATER SPRAY, FOAM, FOG & ALCOHOL
    FOAM
Fire Fighting Procedures:MOVE CONTAINERS FROM FIRE AREA. DON'T SCATTER
    MATERIAL & FIGHT FIRE FROM A MAXIMUM SAFE DISTANCE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:NEGLIGIBLE FIRE HAZARD. CONTAINERS MAY
    EXPLODE IN HEAT OR FIRE. MAY BURN, BUT DOESN'T IGNITE READILY.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:DON'T TOUCH MATERIAL. USE WATER SPRAY TO
    REDUCE VAPORS. PICK-UP W/SAND OR OTHER ABSORBENT MATERIAL W/CLEAN
    SHOVEL & PLACE IN CLEAN DRY CONTAINER FOR LATER DISPOSAL. DIKE
    LARGER SPILLS FOR LATER DISPOS AL. EVACUATE AREA & ISOLATE.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID BREATHING DUST & CONTACT W/SKIN.
    KEEP AWAY FROM FOOD.
