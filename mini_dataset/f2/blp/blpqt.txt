=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT,
    RESTORE USING APPROVED CPR TECHNIQUES. EYES: FLUSH W/LARGE AMOUNTS

    WATER. INGE STION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. ASPIRATION OF VOMITUS CAN

    ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
