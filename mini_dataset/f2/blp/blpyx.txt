=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. SKIN:
    FLUSH W/WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. FLOOD BODY UNDER A SHOWER.
    INGESTION: IF UNCONSCIOUS, HAVING TROUBLE BREATHING OR IN
    CONVULSIONS, DON'T IN DUCE VOMITING, OR GIVE WATER. INHALATION:
    REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES. SEE
    SUPPLEMENTAL.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================
