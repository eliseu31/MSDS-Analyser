=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE OXYGEN IF NEEDED. EYES:
    FLUSH W/WATER FOR 15 MINS. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY W/SOAP & WATER.
    INGESTION: OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:PMCC
Flash Point:390F
Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, FOAM, OR CO2
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS W/FULL
    FACE PIECE & PROTECTIVE CLOTHING.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:GENERATION OF CO2 DUE TO MOISTURE
    CONTAMINATION MAY RUPTURE CONTAINER. PRODUCT IS A SUDDEN PRESSURE
    RELEASE HAZARD.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:EVACUATE/VENTILATE AREA/USE PROTECTIVE
    EQUIPMENT. CONTAIN/COVER/ABSORB W/SAWDUST/VERMICULITE. SHOVEL INTO
    SUITABLE UNSEALED CONTAINER. TREAT W/NEUTRALIZING SOLUTION: 90%
    WATER/8% CONC. AMMONIA/2% LIQU ID DETERGENT. ALLOW TO STAND
    UNCOVERED FOR 48 HRS.
Neutralizing Agent:SOLUTION OF 90% WATER, 8% CONCENTRATED AMMONIA, & 2%
    LIQUID DETERGENT

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T RESEAL CONTAINERS IF THERE IS
    ANY DOUBT ABOUT PRESENCE OF MOISTURE. AVOID MOISTURE CONTAMINATION.
    DON'T STORE DRUMS UNCOVERED OUTSIDE.
Other Precautions:STORE AT TEMPERATURES BETWEEN 50F & 100F. AVERAGE
    SHELF LIFE IN SEALED CONTAINER IS 6 MONTHS FROM DATE OF
    MANUFACTURING. USE W/ADEQUATE VENTILATION.
