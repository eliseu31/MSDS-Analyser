=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS.
    SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. RESTORE
    BREATHING. TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY. INGESTION: DRINK 1 OR 2 GLASSES
    OF WATE R TO DILUTE. DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY.
    OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
