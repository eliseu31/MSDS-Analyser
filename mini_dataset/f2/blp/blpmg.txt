=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR
    15 MINUTES. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF UNCONSCIOUS,
    ADMINISTER ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION/OXYGEN AS NEEDED. INGESTION:
    DON'T INDUCE VO MITING. ASPIRATION HAZARD. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION
    IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:89F
Lower Limits:1.1%
Extinguishing Media:WATER SPRAY, FOAM, DRY CHEMICAL, CO2, OR ANY CLASS
    B EXTINGUISHING AGENT
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:IF POLYMERIZATION TAKES PLACE IN A CLOSED
    CONTAINER, VIOLENT RUPTURE OF CONTAINER IS POSSIBLE. PRODUCT VAPOR
    MAY FORM EXPLOSIVE MIXTURES IN AIR. FLAMMABLE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:REMOVE ALL SOURCES OF IGNITION. VENTILATE
    AREA. ABSORB SPILL W/ABSORBENT MATERIAL SUCH AS SAWDUST,
    VERMICULITE OR SAND & PLACE IN CONTAINER. DIKE THE AREA TO PREVENT
    MATERIAL FROM ENTERING WATER SYSTE MS OR SEWERS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T STORE >100F. AVOID SKIN CONTACT
    & INHALATION OF VAPORS OR SPRAY MISTS. FIREFIGHTING EQUIPMENT
    SHOULD BE DECONTAMINATED AFTER USE.
Other Precautions:AVOID IMPROPER ADDITION OF PROMOTER/CATALYST. A
    PROMOTER/CATALYST USED W/PRODUCT SHOULD ALWAYS BE MIXED SEPARATELY
    & NEVER MIXED TOGETHER.
