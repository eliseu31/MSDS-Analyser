=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. SKIN:
    WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE CPR OR
    OXYGEN IF NEEDED. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. ASPIRATION OF
    MATERIAL I NTO THE LUNGS MAY CAUSE CHEMICAL PNEUMONITIS. OBTAIN
    MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TOC
Flash Point:125F
Lower Limits:1%
Upper Limits:6%
Extinguishing Media:FOAM, CO & DRY CHEMICAL
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS W/FULL
    FACEPIECE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:VAPORS ARE >AIR & MAY TRAVEL ALONG THE
    GROUND TO IGNITION SOURCES. DON'T CUT DRUM W/A TORCH AS AN
    EXPLOSION MAY RESULT.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:EVACUATE AREA. ELIMINATE IGNITION SOURCES.
    WEAR PROTECTIVE CLOTHING. ABSORB SPILL ON PAPER, VERMICULITE OR
    OTHER ABSORBENT MATERIAL. DIKE AREA TO PREVENT SPREADING. PUMP
    LIQUID TO SALVAGE DRUMS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A DRY, COOL AREA AWAY FROM
    HEAT & IGNITION SOURCES. KEEP CONTAINER CLOSED WHEN NOT IN USE.
    KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.
