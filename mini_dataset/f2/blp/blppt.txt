=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES/SKIN: FLUSH W/WATER FOR 15 MINS. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE
    VOMITING. GIVE LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER. GIVE AT LEAST 1 OUNCE OF
    MILK OF MAGNESIA IN AN EQUAL AMOUNT OF WATER, OR GIVE THE WHITES OF
    3 E GGS. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO UNCONSCIOUS PERSON.
    INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION IF
    NECESSARY. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:>122F
Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, ALCOHOL FOAM, OR CO2

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:COVER THE CONTAMINATED SURFACE W/SODIUM
    BICARBONATE OR A SODA ASH-SLAKED LIME MIXTURE (50-50). MIX & ADD
    WATER IF NECESSARY TO FORM A SLURRY. SCOOP UP SLURRY & WASH THE
    SITE W/SODA ASH SOLUTION.
Neutralizing Agent:SODIUM BICARBONATE OR SODA ASH-SLAKED LIME MIXTURE

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

    CLOTHING. DON'T BREATHE CHEMICALS. KEEP AWAY FROM OXIDIZERS. KEEP
