=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:NO  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:YES	 OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:SKIN/EYES: IRRITATION. INHALATION:
    IRRITATION OF THE RESPIRATORY TRACT, HEADACHE, NAUSEA, DIZZINESS &
    SILICOSIS.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:CRYSTALLINE SILICA IS CONSIDERED TO BE A
    2A HUMAN CARCINOGEN.
Effects of Overexposure:INHALATION: SILICOSIS, IRRITATION OF THE
    RESPIRATORY TRACT, HEADACHE, NAUSEA, DIZZINESS. SKIN/EYES:
    IRRITATION.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS.
    SKIN: REMOVE W/SOAP & WATER. INGESTION: DRINK 1 OR 2 GLASSES OF
    WATER TO DILUTE. DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY.
    INHALTION: REM OVE TO FRESH AIR. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NONE
Extinguishing Media:CO2, DRY CHEMICAL, FOAM, OR NFPA APPROVED METHOD
    FOR TREATING CLASS B FIRE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE FULL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT & SCBA. WATER
    SPRAY MAY BE INEFFECTIVE. IF WATER IS USED, FOG NOZZLES ARE
    PREFERABLE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:WATER MAY BE USED TO COOL CLOSED
    CONTAINERS TO PREVENT PRESSURE BUILD-UP, POSSIBLE AUTOIGNITION, &
    EXPLOSION DUE TO EXTREME HEAT.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WASH W/WATER. CONTAIN SPILLED MATERIAL &
    REMOVE W/INERT ABSORBENT. STORE IN CLOSED CONTAINER UNTIL PROPERLY
    DISPOSED.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE <110F & KEEP FROM FREEZING. KEEP
    CONTAINER CLOSED WHEN NOT IN USE. DON'T REUSE EMPTY CONTAINERS.
    KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.
Other Precautions:DON'T GET IN EYES. DON'T BREATH VAPORS/SPARY MIST OR
    SANDING DUST. USE ONLY W/ADEQUATE VENTILATION.
