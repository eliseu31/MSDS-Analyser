=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. WASH EXPOSED AREA WITH A
    LARGE QUANTITY OF SOAP SOLUTION OR WATER FOR 15 MIN.  EYES:
    IMMEDIATELY FLUSH EYES WITH WATER FOR 15 MIN. CONTACT A PHYSICIAN. 
    INHALATION:  REMOVE VICTIM FROM CONTAMINATED AREA TO FRESH AIR.
    APPLY APPROPRIATE FIRST AID.  INGESTION: DILUTE CONTENTS OF STOMACH
    IF PERSON IS CONSCIOUS. INDUCE VOMITING BY ONE OF THE STANDARD
    MATHODS.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================
