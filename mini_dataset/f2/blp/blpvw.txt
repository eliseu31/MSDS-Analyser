=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. SKIN: WIPE EXCESS
    FROM SKIN & FLUSH W/WATER USING SOAP IF AVAILABLE. INHALATION:
    REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY. INGESTION: OBTAIN
    MEDICAL ATTENTI ON IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:>212F
Extinguishing Media:WATER FOG, FOAM, DRY CHEMICAL, OR CO2
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:PRODUCT AS SUPPLIED WILL SPLATTER.
    PRODUCT WHEN DRY MAY DECOMPOSE & IGNITE >400F.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SOAK UP RESIDUE W/AN ABSORBENT SUCH AS CLAY,
    SAND, OR OTHER SUITABLE MATERIAL. PLACE IN NON-LEAKING CONTAINERS
    FOR PROPER DISPOSAL. FLUSH AREA W/WATER TO REMOVE TRACE RESIDUE.
    SEAL CONTAINERS TIGHTLY  FOR PROPER DISPOSAL.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID PROLONGED INHALATION FROM
    SANDING OPERATION OR SPRAY FROM AIR BRUSH. AVOID CONTACT W/EYES.
