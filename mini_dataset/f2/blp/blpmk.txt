=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:YES    IARC:NO	 OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:EYES: IRRITATION & TEARING. SKIN:
    IRRITATION, BURNING SENSATION, REDNESS, & ROUGHNESS. INGESTION: GI
    TRACT IRRITATION, VOMITING, CNS DEPRESSION, LIGHT-HEADEDNESS,
    HARMFUL, UNCONSCIOUSNESS, & DEATH. IN HALATION: ANESTHETIC &
    NARCOTIC EFFECTS, DIZZINESS, DRUNKENESS, UNCONSCIOUSNESS, &
    IRREGULAR HEARTBEATS.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:METHYLENE CHLORIDE IS AN A2 CARCINOGEN
    BY ACGIH.
Effects of Overexposure:EYES: PAIN & INFLAMMATION. SKIN: DRYNESS,
    ABSORPTION, & DERMATITIS. INGESTION: CENTRAL NERVOUS SYSTEM
    DEPRESSION, ASPIRATION OF LIQUID INTO LUNGS MAY CAUSE CHEMICAL
    PNEUMONIA, SYSTEMIC EFFECTS, & HARM FUL. INHALATION: CENTRAL
    NERVOUS SYSTEM, LIVER, & KIDNEY EFFECTS, CARBOXYHEMOGLOBINEMIA, OR
    DEATH.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR 15 MIN. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER.
    APPLY SOOTHING OINTMENT. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF
    BREATHING STOPS, RESUSCITATE/ADMINISTER OXYGEN. INGESTION: DON'T
    INDUCE VOMITING . GIVE PLENTY OF WATER. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY
    MOUTH IF UNCONSCIOUS. NOTE TO PHYSICIAN: ATTENDING PHYSICIAN SHOULD
    DECIDE TO INDUCE VOMITING OR NOT. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES.  (SEE SUPP.)

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:150F
Extinguishing Media:WATER FOG, CO2, OR DRY CHEMICAL
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS & FULL
    IMPERVIOUS PROTECTIVE CLOTHING.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SOAK UP W/ABSORBENT MATERIAL. EVACUATE THE
    AREA & LET EVAPORATE. KEEP OUT OF WATER SUPPLY.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T CUT OR WELD ON OR NEAR DRUMS
    FULL OR EMPTY. AVOID CONTACT W/EYES/SKIN/CLOTHING. DON'T TAKE
    INTERNALLY. DON'T BREATHE VAPORS & SPRAY MIST.
Other Precautions:USE W/ADEQUATE VENTILATION. PERSONNEL HANDLING THIS
    PRODUCT SHOULD BE ACQUAINTED W/ITS CHARACTERISTICS & DANGERS. KEEP
    OUT OF THE REACH OF CHILDREN.
