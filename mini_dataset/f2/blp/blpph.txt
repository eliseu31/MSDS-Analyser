=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    IF BREATHING DIFFICULTY, ADMINISTER OXYGEN. EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR
    15 MIN. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING, GIVE LARGE AMOUNTS OF
    WATER. I F VOMITING OCCURS SPONTANEOUSLY, KEEP HEAD BELOW HIPS TO
    PREVENT ASPIRATION OF LIQUIDS INTO LUNGS. DON'T GIVE ANYTHING BY
    MOUTH TO AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES.
