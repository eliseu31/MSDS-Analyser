=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:INHALATION: IRRITATING TO ALL PARTS OF

    EYES: IRRITATING. SKIN: CAN DEFECT & IRRITATE.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. KEEP WARM. GIVE OXYGEN/CPR
    IF NEEDED. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY W/SOAP & WATER. EYES: FLUSH
    W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR 15 MINS. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN
    ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:98F
Lower Limits:1.1
Extinguishing Media:WATER FOG, FOAM, DRY CHEMICAL, CO2
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:AT ELEVATED TEMPERATURES, POLYMERIZATION
    MAY TAKE PLACE. IF CONTAINED, VIOLENT RUPTURE MAY RESULT. FLAMMABLE
    LIQUID.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:REMOVE SOURCES OF IGNITION & VENTILATE AREA.
    ABSORB W/INERT MATERIAL SUCH AS VERMICULATE/SAND & PLACE IN CLOSED
    CONTAINER FOR DISPOSAL AS SOLID WASTE. WASH AREA W/NON FLAMMABLE
    SOLVENT/TRISODIUM PHOSP HATE & WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN ORIGINAL CLOSED CONTAINERS.
    STORE <80F.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:USE U.S. BUREAU OF MINES APPROVED AIRLINE
    MASK/SCBA.
