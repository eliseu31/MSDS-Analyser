=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGESTION: IF SWALLOWED, CALL POISON CONTROL CENTER, HOSPITAL
    EMERGENCY ROOM/PHYSICIAN IMMEDIATELY. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH
    AIR IMMEDIATELY. IF BREATHING HAS STOPPED, GIVE ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRATION.  KEEP WARM & QUIET. EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTYOF WATER FOR
    15 MINUTES. SKIN: WASH AFFECTED AREA W/SOAP & WATER. OBTAIN MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:CONFINE IN SMALL AREA, USE INERT ABSORBENT.
    PLACE RECOVERED LIQUID & USED ABSORBENT IN SUITABLE CONTAINERS FOR
    PROPER DISPOSAL. WEAR RESPIRATOR & PROTECTIVE CLOTHING. PREVENT
    CONTAMINATION OF SEWERS,  STREAMS & GROUNDWATER W/SPILLED MATERIAL.
