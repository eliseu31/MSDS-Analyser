=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER.
    INGESTION: IF UNCONSCIOUS, MAINTAIN BREATHING & HEART BEAT BY
    GIVING CPR. IF CONSCIOUS, INDUCE VOMITING W/SYRUP OF IPECAC OR
    STICK FINGER TO T HE BACK OF THROAT. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH
    TO AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, CO2, FOAM, OR WATER
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS & FULL
    PROTECTIVE CLOTHING. ISOLATE FIRE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:ACCUMULATION OF GRAIN DUST CAN RESULT IN
    EXPLOSIVE MIXTURES. GRAINS WILL BURN AT HIGH TEMPERATURES.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ISOLATE & POST SPILL. WEAR PROTECTIVE CLOTHING
    & EQUIPMENT. KEEP UNPROTECTED PERSONS & ANIMALS AWAY. IF PURE,
    PACKAGE PRODUCT IN ORINIGAL CONTAINER FOR USE. IF IMPURE, SHOVEL
    WASTE INTO AN APPROVED DR UM. SWEEP AREA & WIPE DOWN W/WET CLOTH OR
    TOWEL.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T BREATH SMOKE OR VAPORS. KEEP OUT
    OF REACH OF CHILDREN & ANIMALS. DON'T CONTAMINATE WATER, FOOD, OR
    FEED BY STORAGE OR DISPOSAL.
Other Precautions:STORE IN ORIGINAL CONTAINER ONLY. PROTECT AGAINST
    RODENT PENETRATION OF CARTON. STORE APART FROM
    PESTICIDES/FERTILIZERS/FOOD/ANIMAL FEED, WHICH MAY CAUSE
    CROSS-CONTAMINATION OF ODOR/INSECT INFESTATION .

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:USE A CANISTER RESPIRATOR OR A FULL FACE
    RESPIRATOR MASK
Ventilation:RECOMMENDED TO KEEP ACCUMULATION OF DUST <TLV.
Protective Gloves:CHEMICAL, SURGICAL , OR WASHED COTTON
Eye Protection:CHEMICAL GOGGLES
Other Protective Equipment:LONG SLEEVED UNIFORM, COVERALLS, OR LAB
    JACKET WHEN HANDLING/BLENDING PRODUCTS W/UNTREATED GRAINS.
Work Hygienic Practices:WASH BEFORE EATING, DRINKING, & SMOKING. SHOWER
