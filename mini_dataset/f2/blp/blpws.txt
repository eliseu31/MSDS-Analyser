=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH
    AIR. GIVE CPR IF NECESSARY. KEEP WARM/QUIET. EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY
    OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. SKIN: WASH AFFECTED AREA W/SOAP & WATER.
    NOTE T O PHYSICIAN: DON'T GIVE STIMULANTS. EPINEPHRINE/EPHEDRINE
    MAY ADVERSELY AFFECT THE HEART W/FATAL RESULTS. OBTAIN MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point: -156F
Extinguishing Media:ALCOHOL FOAM, CO2, DRY CHEMICAL, POLYMER FOAM
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR SCBA. WATER MAY BE INEFFECTIVE, BUT MAY
    BE USED TO COOL EXPOSED CONTAINERS. IF WATER IS USED, FOG NOZZLES
    ARE PREFERRED.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:EXTREMELY FLAMMABLE. VAPORS MAY CAUSE
    FLASH FIRE. CONTAINERS MAY EXPLODE WHEN EXPOSED TO EXTREME HEAT.
    HALOGENATED HYRDOCARBONS EXPLODE IN CONTACT W/ALUMINUM.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:KEEP PEOPLE AWAY. ELIMINATE IGNITION SOURCES.
    DIKE/CONTAIN W/INERT MATERIAL. TRANSFER LIQUIDS TO COVERED METAL
    CONTAINERS FOR RECOVERY/DISPOSAL. USE NON-SPARKING TOOLS. PLACE
    ABSORBENT DIKING MATERIAL S IN COVERED CONTAINER. PREVENT RUNOFF TO
    WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T STORE >95F. STORE LARGE
    QUANTITIES IN COMPLIANCE W/OSHA. DON'T PUNCTURE/INCINERATE. DON'T
    SPRAY NEAR FLAME/HOT SURFACES.
Other Precautions:AVOID BREATHING VAPOR/SPRAY MIST. KEEP OUT OF REACH
    OF CHILDREN.
