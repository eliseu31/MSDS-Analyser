=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. SKIN:
    WIPE OFF W/TOWEL, REMOVE W/PAINT THINNER & WASH W/SOAP & WATER.
    INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE CPR OR OXYGEN IF NEEDED.
    INGESTION: O BTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES. SEE SUPP.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:0F
Extinguishing Media:CLASS B FIRE EXTINGUISHER
Fire Fighting Procedures:WATER MAY BE INEFFECTIVE. DIRECT WATER STREAM
    MAY SPREAD BURNING LIQUID.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:AT TEMPS >120F CANS MAY RUPTURE RELEASING
    FLAMMABLE LIQUID & VAPOR. VAPORS ARE >AIR & MAY TRAVEL ALONG THE
    GROUND OR MOVE BY VENTILATION TO AN IGNITION SOURCE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ELIMINATE IGNITION SOURCES. VENTILATE AREA.
    ALLOW AEROSOL TO ESCAPE, COVER & MIX SPILL W/ABSORBENT MATERIAL.
    COLLECT IN METAL CONTAINERS & ALLOW MATERIAL TO DRY IN OPEN AIR.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T STORE >120F OR PUNCTURE OR
    INCINERATE CANS. KEEP AWAY FROM CORROSIVE OR DAMP ATMOSPHERE. KEEP
    AWAY FROM FOOD. AVOID INHALATION OF SPRAY.
Other Precautions:STORE LARGE QUANTITIES IN BUILDING DESIGNED &
    PROTECTED FOR STORAGE OF CLASS 1A FLAMMABLE LIQUIDS. INTENTIONAL
    MISUSE BY CONCENTRATION OF VAPORS & INHALING CONTENTS CAN BE
    HARMFUL & FATAL. EMPTY CONTA INERS MAY BE HAZARDOUS. 8.5 LB/GAL.
