=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: A BURN FROM MOLTEN WAX SHOULD BE TREATED AS A THERMAL
    BURN. HOT LIQ WAX TENDS TO CLING TO FLESH ESPECIALLY AFTER
    SOLIDIFYING, IT SHOULD BE COOLED AS QUICKLY AS POSS USING, FOR
    EXAMPLE, TAP WATER . INGEST: CALL MD IMMED . INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH
    AIR. SUPPORT BRTHG (GIVE O*2/ARTF RESP). CALL MD . EYE:IMMED FLUSH
    W/POTABLE H*2O FOR A MINIMUM OF 15 MINS, SEEK ASSISTANCE FROM MD .

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:COC
Flash Point:500F,260C
Extinguishing Media:WATER SPRAY, FOAM, DRY CHEMICAL, FOG OR CO*2.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED SCBA AND FULL
    PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT . WHEN WATER IS USED, APPLY USING SPRAY OR FOG
    NOZZLE. WAX IS A SURFACE BURNING MATERIAL.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:NONE

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:IF HOT LIQUID, ATTEMPT TO CONFINE SPILL AND
    LET THE WAX SOLIDIFY. ONCE SOLID, WAX MAY BE RECOVERED AS ANY OTHER
    MATERIAL.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE AWAY FROM OPEN FLAME, BOILERS
    AND STRONG OXIDANTS SUCH AS LIQUID CHLORINE, SODIUM HYPOCHLORITE,
    ETC.
Other Precautions:IF THIS MATERIAL IS PURCHASED AS A POWDER OR IS TO BE
    SUBDIVIDED TO OR COLLECTED AS A POWDER REFER TO ANSI/NFPA PAMPHLET
    NO. 654, "FIRE AND DUST EXPLOSION PREVENTION-CHEMICAL, DYE,
    PHARMACEUTICAL, PLA STICS INDUSTRIES 1982".
