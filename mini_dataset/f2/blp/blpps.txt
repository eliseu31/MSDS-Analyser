=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES/SKIN: FLUSH W/WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. INGESTION: DON'T
    INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER. GIVE AT LEAST 1 OZ
    OF MILK OF MAGNESIA IN AN EQUAL AMOUNT OF WATER OR THE WHITES OF 3
    EGGS. N EVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON.
    INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION.
    OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
