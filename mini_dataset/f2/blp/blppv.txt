=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP &
    WATER. INHALATION: MOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE OXYGEN, ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRATION. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. KEEP WARM. OBTAIN
    MEDICAL ATT ENTION ALL IN CASES.
