=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER. SKIN: WASH W/MILD SOAP &
    WATER. DON'T COVER. TREAT AS 1ST DEGREE BURN. INHALATION: REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR. INGESTION: DRINK LIQUIDS. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH
    TO AN UNCO NSCIOUS PERSON. DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. OBTAIN MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
