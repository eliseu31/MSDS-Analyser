=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:NO  Ingestion:NO
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:YES    IARC:YES	  OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:INHALATION: UPPER RESPIRATORY TRACT
    IRRITATION, NAUSEA, & METAL FUME FEVER. EYES: IRRITATION.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:LEAD IS SUSPECTED HUMAN CARCINOGEN BY
    ACGIH.
Effects of Overexposure:INHALATION: TOXIC EFFECTS AS CENTRAL NERVOUS
    SYSTEM DISTURBANCES, RENAL CHANGES, PERIPHERAL NEUROPATHY,
    GASTROINTESTINAL DISTURBANCES, ANEMIA, & CHROMOSOMAL CHANGES.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. EYES: FLUSH W/WATER. OBTAIN
    MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:DRY POWDER OR SAND
Fire Fighting Procedures:DON'T USE WATER OR HALOGEN ON DUST FIRES.
    FLAMMABLE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:SMALL CHIPS/FINE TURNINGS/DUST MAY
    IGNITE. DAMP ALUMINUM DUST MAY SPONTANEOUSLY HEAT. MOLTEN ALUMINUM
    MAY EXPLODE ON CONTACT W/WATER & CERTAIN METAL OXIDES.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DON'T TOUCH CAST ALUMINUM METAL OR
    HEATED ALUMINUM PRODUCT W/O KNOWING METAL TEMP. ALUMINUM
    EXPERIENCES NO COLOR CHANGE DURING HEATING.
Other Precautions:ALUMINUM POWDER MUST BE PACKAGED/SHIPPED AS A
    FLAMMABLE SOLID. HARD ALLOY INGOTS IN THE 2000/7000 SERIES MUST BE
    STRESS RELIEVED TO PREVENT EXPLOSION WHEN SAWED. DURING
    MELTING/CUTTING/GRINDING ALUMIN UM ALLOYS ARE CONSIDERED NUISANCE
    DUST.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:APPROPRIATE RESPIRATOR DEPENDS UPON POTENTIAL
    AIRBORNE CONTAMINANTS & THEIR CONCENTRATIONS. IF >TLV, USE NIOSH
    APPROVED RESPIRATION EQUIPMENT.
Protective Gloves:RECOMMENDED FOR SHEET/COIL
Eye Protection:SAFETY GLASSES OR SHIELD
Other Protective Equipment:AS NEEDED DEPENDING ON OPERATION & SAFETY
    CODS.
Supplemental Safety and Health
ALUMINUM ALLOYS WILL BE COMPRISED OF VARIOUS COMBINATIONS OF THE
