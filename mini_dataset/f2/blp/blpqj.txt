Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:INHALATION: IRRITATION OF THE

    EYES: IRRITATION. SKIN: IRRITATION & MAY BE HARMFUL IF ABSORBED.
    INGESTION: STOMACH &/OR INTESTINAL IRRI TATION, NAUSEA, VOMITING &
    DIARRHEA.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:SILICA IS LISTED W/IARC AS A CLASS 2A
    CARCINOGEN.
Effects of Overexposure:INHALATION: REPEATED/PROLONGED OVEREXPOSURE TO
    SOLVENTS CAUSE PERMANENT BRAIN & NERVOUS SYSTEM DAMAGE.
    PROLONGED/REPEATED BREATHING OF SPRAY MIST/SANDING DUST OVER A
    PERIOD OF YEARS MAY CAUSE DUST DIS EASE OF THE LUNGS.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: MOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING CEASES, RESTORE
    USING APPROVED CPR TECHNIQUES. EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINS. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. REMOVE SOILED CLOTHING. INGESTION:
    OBTAI N MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:SCC
Flash Point:85F
Lower Limits:1%
Extinguishing Media:USE CO2, DRY CHEMICAL, FOAM, NFPA METHOD FOR CLASS
    B FIRE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE FULL PROTECTIVE CLOTHING/SCBA. WATER SPRAY
    MAY BE INEFFECTIVE. IF WATER IS USED, FOG NOZZLES PREFERABLE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:WATER SPRAY MAY BE USED TO COOL CLOSED
    CONTAINERS TO PREVENT PRESSURE BUILD-UP, POSSIBLE AUTOIGNITION &
    EXPLOSION WHEN EXPOSED TO EXTREME HEAT.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:VENTILATE AREA. CONTAIN SPILLED MATERIAL &
    REMOVE W/INERT ABSORBENT & NON-SPARKING TOOLS. STORE IN CLOSED
    CONTAINER UNTIL PROPERLY DISPOSED OF.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP CONTAINER CLOSED WHEN NOT IN USE.
    DON'T TRANSFER CONTENTS TO BOTTLES OR OTHER UNLABELLED CONTAINERS.
    DON'T REUSE EMPTY CONTAINERS. (SEE SUPP.).
Other Precautions:DON'T BREATHE VAPORS/SPRAY MIST/SANDING DUST.
    INTENTIONAL MISUSE BY DELIBERATELY CONCENTRATING/INHALATION THE
    CONTENTS MAY BE HARMFUL/FATAL. VAPORS MAY IGNITE EXPLOSIVELY/MAY
    SPREAD LONG DISTANCES/BEY OND CLOSED DOORS. DON'T GET IN EYES/SKIN
