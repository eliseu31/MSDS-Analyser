=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. SKIN: DON'T ATTEMPT TO
    REMOVE HOT ASPHALT. COOL W/ICE, COVER W/PETROLEUM JELLY TO SOFTEN.
    OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:COC
Flash Point:530F
Extinguishing Media:CO2, DRY CHEMICAL OR FOAM
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE WATER ONLY TO COOL FIRE EXPOSED
    CONTAINERS. DON'T ENTER CONFINED FIRE SPACE W/O PROPER PROTECTIVE
    EQUIPMENT. WEAR A SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:AVOID WATER CONTACT W/HOT ASPHALT.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SHUT OFF LEAK SOURCE & CONTAIN SPILL W/EARTH
    OR SAND.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID SKIN CONTACT & BREATHING VAPORS.
