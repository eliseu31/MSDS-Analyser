=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. RESTORE & SUPPORT CONTINUED
    BREATHING. HAVE TRAINED PERSON GIVE OXYGEN IF NECESSARY. GET
    MEDICAL HELP FOR ANY BREATHING DIFFICULTY. SKIN: WASH OFF QUICKLY
    W/PLENTY OF  WATER, THEN SOAP & WATER. EYES: FLUSH IMMEDIATELY
    W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. INGESTION: OBTAIN
    MEDICAL TREATMENT IMMEDIATELY. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:76F
Lower Limits:1.7%
Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL OR FOAM
Fire Fighting Procedures:WATER MAY BE USED TO COOL & PROTECT EXPOSED
    CONTAINERS. FLAMABLE LIQUID.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:CLOSED CONTAINERS EXPLODE WHEN EXPOSED TO
    EXTREME HEAT OR FIRE. VAPORS CAN FORM EXPLOSIVE MIXTURES IN AIR AT
    ELEVATED TEMPS. MAY DECOMPOSE EMITTING TOXIC GASES.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:COMPLY W/ALL APPLICABLE HEALTH & ENVIRONMENTAL
    REGULATIONS. ELIMINATE ALL SOURCES OF IGNITION. VENTILATE AREA
    W/EXPLOSION PROOF EQUIPMENT. SPILLS MAY BE COLLECTED
    W/NONCOMBUSTIBLE ABSORBENT MATERIALS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE <80F/VENTILATE AREA. DON'T TAKE
    INTERNALLY. KEEP CONTAINERS TIGHTLY CLOSED/UPRIGHT WHEN NOT IN USE.
    EMPTY CONTAINERS CONTAIN HAZARDOUS RESIDUES.
Other Precautions:KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN. AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN &
    EYES & INHALATION OF VAPORS. GROUND EQUIPMENT WHEN TRANSFERRING TO
    PREVENT ACCUMULATION OF STATIC CHARGE. INTENTIONAL MISUSE BY
    DELIBERATELY CONCE NTRATING/INHALING CAN BY HARMFUL/FATAL.
