    VAPORS. USE PROTECTIVE MEASURES OUTLINED IN CONTROL MEASURES.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN CLSD, PROPERLY LBLD CNTNR IN
    A COOL, VENTILATED AREA.KEEP AWAY FROM HEAT, OPEN FLAMES &
    OXIDIZING AGENTS. DO NOT TRANSFER TO UNMARKER CNTNRS.
Other Precautions:IF OXYGEN LEVEL IS BELOW 19.5% &/OR RESIN VAP
    CONCENTRATION IS 2% FOR CANISTERS OR 0.1% FOR CARTRIDGES OR ABOVE,
    USE NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED SCBA UNITS. USE WITH ADEQUATE VENTILATION.
    AVOID SKIN AND EYE C ONTACT. HARMFUL OR FATAL IF SWALLOWED.
