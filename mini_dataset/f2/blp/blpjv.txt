=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. INGESTION: GIVE LARGE
    QUANTITIES OF WATER, THEN SEVERAL GLASSES OF MILK. INDUCE VOMITING.
    EYES: FLUSH W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR 15 MINS. SKIN: WASH
    W/PLENTY OF SOAP  & WATER. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
