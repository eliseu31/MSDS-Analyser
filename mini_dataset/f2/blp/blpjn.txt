=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER
    OR SKIN CLEANSER. EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE
    TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING DIFFICULTY, ADMINISTER OXYGEN. IF
    BREATHING S TOPS, BEGIN ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. KEEP WARM & QUIET.
    OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
