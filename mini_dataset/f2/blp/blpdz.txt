=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:NO
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:ACUTE- MAY BE HARMFUL BY INHALATION,
    INGESTION OR SKIN ABSORPTION. CAUSES EYE AND SKIN IRRITATION.
    MATERIAL IS IRRITATING TO MUCOUS MEMBRANES AND UPPER RESPIRATORY
    TRACT.
Effects of Overexposure:MATERIAL IS IRRITATING TO EYES, SKIN, MUCOUS
    MEMBRANES AND UPPER RESPIRATORY TRACT.
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:PRE-EXISTING SKIN DISORDERS

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:CALL A PHYSICIAN IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST. INHALATION: REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR. GIVE CPR/OXYGEN IF NEEDED. EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MINUTES, HOLDING EYELIDS OPEN. SKIN: WASH WITH SOAP &
    WATER. IN GESTION: CALL A PHYSICIAN.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NONE
Extinguishing Media:USE WATER FOG, CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, OR DRY
    CHEMICAL FOR SURROUNDING FIRE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR FIRE FIGHTING PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT AND A
    FULL FACED SELF CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS. EVACUATE AREA. COOL
    FIRE EXPOSED CONTAINERS WITH WATER SPRAY.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:TOXIC FUMES

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WEAR RESPIRATOR, CHEMICAL SAFETY GOGGLES,
    RUBBER BOOTS AND HEAVY RUBBER GLOVES. VENTILATE AREA. AVOID RAISING
    DUST. CAREFULLY SWEEP UP AND PUT IN A BAG FOR WASTE DISPOSAL. WASH
    SPILL SITE AFTER MATERI AL PICKUP IS COMPLETE.
Neutralizing Agent:WEAK ACID

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORAGE-STORE IN A COOL DRY PLACE.
    KEEP TIGHTLY CLOSED.
Other Precautions:INCOMPATIBLE WITH ACID SALTS, SALTS OF ZINC AND IRON,
    ALKALOIDS, ALUM, TARTAR EMETIC AND CALOMEL. AVOID BREATHING DUST.
    AVOID CONTACT WITH EYES, SKIN AND CLOTHING. AIR-SENSITIVE.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:NONE REQUIRED WHERE ADEQUATE VENTILATION
    CONDITION EXISTS.IF AIRBORNE CONCENTRATION IS HIGH, USE A
    NIOSH/MSHA APPROVED RESPIRATOR OR DUST MASK.
Ventilation:MECHANICAL (GENERAL) EXHAUST
Protective Gloves:NATURAL RUBBER.
Eye Protection:SAFETY GOGGLES
Other Protective Equipment:PROTECTIVE CLOTHING AND EQUIPMENT TO PREVENT
    ANY POSSIBILITY OF SKIN CONTACT. EYE WASH STATION & SAFETY SHOWER.
Work Hygienic Practices:WASH THOROUGHLY AFTER USE AND BEFORE EATING OR
    DRINKING. LAUNDER CONTAMINATED CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE.
