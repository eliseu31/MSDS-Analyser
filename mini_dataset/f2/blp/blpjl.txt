=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. SKIN:
    WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE CPR OR
    OXYGEN IF NEEDED. KEEP INDIVIDUAL CALM. INGESTION: TREAT
    SYMPTOMATICALLY. OB TAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
