=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE MOUTH-TO-MOUTH
    RESUSCITATION OR OXYGEN IF NECESSARY. KEEP VICTIM WARM/REST. SKIN
    WASH W/PLENTY OF SOAP & WATER. EYES: W/RUNNING WATER FOR AT LEAST
    15 MINS. INGEST ION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. IF VICTIM IS CONSCIOUS,
    GIVE LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER. DON'T ATTEMPT TO GIVE FLUIDS TO
    UNCONSCIOUS VICTIM. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NONE
Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, CO2, WATER SPRAY
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE WATER TO KEEP FIRE-EXPOSED
    CONTAINERS/SURROUNDING EQUIPMENT & STRUCTURES COOL & TO FLUSH
    SPILLS AWAY FROM FIRE. USE SCBA W/POSITIVE PRESSURE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:MOP UP/SOAK UP/CONTAIN BY DIKING
    W/NON-COMBUSTIBLE ABSORBENT INORGANIC MATERIAL. PREVENT
    RUNOFF/DUMPING INTO SEWERS/STORM DRAINS/SURFACE WATER/SOIL.
    TRANSFER CONTAMINATED ABSORBENT TO A DOT-APPROVED C ONTAINER. USE
    PROTECTIVE EQUIP & WATER TO FLUSH.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:USE/STORE W/ADEQUATE VENTILATION.
    GROUND ALL EQUIPMENT. AVOID BREATHING VAPORS, MISTS, CONTACT W/SKIN
    & EYES. KEEP CONTAINERS COOL/DRY.
Other Precautions:DON'T CONSUME FOOD, DRINK, TOBACCO IN AREAS WHERE
    THEY MAY BECOME CONTAMINATED W/MATERIAL. KEEP CONTAINER TIGHTLY
    CLOSED WHEN NOT IN USE. DON'T WEAR CONTACT LENSES. DON'T
    CUT/GRIND/WELD/DRILL ON/NEAR  CONTAINER. TRAIN ALL EMPLOYEES.
