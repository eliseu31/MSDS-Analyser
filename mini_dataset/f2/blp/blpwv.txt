=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR 15 MIN. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER OR
    WATERLESS HAND CLEANER FOLLOWED BY SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE
    TO FRESH AIR & PROVIDE OXYGEN IF BREATHING IF DIFFICULT. INGESTION:
    DON T' INDUCE VOMITING. NO TREATMENT IS NECESSARY UNLESS LARGE
    QUANTITIES ARE INGESTED. DON'T WAIT FOR SYMPTOMS TO DEVELOP. OBTAIN
    MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
