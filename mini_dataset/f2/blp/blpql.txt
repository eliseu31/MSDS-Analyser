=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. CPR IF NECESSARY. SKIN: WASH
    W/SOAP & WATER. EYES: FLUSH W/WATER. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE
    VOMITING. NOTE TO PHYSICIAN: VISCOSITY <100 SUS (100F): CAREFUL
    GASTRIC LAVAG E W/TIGHT FITTING/CUFFED TUBE IS PREFERRED OVER
    EMESIS. VISCOSITIY >100 SUS (100F): EMESIS MAY BE INDUCED.
    SUBCUTANEOUS INJECTION REQUIRES SURGICAL DEBRIDEMENT. OBTAIN
    MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
