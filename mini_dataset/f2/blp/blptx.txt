    WARM & QUIET. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF
    WATER FOR 15 MINS. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================
