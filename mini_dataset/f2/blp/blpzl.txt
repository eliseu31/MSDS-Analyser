=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR 15 MINS. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY W/SOAP
    & RINSE W/WATER. INGESTION: INDUCE VOMITING & GIVE WATER. IF
    UNCONSCIOUS, HAVING TROUBLE BREATHING OR IN CONVULSIONS, DON'T
    INDUCE VOMITIN G OR GIVE WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:PMCC
Flash Point:76F
Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, ALCOHOL FOAM, CO2, OR OTHER
    EXTINGUISHING AGENT SUITABLE FOR CLASS B FIRES.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE WATER TO KEEP FIRE-EXPOSED CONTAINERS
    COOL. FOR LARGE FIRES, USE WATER SPRAY OR FOG, THOROUGHLY DRENCHING
    THE BURNING MATERIAL.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:FLAMMABLE LIQUID. EXPLOSIONS MAY OCCUR
