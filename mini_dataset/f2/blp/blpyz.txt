=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF
    WATER FOR 15 MINS. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WARM WATER. INGESTION: DRINK
    1 OR 2 GLASSES OF WATER, MILK OR BEATEN EGG TO DILUTE. DON'T INDUCE
    VOMITIN G AS ADDITIONAL ESOPHOGEAL DAMAGE MAY OCCUR. TREAT
    SYMPTOMATICALLY. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES. NOTE TO
    PHYSICIAN: INDUCE GASTRIC LAVAGE.
