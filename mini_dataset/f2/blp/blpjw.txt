=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: MOVE TO FRESH AIR & TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY. IF
    BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN. IF BREATHING STOPS, GIVE
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. EYES: FLUSH W/WATER 5-6 MINS. SKIN: WASH
    W/SOAP & WATER.  INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. IF IT OCCURS,
    KEEP HEAD BELOW HIPS TO PREVENT ASPIRATION OF LIQUID INTO LUNGS.
    OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES. (SEE SUPP.).

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SPILL IS UNLIKELY, PRODUCT IS IN AN AEROSOL
    CONTAINER. IF SPILLED, PREVENT FROM ENTERING SEWER OR WATER
    SOURCES. VENTILATE ENCLOSED AREA. CLEAN UP W/RAGS OR ABSORB W/SAND,
    COTTON WASTE OR OTHER ABSORB ANTS.
