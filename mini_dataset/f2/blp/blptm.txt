=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION
    IF NECESSARY. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MIN. INGESTION: DRINK 1 OR 2 GLASSES OF WATER TO DILUTE.
    DON'T  INDUCE VOMITING. TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY. OBTAIN MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
