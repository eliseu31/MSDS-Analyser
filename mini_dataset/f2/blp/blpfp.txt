=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGEST:WASH OUT MOUTH WITH WATER IF PERSON IS CONSCIOUS. CALL
    MD. SKIN:FLUSH WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES. REMOVE CONTAMIANTED CLOTHING AND SHOES AND CALL MD.
    EYE:FLUSH WITH C OPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15MINUTES.
    ASSURE ADEQUATE FLUSHING BY SEPARATING THE EYELIDS WITH FINGERS.
    CALL MD. INHAL:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING BECOMES DIFFICULT,
    CALL MD.
