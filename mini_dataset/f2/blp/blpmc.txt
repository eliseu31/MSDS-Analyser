=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15-30 MINS.
    INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER OR
    MILK. SKIN: FLUSH W/WATER & TREAT AS THERMAL BURN. INHALATION:
    REMOVE TO FRESH A IR. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
    PHYSICIAN NOTE: IF LAVAGE IS PERFORMED, DO ENDOTRACHEAL &/OR
    ESOPHAGOSCOPIC CONTROL. NO SPECIFIC ANTIDOTE. SUPPORTIVE CARE.

Handling and Storage Precautions:GROUND ALL TRANSFER EQUIPMENT. HOLD

    CONTACT W/COPPER OR COPPER-BEARING ALLOYS.
Other Precautions:USE ONLY W/ADEQUATE VENTILATION. FOLLOW GOOD

    EXPOSURE AT HIGH TEMPERATURES. AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN.
