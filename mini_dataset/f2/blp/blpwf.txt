=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING STOPS, GIVE
    MOUTH-TO-MOUTH RESUSCITATION. IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE
    OXYGEN. KEEP WARM & AT REST. SKIN: WASH W/PLENTY OF SOAP & WATER.
    EYES: WASH W/W ATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE
    VOMITING. IF VICTIM IS CONSCIOUS, GIVE LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER.
    DON'T GIVE FLUIDS TO UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION
    IN ALL CASES.

Flash Point Method:PMCC

Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, FOAM, OR CO2

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================
