=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: THOROUGHLY WASH W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, ADMINISTER OXYGEN. IF
    BREATHING HAS STOPPED, GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. KEEP WARM &
    QUIET. DON'T GIV E STIMULANTS. EPINEPHRINE OR EPHEDRINE MAY
    ADVERSELY AFFECT THE HEART W/FATAL RESULTS. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE
    VOMITING. EYES: FLUSH W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER. OBTAIN MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
