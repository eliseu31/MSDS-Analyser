=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:NO  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:YES	 OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:INHALATION: IRRITATION OF THE
    RESPIRATORY TRACT, HEADACHE, NAUSEA, DIZZINESS, WEAKNESS & FATIGUE,
    UNCONSCIOUSNESS & RESPIRATORY ARREST. EYES: INJURY. SKIN:
    IRRITATION. INGESTION: STOMACH & OR INTESTIN AL IRRITATION, NAUSEA,
    VOMITING/DIARRHEA.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:CRYSTALLINE SILICA (CRITOBALITE) IS
    CONSIDERED TO BE A 2A HUMAN CARCINOGEN.
Effects of Overexposure:INHALATION: PERMANENT BRAIN & NERVOUS SYSTEM
    DAMAGE & SILICOSIS. INGESTION: ASPIRATION OF VOMITUS CAN CAUSE
    CHEMICAL PNEUMONITIS, WHICH CAN BE FATAL. SKIN: IRRITATION. EYES:
    INJURY.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS.
    SKIN: REMOVE W/SOAP & WATER. INGESTION: DRINK 1 OR 2 GLASSES OF
    WATER TO DILUTE. DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY.
    INHALATION: RE MOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING CEASES,RESTORE USING
    APPROVED CPR TECHNIQUES. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:SCC
Flash Point:102F
Extinguishing Media:CO2, DRY CHEMICAL, FOAM, OR OTHER NFPA APPROVED
    METHOD FOR TREATING CLASS B FIRE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE FULL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT & SCBA. WATER
    SPRAY MAY BE INEFFECTIVE. IF WATER IS USED, FOG NOZZLES ARE
    PREFERABLE.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:WATER SPRAY MAY BE USED TO COOL CLOSED
    CONTAINERS TO PREVENT PRESSURE BUILD-UP, POSSIBLE AUTOIGNITION, &
    EXPLOSION DUE TO EXTREME HEAT. COMBUSTIBLE LIQUID.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:CONTAIN SPILLED MATERIAL & REMOVE W/INERT
    ABSORBENT & NON-SPARKING TOOLS. STORE IN CLOSED CONTAINERS UNTIL
    PROPERLY DISPOSED OF. REMOVE ALL SOURCES OF IGNITION. VENTILATE
    AREA.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP CONTAINER TIGHTLY CLOSED WHEN NOT
    IN USE. DON'T TRANSFER CONTENTS TO BOTTLES/UNLABELLED CONTAINERS.
    USE ONLY W/ADEQUATE VENTILATION.
Other Precautions:DON'T REUSE EMPTY CONTAINER. KEEP AWAY FROM CHILDREN.
    DON'T BREATH VAPORS/SPARY MIST/SANDING DUST. DON'T GET ON
    EYES/SKIN. DON'T SMOKE. NEVER USE WELDING/CUTTING TORCH ON OR NEAR
    CONTAINER, EVEN WHEN  EMPTY, AS PRODUCT/RESIDUE MAY IGNITE.
