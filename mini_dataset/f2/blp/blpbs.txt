=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: PROVIDE FRESH AIR; IF BREATHING LABORED, GIVE OXYGEN
    OR ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY W/SOAP & WATER.
    EYES: FLUSH IMMEDIATELY W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR A MINIMUM OF 15
    MINUTES & GET  MED ATTN. INGEST: DRINK 1 OR 2 GLASSESOF WATER TO
    DILUTE. DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. CONSULT MD OR POISON CONTROL CENTER
    IMMEDIATELY. TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY.
