=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:GET MEDICAL ASSISTANCE IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST.EYE:IMMEDIATELY
    FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES,HOLDING EYELIDS OPEN.SKIN:WASH WITH
    SOAP & WATER FOR 15 MINUTES.INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.GIVE CPR
    IF NE EDED.INGESTION:IF CONSCIOUS, DRINK 2-4 GLASSES OF
    MILK/WATER.CONTACT DOCTOR OR POISON CONTROL CENTER IMMEDIATELY.
