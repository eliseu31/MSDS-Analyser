=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR/GIVE OXYGEN IS REQUIRED. IF
    NOT BREATHING, GIVE CPR. EYES/SKIN: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR 15
    MIN. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. LUNG ASPIRATION HAZARD
    >PASSING TH ROUGH INTESTINAL TRACT. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES. PHYSICIAN NOTE: CATECHOLAMINE DRUGS, AS EPINEPHRINE,
    INCREASES RISK OF ELICITING CARDIAC DYSRYTHMIAS. CONSIDER AS LAST
    RESORT.
