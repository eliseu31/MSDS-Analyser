=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN/EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR 15 MINS. INHALATION:
    MOVE TO FRESH AIR. INGESTION: RINSE MOUTH & GIVE PLENTY OF WATER,
    FOLLOWED BY MILK, EGG WHITE OR GRUEL. DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. OBTAIN
    MEDICA L ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL. DON'T USE WATER.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ABSORB SPILL W/AN ABSORBENT MATERIAL, PICK UP
    & DEPOSIT IN A SEALABLE CONTAINER FOR DISPOSAL AS A HAZARDOUS
    WASTE. THOROUGHLY CLEAN AREA W/A DETERGENT SOLUTION. RINSE AREA
    THOROUGHLY W/CLEAN WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID CONTACT W/SKIN & EYES. AVOID
    INHALATION OF VAPORS. KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN. CAN SEVERE
    BURN WILL ATTACK ORGANIC MATERIAL.
Other Precautions:DON'T SPILL ON FLOOR, WOOD, PAINTED SURFACES. DON'T
    USE W/OTHER DRAIN OPENERS. NEVER USE A PLUNGER W/THIS PRODUCT.
    DON'T USE HOT WATER BEFORE OR AFTER USING THIS PRODUCT/COLD WATER
    ONLY. DON'T STORE W /OXIDIZABLE MATERIAL.
