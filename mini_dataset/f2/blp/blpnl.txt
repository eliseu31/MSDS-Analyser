=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN/EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS.
    INHALATION: MOVE PERSON TO FRESH AIR. INGESTION: INDUCE VOMITING BY
    GIVING 2 GLASSES OF WATER & STICKING FINGER DOWN THROAT. OBTAIN
    MEDICAL ATT ENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC

Extinguishing Media:WATER DILUTION RAISES FLASH POINT
Fire Fighting Procedures:FIRE EXPOSED DRUMS SHOULD BE COOLED W/STREAM
    OF WATER.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ELIMINATE ALL FLAME, IGNITION & HIGH-HEAT
    SOURCES. ABSORB SPILL ON INERT ABSORBENT MATERIAL. PICK UP & PLACE
    RESIDUE IN A SUITABLE WASTE CONTAINER OR, IF PERMITTED, FLUSH TO
    SEWER. FLUSH THOROUGHLY W/ WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:COMBUSTIBLE. STORE TIGHTLY CLOSED
    CONTAINER IN A DRY AREA AT TEMP. BETWEEN 40-120F. AVOID CONTACT
    W/SKIN & EYES. DON'T BREATHE SPRAY MISTS OR VAPORS.
Other Precautions:KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN. DON'T WEARING CONTACT
    LENSES.
