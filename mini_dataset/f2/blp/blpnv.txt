=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/TAP WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. SKIN: WASH
    W/SOAP & WATER. AFTER DRYING SKIN APPLY VITAMIN E OIL OR CREAM,
    OR,IF NOT AVAILABLE, USE ANY VEGETABLE OIL. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE
    VOMITING. I F VOMITING OCCURS KEEP HEAD LOWER THAN HIPS TO PREVENT
    ASPIRATION OF LIQUID INTO LUNGS. DRINK A LARGE QTY OF MILK, EGG
    WHITES OR GELATIN. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. OBTAIN MED ATTN
    IN ALL CASES.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:ABSORB SMALL SPILLS W/ABSORBENT MATERIAL SUCH
    AS CLAY. PUT ABSORBENT INTO CONTAINERS FOR DISPOSAL. AREA MAY BE
    WASHED DOWN W/WATER & HOUSEHOLD DETERGENTS. KEEP OUT OF SEWERS &
    WATERWAYS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A COOL DRY PLACE AWAY FROM
    FOOD, FEED OR CLOTHING. MININIUM SKIN CONTACT. DON'T GET IN EYES,
    ON SKIN OR ON CLOTHING.
Other Precautions:WASH W/SOAP & WATER BEFORE EATING, DRINKING, SMOKING
    OR USING TOILET FACILITIES. ITEMS WHICH CAN'T BE ADEQUATELY CLEANED
    SHOULD BE DISPOSED OF. THE IS EQUIVALENT TO 233 GALLONS OF GOLD
    CREST TRIBUTE.
