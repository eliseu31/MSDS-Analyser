=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER. USE SKIN CREAM W/LANOLIN. EYES:
    FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR 15 MINS. INHALATION: MOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    IF NEEDED, ADMINISTER OXYGEN. INGESTION: GIVE W/PLENTY OF WATER.
    DON'T IN DUCE VOMITING. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTIONIN ALL CASES.
