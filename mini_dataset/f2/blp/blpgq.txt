=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION.EYE:FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES.SKIN:IF HOT WAX STRIKES THE SKIN,IMMERSE THE AREA IN
    WATER.APPLY ICED WATER/ICE PACKED(IF <10% OF THE BODY BURNED).DO
    NOT REMOVE WAX .IF COOL WAX CONTACTS SKIN,WASH WITH WARM SOAPY
    WATER.INHALATION:REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.INGESTION:CONSULT MEDICAL
    PERSONNEL.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:435F,224C
Extinguishing Media:USE WATER FOG, CARBON DIOXIDE, FOAM, OR DRY
    CHEMICAL.DO NOT USE A DIRECT STREAM OF WATER.PRODUCT WILL FLOAT &
    REIGNITE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:DO NOT ENTER CONFINED FIRE SPACE WITHOUT FULL
    BUNKER GEAR MATERIAL,INCLUDING A POSITIVE PRESSURE NIOSH APPROVED
    SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:MATERIAL WILL NOT BURN UNLESS PREHEATED.
    COOL FIRE EXPOSED CONTAINERS WITH WATER.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:MAY BURN ALTHOUGH NOT READILY IGNITABLE.USE
    CAUTIOUS JUDGMENT WHEN CLEANING UP LARGE MOLTEN SPILLS.FOR LARGE
    SPILLS,WEAR RESPIRATOR & PROTECTIVE CLOTHING.SHUT OFF SOURCE OF
    LEAK IF SAFE TO DO SO.DIKE	& CONTAIN.ALLOW TO COOL.SMALL
    SPILLS:COOL & REMOVE
Neutralizing Agent:NOT APPLICABLE.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORAGE-STORE IN A COOL PLACE.
Other Precautions:MINIMIZE BREATHING OF VAPORS, FUMES, OR MIST. AVOID
    CONTACT WITH HOT MATERIAL.
