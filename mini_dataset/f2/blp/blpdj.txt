    LEAST 15 MINUTES. IF IRRITATION PERSISTS, SEE MD, AMERICAN RED
    CROSS. INGEST: CALL MD IMMEDIATELY . INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    SUPPORT B REATHING (GIVE O*2/ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION) .

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID FREEZING/PRLNGD STORAGE >50C
    BECAUSE PROD CONTAINS WATER WHICH WILL FREEZE & DAM PROD/DRY-OUT
    ALSO DAMAGING PROD. AVOID CONT (SEE SUPP DATA)
Other Precautions:AVOID PACKING PROD IN AN UNPROTECTED GLASS COLUMN.
    EXPANSION OF THE PROD CAN CAUSE GLASS COLUMN TO SHATTER. WHEN
    TRANSFERRING PROD TO OTHER CNTNRS VIA HYDRAULIC (WATER) SLURRY ALL
    SAFETY PRACTICES EST ABLISHED FOR USE OF WATER (SUPP DATA)
