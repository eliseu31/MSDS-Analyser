=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN/EYES: FLUSH W/FLOWING WATER FOR 15 MINS. INGESTION:
    DRINK LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER. DON'T USE SODIUM BICARBONATE OR
    INDUCE VOMITING. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. ADMINISTER OXYGEN
    AFTER SEVERE  EXPOSURE. IF NOT BREATHING, GIVE MOUTH-TO-MOUTH
    RESUSCITATION. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:NONE
Extinguishing Media:WATER.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR FULL PROTECTIVE CLOTHING. USE FOG NOZZLE
    TO DISPERSE FUMES.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:REACTS W/METALS W/EVOLUTION OF HYDROGEN
    WHICH CAN FORM EXPLOSIVE MIXTURE W/AIR.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:DILUTE W/WATER & NEUTRALIZE BY SPRINKLING SODA
    ASH, CAUSTIC SODA OR LIME OVER SURFACE, THEN FLUSHING TO DRAIN
    W/WATER.		     R.
Neutralizing Agent:ALKALI, CAUSTIC SODA, SODA ASH, LIME

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A DRY, WELL-VENTILATED
    LOCATION AWAY FROM EXCESSIVE HEAT OR POSSIBLE CONTACT W/ALKALIES.
    PROTECT FROM MECHANICAL DAMAGE.
Other Precautions:KEEP CONTAINER CLOSED. AVOID BREATHING MIST OF FUMES.
    LOOSEN CAP CAREFULLY TO VENT BEFORE REUSE.
