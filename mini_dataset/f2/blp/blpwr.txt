=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, DRY SAND OR CO2. DON'T USE WATER.
Fire Fighting Procedures:ISOLATE FIRE/ALLOW TO BURN ITSELF OUT. DON'T
    DISTURB BURNING METAL WHEN EXTINGUISHING. WEAR SELF-CONTAINED
    BREATHING APPARATUS & PROTECTIVE CLOTHING/EQUIPMENT.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:EXPLOSION MAY OCCUR BY EXPOSING ANY
    CONCENTRATED DUST SUSPENSION IN AN ENCLOSED INDUSTRIAL AREA TO A
    SPARK/FLAME REGARDLESS OF THE CHEMICAL NATURE OF THE DUST.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:AVOID GENERATION OF AIRBORNE DUST. USE VACUUM
    SUCTION TO CLEAN SPILL. USE HIGH EFFICIENCY PARTICULATE ARRESTING
    FILTER IF VACUUM EXHAUST IF DISCHARGED. USE DUST SUPPRESSANT WHERE
    SWEEPING IS NECESSARY .

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID/CONTROL OPERATIONS WHICH CREATE
    DUSTING. KEEP IN CLOSED CONTAINERS. DON'T STORE NEAR STRONG
    OXIDIZERS. DON'T WEAR CONTACT LENSES.
