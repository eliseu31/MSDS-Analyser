=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: FLUSH THOROUGHLY W/LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER. EYES: FLUSH
    THOROUGHLY W/WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS. INHALATION: REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR. GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION IF BREATHING HAS STOPPED.
    INGESTION:	IF CONSCIOUS, GIVE WATER FREELY. OBTAINMEDICAL
    ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.
