=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER FOR 15 MINS. SKIN:
    WASH THOROUGHLY W/SOAP & WATER. INGESTION: INDUCE VOMITING. OBTAIN
    MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:241F
Extinguishing Media:FOAM WATER SPRAY (FOG), DRY CHEMICAL, CO2
Fire Fighting Procedures:WATER SHOULDN'T BE USED EXCEPT AS A FOG TO
    KEEP NEARBY CONTAINERS COOL. WEAR A SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING
    APPARATUS.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:DIKE SPILL AREA W/ABSORBENT EARTH OR SAWDUST.
    COLLECT IN METAL CONTAINER FOR DISPOSAL.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE IN A COOL DRY PLACE. AVOID
    FREEZING.
Other Precautions:AVOID INHALATION OF CONCENTRATED VAPORS. AVOID
    SPLASHING IN EYES. AVOID SKIN CONTACT W/WET OR DRY PAINT.
