=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGESTION: CALL PHYSICIAN. DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. DRINK
    LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER, EGG WHITES OR GELATIN SOLUTION. EYE:
    FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. GET PROMPT MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    SKIN: WIPE OFF A CID GENTLY. IMMEDIATELY FLOOD THE SURFACE WITH
    WATER USING SOAP FREELY. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
