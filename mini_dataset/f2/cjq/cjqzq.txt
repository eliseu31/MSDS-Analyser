=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES OR SKIN: IN CASE OF CONTACT, IMMEDIATELY FLUSH EYES OR
    SKIN WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES WHILE
    REMOVING CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND SHOES. ASSURE ADEQUATE FLUSHING
    OF THE E YES BY SEPARATING THE EYELIDS WITH FINGERS. INHAL: REMOVE
    TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BRATHING GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. IF
    BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN. INGESTION: WASH OUT MOUTH WITH
    WATER PROVID ED PERSON IS CONSCIOUS. CALL A PHYSICIAN.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WEAR NIOSH SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS,
    RUBBER BOOTS AND HEAVY RUBBER GLOVES. SWEEP UP, PLACE IN A BAG AND
    HOLD FOR WASTE DISPOSAL. AVOID RINSING DUST. VENTILATE AREA AND
    WASH SPILL SITE AFTER  MATERIAL PICKUP IS COMPLETE.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:TAKE OFF IMMEDIATELY ALL CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHING. WEAR SUITABLE PROTECTIVE CLOTHING, GLOVES AND EYE/FACE
    PROTECTION. DO NOT BREATHE DUST. VERY HYGROSCOPIC. STORE UNDER
    NITROGEN. KEEP TIGHTLY CLOSED. STO RE IN A COOL DRY PLACE.
Other Precautions:DO NOT GET IN EYES, ON SKIN, OR CLOTHING.
