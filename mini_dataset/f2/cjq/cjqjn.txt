=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH IMMEDIATELY W/PLENTY OF COOL WATER FOR 15 MINS.
    SKIN: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR. INGESTION: DON'T INDUCE VOMITING. DRINK LARGE QUANTITIES
    OF WATE R TO DILUTE THE CAUSTICS. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH IF
    UNCONSCIOUS. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:USE EXTINGUISHING MEDIA APPROPRIATE FOR SURROUNDING
    FIRE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR A SELF CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS
    W/FULL PROTECTIVE CLOTHING.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WEAR PROTECTIVE GOGGLES & GLOVES. SWEEP THE
    EXCESS POWDER UP & RETURN TO CONTAINER. FLUSH ANY RESIDUES W/PLENTY
    OF WATER FOLLOW W/A MILD HYDROCHLORIC ACID RINSE.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP CONTAINERS TIGHTLY CLOSED WHEN
    NOT IN USE. DON'T ALLOW WATER TO GET INTO CONTAINER. WHEN HANDLING,
    WEAR GOGGLES/FACE SHIELD & A LONG-SLEEVED SHIRT. KEEP OUT OF REACH
    OF CHILDREN.
Other Precautions:NEVER USE IN AN ALUMINUM CONTAINER. NEVER MIX W/ANY
    ACID/ANY OTHER CHEMICALS. READ & FOLLOW ALL LABEL DIRECTIONS &
    PRECAUTIONS CAREFULLY BEFORE USING THE PRODUCT. THESE PRODUCTS ARE
    INTENDED FOR INDUS TRIAL & INSTITUTIONAL USE ONLY. NOT FOR
    HOUSEFOLD USE/RESALE.
