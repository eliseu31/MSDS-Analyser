======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DANGER: FLAMM, LIQUEFIED GAS UNDER
    PRESS. USE PIPING & EQUIP ADEQ DESIGNED TO WITHSTAND PRESS TO BE
    ENCOUNTERED. MAY FROM EXPLO MIXTS W/AIR. GROUND ALL EQUIP. ONLY USE
    SPARK-PROOF TOOLS & EXPLO-PROOF  EQUIP. KEEP AWAY FROM HEAT, SPKS &
    OPEN FLAME. STORE & USE W/ADEQ VENT AT ALL TIMES.

    WHEN EMPTY. KEEP AWAY FROM OXIDIZING AGENTS. MIXTS: WHEN TWO/MORE

    INFO FOR EACH COMPONENT BEFORE YOU PRDCE MIXT. (SUP DAT )
