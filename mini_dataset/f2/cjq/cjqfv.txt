=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH W/WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN, LIFTING
    UPPER & LOWER LIDS OSSCASIONALLY TO FACILITATE THOROUGH WASHING. IF
    CONCENTRATED, CALL A PHYSICIAN FOR TREATMENT. SKIN: (CONCENTRATED)
    WASH  W/SOAP & WATER & WIPE CLEAN. INGEST: DRINK PLENTY OF MILK OR
    WATER. DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. IF VOMITING OCCURS, GIVE MILK OR
    WATER AGAIN. AVOID ALCOHOL. CALL A PHYSICIAN FOR TREATMENT FOR
    PROBABLE MU COSAL DAMAGE. MEASURES AGAINST CIRCULATORY SHOCK &
    BREATHING SUPPORT MAY BE NEEDED & MUST BE ADMINISTERED BY A
    QUALIFIED MED PERSON. INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. SUPPORT BREATHING
    (GIVE OXYGEN OR ARTIF ICIAL RESPIRATION) .
