=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. SKIN: WASH
    AREA WITH SOAP AND WATER. INGESTION: DRINK WATER, INDUCE VOMITING.
    INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. CALL A PHYSICIAN.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:NONE

Fire Fighting Procedures:USE NIOSH APPROVED PRESSURE DEMAND SCBA AND
    FULL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT . STORAGE CONTAINERS EXPOSED TO FIRE
    SHOULD BE KEPT COOL WITH WATER.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SPILLS SHOULD BE SOAKED UP WITH ABSORBENT.
    AREA SHOULD THEN BE FLUSHED WITH WATER. ALL RINSATE SHOULD BE
    CONTAINERIZED AND LABELED. SPILLS ON AREAS THAT ARE NOT ON PAVEMENT
    CAN BE HANDLED BY REMOVING	THE AFFECTED SOILS.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:LABEL ALL CONTAINERS. STORE CONTAINERS
    IN A COOL, DRY, WELL VENTILATED AREA. STORE AWAY FROM HEAT AND
    FLAMES.NO SMOKING IN AREA OF USE. DO NOT USE IN THE GENERAL
    VICINITY OF ARC WELDING, OPEN FLAMES O R HOT SURFACES. HEAT AND/OR
    UV RADIATION MAY CAUSE THE FORMATION OF HCL AND/OR PHOSGENE .
Other Precautions:NIA.
