=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. CALL
    PHYSICIAN. SKIN: FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER. WASH CLOTHING BEFORE
    REUSE.  INHALATION: MOVE TO FRESH AIR. PROVIDE CPR/OXYGEN IF
    NEEDED. INGESTION:	DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING, GIVE 2 GLASSES OF
    WATER. NEVER GIVE FLUID OR INDUCE VOMITING IF PATIENT IS
    UNCONSCIOUS OR HAS CONVULSIONS. CALL A PHYSICIAN.
