=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: CHECK FOR & REMOVE ANY CONT LENSES. IMMED FLUSH EYES
    W/RUNNING WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINS, KEEPING EYELIDS OPEN. COLD
    WATER MAY BE USED. DO NOT USE EYE OINTMENT. SEEK MED ATTN. SKIN: IF
    CHEM GOT  ONTO CLOTHED PORTION OF BODY, REMOVE CONTAM CLOTHES AS
    QUICKLY AS POSS, PROTECTING YOUR OWN HANDS & BODY. PLACE VICTIM
    UNDER DELUGE SHOWER. IF CHEM TOUCHES VICTIM'S EXPOSED SKIN, SUCH AS
    HANDS, NEUTR ALIZE EXPOSED SKIN W/DILUTE SOLN OF SODIUM CARBONATE.
    GENTLY & THORO WASH CONTAM SKIN W/RUNNING WATER & NON-ABRASIVE
    SOAP. BE PARTICULARLY CAREFUL TO CLEAN FOLDS, CREVICES, CREASES &
    GROIN. (SUP DAT)

    PRODUCT. IN CASE OF INSUFFICIENT VENTILATION, WEAR SUITABLE NIOSH
    APPROVED RESPIRATORY EQUIPMENT.  IF INGESTED, SEEK MEDICAL ADVICE
    IMMEDIATELY AND SHOW THE CONTAINER OR THE LABEL.
