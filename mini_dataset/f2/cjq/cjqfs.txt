=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: WASH WITH PLENTY OF WATER. SEEK MEDICAL ADVICE. EYES:
    WASH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. SEEK MEDICAL
    ADVICE. INGESTION: WASH MOUTH OUT WITH WATER AND SEEK MEDICAL
    ADVICE. INHALA TION: EXPOSE TO FRESH AIR. IF BREATHING PROBLEMS
    PERSIST, SEEK MEDICAL ADVICE.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:WATER SPRAY, CARBON DIOXIDE, DRY CHEMICAL POWDER,
    ALCOHOL OR POLYMER FOAM.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR NIOSH APPROVED SCBA AND FULL PROTECTIVE
    EQUIPMENT .
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:NOT A FLAMMABLE SOLID AS DEFINED BY
    D.O.T. REGULATIONS, SECTION 173.150.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WEAR PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT. SWEEP UP, PLACE IN
    A BAG AND HOLD FOR WASTE DISPOSAL. AVOID RAISING DUST. VENTILATE
    AREA AND WASH SPILL SITE AFTER MATERIAL PICK UP IS COMPLETE. WASH
    WITH ACETONE OR ALCOHOL , THEN WITH SOAP AND WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:POTENTIALLY HARMFUL IF INHALED OR
    INGESTED. DO NOT GET IN EYES, ON SKIN, OR ON CLOTHING. POTENTIAL
    SKIN AND EYE IRRITANT.
Other Precautions:STORE IN FREEZER AT BELOW -20C.
