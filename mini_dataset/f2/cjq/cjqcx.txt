=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE CONTACT: FLUSH THOROUGHLY WITH WATER. IF IRRITATION
    OCCURS, CALL A PHYSICIAN. SKIN CONTACT: WASH CONTACT AREAS WITH
    SOAP AND WATER. HIGH PRESSURE ACCIDENTAL INJECTION THROUGH THE SKIN
    REQUIRES IMM EDIATE MEDICAL ATTENTION FOR POSSIBLE INCISION,
    IRRIGATION AND/OR DEBRIDEMENT. INHALATION: NOT EXPECTED TO BE A
    PROBLEM. INGESTION: NOT EXPECTED TO BE A PROBLEM. HOWEVER, IF MORE
    THAN 1/2 LITER (PINT)  INGESTED, IMMEDIATELY GIVE 1 TO 2 GLASSES OF
    WATER AND CALL A PHYSICIAN, HOSPITAL EMERGENCY ROOM OR POISON
    CONTROL CENTER FOR ASSISTANCE. DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING OR GIVE
    ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCONS CIOUS PERSON.
