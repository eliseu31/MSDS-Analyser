=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: IF INHALED, REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BRTHG, GIVE
    ARTF RESP. IF BRTHG IS DFCLT, GIVE OXYGEN. CALL MD. SKIN: FLUSH
    SKIN W/WATER AFTER CONTACT. WASH CONTAMD CLTHG BEFORE REUSE. EYE:
    IN CASE OF	CONTACT, IMMED FLUSH EYES W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MINUTES. CALL MD. INGEST: IF SWALLOWED, DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING. IMMED GIVE 2 GLASSES OF WATER. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY
    MOUTH TO AN UNCON PERS ON. CALL MD. NOTES TO MD: ACTIVATED CHARCOAL
    MIXTURE MAY BE ADMINISTERED. TO PREPARE ACTIVIATED CHARCOAL
    MIXTURE, SUSPEND 50 GRAMS ACTIVATED CHARCOAL IN 400 ML WATER & MIX
    THOROUGHLY. ADMINISTER 5 ML/ KG OR 350 ML FOR AVERAGE ADULT.
