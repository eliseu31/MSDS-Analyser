=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY. IF
    SYMPTOMS ARE PRESENT, GET MED ATTN. SKIN: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH SKIN
    W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES WHILE REMOVING
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING &  SHOES. WASH CONTAMINATED CLOTHING BEFORE
    REUSE. DESTROY OR THOROUGHLY CLEAN CONTAMINATED SHOES. GET MED ATTN
    IF SYMPTOMS ARE PRESENT AFTER WASHING. EYE: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH EYES
    W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT  LEAST 15 MINUTES & GET MED ATTN. INGEST:
    IF SWALLOWED, CALL MD OR POISON CONTROL CENTER IMMEDIATELY. ONLY
    INDUCE VOMITING AT INSTRUCTIONS OF MED PERS OR POISON CONTROL
    CENTER. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY  MOUTH TO UNCON PERSON.
