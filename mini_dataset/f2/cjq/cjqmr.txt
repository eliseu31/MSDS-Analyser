First Aid:CALL PHYSICIAN IF SYMPTOMS PERSIST. EYE:FLUSH WITH WATER FOR
    15 MINUTES. HOLD EYELIDS OPEN. SKIN:WASH WITH SOAP & WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP CONTAINER SEALED TO PREVENT
    DRYING OR MOISTURE LOSS. USE IN VENTILATED AREA. STORE BELOW 49
    C(120F) AND ABOVE 0C (32F0
Other Precautions:DO NOT RAISE DUST. AVOID CONTACT WITH EYES. AVOID
    INHALATION OF DUST. WASH THOROUGHLY AFTER HANDLING MATERIAL. KEEP
    OUT OF REACH OF SMALL CHILDREN. DO NOT INGEST.
