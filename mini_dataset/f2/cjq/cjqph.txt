=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: HOLD EYELIDS APART & IMMED FLUSH W/PLENTY OF H*2O FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MIN. SEEK MED ADVICE. SKIN: REMOVE PROD & IMMED FLUSH
    AFFECTED AREA W/H*2O FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN. REMOVE CONTAMD CLTHG &
    SHOES. DES TROY CONTAMD LEATHER APPAREL. COVER AFFECTED AREA
    W/STERILE DRESSING/CLEAN SHEETING & TRANSPORT FOR MED CARE. DO NOT
    APPLY GREASES/OINTMENTS. CONTROL SHOCK, IF PRESENT. LAUNDER CONTAMD
    CLTHG PRIOR TO  REUSE. INHAL: MOVE PATIENT TO FRESH AIR. IF
    BREATHING HAS STOPPED/IS LABORED GIVE ASSISTED RESP (E.G.
    MOUTH-TO-MOUTH). PVNT ASPIR OF VOMIT. TURN VICTIM'S HEAD TO SIDE.
    SEEK MED ADVICE. (OTHER INFO)

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point:=110.C, 230.F

    OF LARGE FIRE USE: WATER SPRAY, ALCOHOL FOAM. IN CASE OF SMALL FIRE

Fire Fighting Procedures:USE NIOSH APPROVED SCBA & FULL PROTECTIVE
    EQUIPMENT . RETAIN EXPENDED LIQUIDS FROM FIRE FIGHTING FOR LATER
    DISPOSAL.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:MAY GENERATE TOXIC OR IRRITATING
    COMBUSTION PRODUCTS. CONTACT OF LIQUID WITH SKIN MUST BE PREVENTED.

    GASES. MAY GENERATE AMMONIA GAS . PERSONNEL IN VICINITY AND
    DOWNWIND SHOULD BE EVACUATED.
