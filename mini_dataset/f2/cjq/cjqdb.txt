=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. RESTORE NORMAL BREATHING.
    TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY. SEE PHYSICIAN. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY WITH
    SOAP AND WATER. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. CONSULT PHYSICIAN IF
    IRRITATIO N PERSISTS. EYES: FLUSH IMMEDIATELY WITH PLENTY OF WATER
    FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES AND GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. INGESTION: DRINK
    1 OR 2 GLASSES OF WATER TO DILUTE. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO
    AN UNCON SCIOUS PERSON. DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING (UNLESS METHANOL;
    LISTED IN SECTION 2). CONSULT PHYSICIAN OR POISON CONTROL CENTER
    IMMEDIATELY. TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY.
