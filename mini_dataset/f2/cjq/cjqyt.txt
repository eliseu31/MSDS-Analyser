=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH AT ONCE W/COOL RUNNING WATER. REMOVE CONT LENSES.
    CONTINUE FLUSHING FOR AT LST 15 MINS, HOLDING EYELIDS APART SO AS
    TO RINSE ENTIRE EYE. CALL PHYS IMMED. SKIN: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH
    W/PLENTY O F COOL RUNNING WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINS WHILE REMOVING
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING & SHOES; DISCARD OR WASH WELL BEFORE REUSE.
    IF SWALLOWED: RINSE MOUTH AT ONCE, THEN DRINK 1-2 LG GLASSES OF
    WATER. DO NOT I NDUCE VOMIT. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO UNCON
    PERSON. IF INHALED: IMMEDIATELY MOVE TO FRESH AIR. IMMED CALL MED
    EMER NUMBER, 1-800-328-0026, A POIS CTL CTR/PHYSICIAN.
