=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES:IMMED FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINS, OCCAS
    LIFTING UPPER & LOWER LIDS. GET MD IMMED. DO NOT ALLOW VICTIM TO
    RUB/KEEP EYES CLSD. SKIN:FLUSH W/PLENTY OF SOAP & WATER FOR AT LST
    15 MINS  WHILE REMOVING CONTAM CLTHG & SHOES. GET MED AID IF IRRIT
    DEVELS/PERSISTS. WASH CLTHG BEFORE REUSE. INGEST:DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMIT. IF VICTIM IS CONSCIOUS & ALERT, GIVE 2-4 CUPFULS OF
    MILK/WATER. NEVER G IVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO UNCON PERS. GET MD
    IMMED. CALL POIS CTL CTR. INHAL:REMOVE FROM EXPOS TO FRESH AIR
    IMMED. IF NOT BRTHG, GIVE ARTF RESP. IF BRTHG IS DFCLT, GIVE OXYG.
    GET MED AID. NOTES TO MD:  TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY & SUPPORTIVELY.
