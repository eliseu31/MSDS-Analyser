=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES-IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MINUTES, LIFTING LIDS. CALL A PHYSICIAN AT ONCE.
    SKIN-IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. CALL A PHYSICIAN.
    REMOVE CONTAMINA TED CLOTHES IMMEDIATELY & LAUNDER BEFORE REUSE.
    INGESTED-IMMEDIATELY DRINK A LARGE QUANTITY OF WATER. DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING! CALL A PHYSICIAN AT ONCE. DO NOT GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH
    IF PERSON IS UNCON SCIOUS OR HAVING CONVULSIONS. INHALED-REMOVE
    VICTIM TO FRESH AIR. SUPPORT RESPIRATION IF NEEDED. CALL A
    PHYSICIAN.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP TIGHTLY SEALED. STORE IN A COOL,
    DRY, WELL-VENTILATED AREA. DO NOT STORE AT TEMPERATURES ABOVE
    52C/125F AND LOW HUMIDITY. DO NOT TAKE INTERNALLY. AVOID CONTACT
    WITH EYES, SKIN OR CLOTHING. UPON C ONTACT WITH SKIN OR EYES, WASH
