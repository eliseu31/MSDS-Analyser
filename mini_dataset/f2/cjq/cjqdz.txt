=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH IMMEDIATELY WITH LARGE AMOUNT OF WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MINUTES. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY. SKIN: REMOVE
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING IMMEDIATELY. WASH WITH LARGE AMOUNT OF WATER.
    GET MEDI CAL ATTENTION IF NEEDED. INHALATION: WHEN SAFE TO ENTER
    AREA, REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE. KEEP WARM AND AT REST. INGESTION: TRY
    TO GET THE VICTIM TO VOMIT. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:REGULAR DRY CHEMICAL, CARBON DIOXIDE, WATER,
    REGULAR FOAM. LARGE FIRES: USE REGULAR FOAM OR FLOOD WITH FINE
    WATER SPRAY.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR NIOSH APPROVED SCBA AND FULL PROTECTIVE
    EQUIPMENT . SHUT OFF FUEL TO FIRE IF POSSIBLE TO DO SO WITHOUT
    HAZARD.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:SLIGHT FIRE HAZARD. DUST-AIR MIXTURES MAY
    IGNITE OR EXPLODE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:AVOID RAISING DUST AND CAREFULLY SWEEP UP.
    SHUT OFF ALL SOURCES OF IGNITION. IN CASE OF SPILL, SWEEP UP
    POLYMER AND DISPOSE OF IN AS RECOMMENDED IN WASTE DISPOSAL METHOD
    SECTION.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:WEAR PROPER PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT IN
    ORDER TO AVOID CONTACT WITH SKIN AND EYES. PREVENT BUILD-UP OF
    ELECTROSTATIC CHARGES (E.G, BY GROUNDING). SEPARATE FROM OXIDIZING
    MATERIALS. KEEP AWAY FROM HEAT OR  SUNLIGHT, AND STORE IN A COOL
    DRY LOCATION.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:TO PREVENT ANY CONTACT, WEAR NIOSH APPROVED DUST
    RESPIRATOR, NIOSH APPROVED SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS.
Ventilation:USE WITH LOCAL EXHAUST VENTILATION.
Protective Gloves:WEAR IMPERVIOUS GLOVES .
Eye Protection:ANSI APPROVED CHEMICAL WORKERS GOGGLES .
Other Protective Equipment:EYEWASH AND DELUGE SHOWER MEETING ANSI
    DESIGN CRITERIA . TO PREVENT ANY CONTACT, WEAR PROTECTIVE CLOTHING.
Work Hygienic Practices:WASH HANDS AND FACE AFTER USING MATERIALS.
