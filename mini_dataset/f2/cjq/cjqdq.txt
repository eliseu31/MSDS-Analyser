    LEAST 15 MINUTES. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP AND WATER.
    INGESTION: S EEK MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY. SEEK MEDICAL
    ATTENTION FOR ALL CASES OF OVEREXPOSURE.
