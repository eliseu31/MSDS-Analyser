=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES AND CALL PHYSICIAN. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY WITH PLENTY OF
    WATER AND SOAP. LAUNDER CONTAMINATED CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE.
    INHALATION: IF  AFFECTED, REMOVE TO FRESH AIR, ADMINISTER OXYGEN
    AND CALL PHYSICIAN. INGESTION: GIVE 1-2 GLASSES OF WATER. SEEK
    PHYSICIAN IMMEDIATELY. SHOW MSDS OR LABEL. DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.
    ASPIRATION CAUSES SE VERE LUNG IRRITATION, COUGHING AND PULMONARY
    EDEMA.
