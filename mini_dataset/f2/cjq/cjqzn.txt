=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING, GIVE ARTIFICIAL
    RESPIRATION. IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN. GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IMMED. INGEST: IF SWALLOWED, DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE
    LARGE  QUANTITIES OF WATER. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN
    UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMED. SKIN: IMMED FLUSH
    W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES WHILE REMOVING
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHI NG & SHOES. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMED. WASH
    CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE. THOROUGHLY CLEAN SHOES BEFORE REUSE. EYES:
    IMMED FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES, LIFTING
    LOWER & UPPER EYELIDS OCC ASIONALLY. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMED.
