=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:MERCURY: HIGH CONCENTRATION COULD
    RESULT IN PNEUMONITIS. POSSIBLE TREMORS & NEUROPSYCHIATRIC
    PROBLEMS. PHOSPHOR DUST: POSSIBLE IRRITATION.
Effects of Overexposure:CHEST PAIN, TREMORS, NEUROPSYCHIATRIC PROBLEMS,
    SHORTNESS OF BREATH, COUGHING, IRRITATION, NAUSEA
Medical Cond Aggravated by Exposure:PERSONS W/RESPIRATORY PROBLEMS.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/WATER FOR 15 MINS. SKIN: WASH W/SOAP & WATER.
    INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR/RESTORE/SUPPORT BREATHING. OBTAIN
    MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL CASES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:USE EXTINGUISHING AGENTS SUITABLE FOR SURROUNDING
    FIRE.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE A SELF CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS TO
    PREVENT INHALATION OF DUST & FUMES THAT MAY BE GENERATED DURING
    FIRE FIGHTING.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:WHEN EXPOSED TO HIGH TEMPERATURE MERCURY
    VAPORIZES TO FORM TOXIC FUMES.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:VENTILATE AREA. CLEAN UP W/VACUUM/OTHER
    NON-DUST GENERATING PROCEDURES. USE USUAL PRECAUTIONS FOR
    COLLECTION OF BROKEN GLASS. DON'T DISCHARGE MERCURY DOWN THE DRAIN.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:HANDLE LAMPS CAREFULLY TO AVOID
    BREAKAGE.
Other Precautions:LAMPS MADE W/UPI COATINGS PRODUCES ULTRAVIOLET ENERGY
    IN THE WAVELENGTHS 391-449 NANOMETERS WHEN LAMPS ARE LIT. USE
    APPROPRIATE SAFETY PRECAUTIONS TO PREVENT EXPOSURE TO EYES & SKIN
    FROM ULTRAVIOLET E NERGY.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============

Respiratory Protection:USE APPROPRIATE NIOSH APPROVED RESPIRATOR IF
    DUST CONCENTRATIONS EXCEED TLV.
Ventilation:ADEQUATE LOCAL EXHAUST TO MAINTAIN PEL/TLV.
Protective Gloves:CLOTH GLOVES FOR PROLONGED CONTACT.
Eye Protection:SAFETY GOGGLES WHEN BREAKING LAMPS.
Work Hygienic Practices:WASH THOROUGHLY AFTER HANLDING & BEFORE
    EATING/SMOKING.
