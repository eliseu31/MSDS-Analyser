=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF H*2O FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN,
    OCCASIONALLY LIFTING UPPER & LOWER EYELIDS. GET MED AID IMMED.
    SKIN: FLUSH W/PLENTY OF SOAP & H*2O FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN WHILE
    REMOVING CONTAMD CLOTHING  & SHOES. GET MED AID IF IRRIT
    DEVELOPS/PERSISTS. INGEST: IF VICTIM IS CONSCIOUS & ALERT, GIVE 2-4
    CUPFULS OF MILK OR WATER. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCON
    PERSON. GET MED AID IMMED. INHAL:  GET MEDICAL AID IMMEDIATELY.
    REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE TO FRESH AIR IMMEDIATELY. IF NOT BREATHING,
    GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. IF BREATHING IS DIFFICUT, GIVE OXYGEN.
    NOTES TO MD: TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY AND  SUPPORTIVELY.
