=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: GENTLY LIFT EYELIDS & FLUSH CONTINUOUSLY W/WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MINUTES. IF IRRITATION DEVELOPS, GET MEDICAL AID. SKIN:
    GET MEDICAL AID IF IRRITATION DEVELOPS/PERSISTS. FLUSH SKIN
    W/PLENTY OF S OAP & WATER. INGESTION: IF VICTIM IS CONSCIOUS &
    ALERT, GIVE 2-4 CUPFULS OF MILK/WATER. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH
    TO AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. GET MEDICAL AID IF IRRITATION/SYMPTOMS
    OCCUR. INHALATION:	REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE TO FRESH AIR IMMEDIATELY.
    IF NOT BREATHING, GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. IF BREATHING IS
    DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN. GET MEDICAL AID IF COUGH/OTHER SYMPTOMS
    APPEAR. NOTES TO PHYSICIA N: TREAT SYMPTOMATICALLY & SUPPORTIVELY.
