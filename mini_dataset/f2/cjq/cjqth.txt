=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:EYES-CAUSES SEVERE IRRITATION,
    EXPERIENCED AS DISCOMFORT OR PAIN, EXCESSIVE BLINKING & TEAR
    PEODUCTION, MARKED BY EXCESS REDNESS & SWELLING OF THE CONJUCTIVA,
    & CHEMICAL BURNS OF THE EYE.  SKIN-SUBSTA NCE MAY CAUSE SKIN
    IRRITATION. PROLONGED CONTACT WILL CAUSE CAUSTIC BURN ON SKIN. 
    INGESTION-MAY CAUSE ABDOMINAL DISCOMFORT, NAUSEA, VOMITING AND
    DIARRHEA. INHALATION-THIS FORMULA DOES NOT EMIT ANY HA RMFUL
    VAPORS.
Explanation of Carcinogenicity:NOT CARCOGENIC PER PSHA, NTP, IARC FOR
    LISTED INGREDIENTS.
Effects of Overexposure:EYES: BURNING AND OR ITCHING OF EYE. SKIN:
    REDNESS OF THE SKIN AND IN SEVERE CASES BLISTERS.  INGESTION: BURNS
    OF TISSUES OF MOUTH, THROAT AND STOMACH.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES-IMMEDIATELY FLUSH EYES WITH PLENTY OF WATER & CONTINUE
    WASHING FOR 15 MINS. DO NOT REMOVE CONTACT LENSES, IF WORN. OBTAIN
    MEDICAL ATTENTION WITHOUT DELAY, PREFERABLY FROM AN OPTHAMOLOGIST.
    SKIN-F OR BURNS, TREAT ACCORDING TO SEVERITY. FOR IRRITATION, WASH
    THOROUGHLY, SEEK MEDICAL ADVICE FOR PROPER SKIN CREAM. INGESTION-IF
    SWALLOWED, DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. CALL A PHYSICIAN OR POISON
    CONTROL CE NTER IMMEDIATELY. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN
    UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. PERFORM MOUTH-TO-MOUTH RESUSITATION IF VICTIM
    IS NOT BREATHING.
