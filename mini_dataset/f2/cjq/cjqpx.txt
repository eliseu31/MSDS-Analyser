=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:ACUTE: MAY BE HARMFUL BY INHALATION,
    INGESTION OR SKIN ABSORPTION. MATERIAL IS EXTREMELY DESTRUCTIVE TO
    TISSUE OF MUCOUS MEMBRANES AND UPPER RESPIRATORY TRACT, EYES AND
    SKIN. INHALATION MAY RESULT IN  SPASM, INFLAMMATION AND EDEMA OF
    THE LARYNX AND BRONCHI, CHEMICAL PNEUMONITIS AND PULMONARY EDEMA.
    SYMPTOMS OF EXPOSURE MAY INCLUDE BURNING SENSATION, COUGHING,
    WHEEZING, LARYNGITIS, SHORTNESS OF BREA TH, HEADACHE, NAUSEA AND
    VOMITING.
Effects of Overexposure:SEE HEALTH HAZARDS.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:IN CASE OF EXPOS, OBTAIN MED ATTN IMMED. SKIN: WIPE OFF
    EXCESS MATL W/DRY CLOTH. WASH REMAINING MATL OFF W/COPIOUS AMTS OF
    WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINS. REMOVE CLTHG & SHOES PRIOR TO SHOWERING.
    WASH CONTA M CLTHG BEFORE REUSE. DISCARD CONTAM SHOES. EYES: FLUSH
    W/COPIOUS AMTS OF WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINS. ASSURE ADEQ FLUSHING BY
    SEPARATING EYELIDS W/FINGERS. CALL MD. APPLY COOL PACKS ON EYES
    WHILE TRANSP ORTING PATIENT TO MED FACILITY. INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH
    AIR. IF NOT BRTHG GIVE ARTF RESP. IF BRTHG IS DFCLT, GIVE OXYG.
    CALL MD. INGEST: WASH OUT MOUTH W/WATER PROVIDED PERSON IS
    CONSCIOUS. CALL MD. DO  NOT INDUCE VOMITING.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:EVACUATE AREA. WEAR NIOSH APPROVED
    SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS, RUBBER BOOTS AND HEAVY RUBBER
    GLOVES. SWEEP UP, PLACE IN A BAG AND HOLD FOR WASTE DISPOSAL. AVOID
    RAISING DUST. VENTILATE AREA AN D WASH SPILL SITE AFTER MATERIAL
    PICKUP IS COMPLETE.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:DO NOT BREATHE DUST. DO NOT GET IN
    EYES, ON SKIN, ON CLOTHING. AVOID PROLONGED OR REPEATED EXPOSURE.
    KEEP TIGHTLY CLOSED. STORE IN A COOL, DRY PLACE.
Other Precautions:CORROSIVE. CAUSES BURNS.MOISTURE SENSITIVE. HANDLE
    AND STORE UNDER NITROGEN.
