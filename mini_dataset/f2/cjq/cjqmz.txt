=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: WASH CONTACT AREAS WITH WATER FOR 15 MINUTES.
    INGESTION: DRINK MILK, WATER, FRUIT JUICE. INDUCE VOMITING WITH OIL
    OF IPECAC. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. EYES: FLUSH WITH POTABLE WATER
    FOR AT LEAST 15  MINUTES. SEE MD . INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR.
    SUPPORT BREATHING (GIVE OXYGEN OR ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION) .

===================  Toxicological Information	===================

Toxicological Information:POISON. CORROSIVE. MAY BE FATAL IF SWALLOWED.
    MAY CAUSE SEVERE AND PERMANENT EYE DAMAGE, INCLUDING BLINDNESS.
    HARMFUL IF INHALED. MAY CAUSE ARGYRIA, A BLUE-GREY DISCOLORATION OF
    THE SKIN, EYES, AND MU COUS MEMBRANES. CAN PRODUCE METHEMOGLOBIN
    WHICH, IN SUFFICIENT CONCENTRATION, CAUSES CYANOSIS. MAY CAUSE
    EMPHYSEMA AND ARTERIOSCLEROSIS. MAY CAUSE KIDNEY AND LIVER DAMAGE.
