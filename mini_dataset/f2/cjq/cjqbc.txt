=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Routes of Entry: Inhalation:YES  Skin:YES  Ingestion:YES
Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:POTASSIUM HYDROXIDE IS CORROSIVE TO
    SKIN AND EYES. LEAD, CADMIUM AND SILVER BIOACCUMULATE IN HUMAN
    TISSUES. CADMIUM OXIDE IS A SUSPECTED HUMAN CARCINOGEN. LEAD AND
    CADMIUM APPEAR ON THE NAVY LIST OF O CCUPATIONAL CHEMICAL
    REPRODUCTIVE HAZARDS. SEEK CONSULTATION FROM APPROPRIATE HEALTH
    PROFESSIONALS CONCERNING LATEST HAZARD LIST INFO AND SAFE HANDLING
    AND EXPOSURE INFO .
Effects of Overexposure:TO POTASSIUM HYDROXIDE: SKIN AND EYE
    IRRITATION. TO SILVER: AGRYIA. TO CADMIUM OXIDE: DRYNESS OF THROAT,
    HEADACHE, COUGH, SHORTNESS OF BREATH, VOMITING. TO LEAD OXIDE:
    ABDOMINAL PAIN, NAUSEA, VOMITING , METALLIC TASTE IN MOUTH.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGEST: CALL MD IMMEDIATELY . EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH EYES
    WITH POTABLE WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. SEEK ASSISTANCE FROM MD
    . SKIN: IN RESPONSE TO KOH - FLUSH AFFECTED AREA WITH COPIOUS 
    AMOUNTS OF WATER. SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY. INHALATION:
    IN RESPONSE TO METAL FUMES, DUSTS - REMOVE VICTOM FROM AREA. SEEK
    MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL EXTINGUISHER.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE NIOSH APPROVED SCBA AND FULL PROTECTIVE
    EQUIPMENT . AVOID BREATHING FUMES.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:ACRID SMOKE FROM BURNING PLASTIC
    COMPONENTS.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:CONTAINS POTASSIUM HYDROXIDE; NEUTRALIZE WITH
    BORIC ACID. DO NOT FLUSH TO SANITARY SEWER. COLLECT ALL SPILLAGE
    AND DEBRIS FOR PROPER DISPOSAL. USE NIOSH APPROVED FULL FACE
    RESPIRATOR WITH HEPA FILTERS  OR A PAPR WITH HEPA FILTERS. DO NOT
    CREATE ANY DUST. DO NOT SWEEP UP THE DEBRIS.
Neutralizing Agent:NEUTRALIZE WITH BORIC ACID.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID SHORT CIRCUITING THE TERMINALS.
    AVOID EXPOSURE TO INTENSE HEAT OR FIRE.
Other Precautions:DO NOT OPEN THESE CELLS AND EXPOSE THE INTERNAL
    COMPONENTS.
