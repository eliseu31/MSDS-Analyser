=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. DO NOT RUB.
    CONSULT PHYSICIAN IF IRRITATION PERSISTS. SKIN: WASH WITH MILD SOAP
    AND WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. INGESTION: INDUCE
    VOMITING.  CONSULT PHYSICIAN.
