=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. GET
    MED ATTN IF IRRITATION OCCURS. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP &
    WATER. OBTAIN IMMEDIATE MED ATTN IF GREASE IS INJECTED UNDER SKIN.
    INGEST:  DO NOT INDUCE VOMIT. IMMED GIVE 2 GLASSES OF CHARCOAL
    SLURRY OR PLAIN WATER. DO NOT ATTEMPT TO GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO
    UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. CONT A PHYSICIAN. INHAL: REMOVE FROM EXPOSURE
    AREA TO FRES H AIR IMMEDIATELY. ADMINISTER OXYGEN IF DEEMED
    NECESSARY. POLYMER FUME FEVER SYMPTOMS SHOULD SUBSIDE WITHIN 24
    HOURS.
