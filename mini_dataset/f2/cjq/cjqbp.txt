Effects of Overexposure:EYES: REDNESS, TEARING, BLURRED VISION. 
    INHALATION: SYMPTOMS OF NERVOUS SYSTEM DEPRESSION.	INGESTION:

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MIN,
    LIFTING EYELIDS TO INSURE COMPLETE FLUSHING OF SURFACE.  GET
    MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING. WASH SKIN
    AREA WITH SOA P AND WATER. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IF SYMPTOMS
    DEVELOP AND PERSIST. INHALATION: MOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT
    BREATHING,GIVE CPR. IF BREATHING DIFFICULT GIVE OXYGEN. GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.  IN GESTION: GET  IMMEDIATE MEDICAL
    ATTENTION AND DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:COC
Flash Point:=118.3C, 245.F
Autoignition Temp:=343.3C, 650.F
Lower Limits:3.2
Extinguishing Media:NFPA CLASS B EXTINGUISHERS (CARBON DIOXIDE OR FOAM)
    FOR CLASS IIIB FIRES.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WATERSPRAY MAY BE INEFFECTIVE ON FIRE BUT CAN
    PROTECT FIRE-FIGHTERS AND COOL CLOSED CONTAINERS. USE FOG NOZZLES
    IF WATER IS USED.DON'T ENTER CONFINED FIRE-SPACE WITHOUT FULL
    BUNKER GEAR. HELMET WITH F ACE SHIELD, BUNKER COATS, GLOVES &
    BOOTS. USE NIOSH APPROVED POSITIVE PRESSURE SELF CONTAINED
    BREATHING APPARATUS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:SLIGHTLY COMBUSTIBLE.  KEEP CONTAINERS
    TIGHTLY CLOSED. ISOLATE FROM OXIDIZERS, HEAT AND OPEN FLAME. CLOSED
    CONTAINERS MAY EXPLODE IF EXPOSED TO EXTREME HEAT.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:STOP SPILL AT SOURCE. DIKE AREA AND CONTAIN
    SPILL.  CLEAN UP REMAINDER WITH ABSORBENT MATERIALS. MOP UP AND
    DISPOSE OF PROPERTY.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:STORE AWAY FROM OXIDIZERS, HEAT, OPEN
    FLAMES. USE WITH ADEQUATE VENTILATION. AVOID BREATHING VAPOR/MIST.
    DO NOT FLAME CUT, BRAZE, OR WELD CONTAINERS. STORE IN A COOL
    VENTILATED AREA.
Other Precautions:DO NOT STORE ABOVE 120F. STORE LARGE AMOUNTS IN
    STRUCTURES MADE FOR OSHA CLASS IIIB LIQUIDS. KEEP CONTAINERS
    TIGHTLY CLOSED AND UPRIGHT WHEN NOT IN USE TO PREVENT LEAKAGE.
    AVOID FREE FALL OF LIQUID. G ROUND CONTAINERS WHEN TRANSFERRING.
