=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH WITH COOL WATER FOR 15 MIN. IF IRRITATION
    PERSISTS, SEE PHYSICIAN.  INGESTION: GIVE TWO LARGE GLASSES OF
    WATER. DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. CALL PHYSICIAN. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING
    BY MOUTH TO AN U NCONSCIOUS PERSON.

Spill Release Procedures:PICK UP WITH MOP, WET/DRY VAC OR ABSORBENT

    ALLOWING TRAFFIC.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN. KEEP
    CONTAINER CLOSED WHEN NOT IN USE. IF CONCENTRATED OR DILUTED
    PRODUCT IS ALLOWED TO DRY ON GLASS, ETCHING MAY OCCUR. STORE AT
    ROOM TEMPERATURE. MAY DAMAGE SOME PLAST ICS.
