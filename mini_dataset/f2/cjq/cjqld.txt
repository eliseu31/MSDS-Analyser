==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SWEEP UP OR VACUUM SPILLED TONER AND CAREFULLY
    TRANSFER INTO SEALABLE WASTE CONTAINER. SWEEP SLOWLY TO MINIMIZE
    GENERATION OF DUST DURING CLEAN-UP. IF A VACUUM IS USED, THE MOTOR
    MUST BE RATED AS DUST  TIGHT. A CONDUCTIVE HOSE BONDED TO THE
    MACHINE SHOULD BE USED TO REDUCE STATIC BUILDUP (SEE SECTION 5).
    RESIDUE CAN BE REMOVED WITH SOAP AND COLD WATER.
