=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGESTION: IF CONSCIOUS GIVE TWO GLASSES OF WATER & INDUCE
    VOMITING, CALL A PHYSICIAN IMMMEDIATELY.  SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHING & FLUSH SKIN WITH WATER.  INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR,
    CALL  A PHYSICIAN IF DISCIOMFORT PERSIST.  EYE: FLUSH WITH WATER
    IMMEDIATELY & CONTINUE WASHING THE EYES FOR SEVERAL MINUTES.
