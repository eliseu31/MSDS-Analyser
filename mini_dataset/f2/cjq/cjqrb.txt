=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES.
    SKIN: WASH OFF WITH SOAP AND WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO THE FRESH
    AIR. INGESTION: GIVE TEASPOON OF DIBASIC SODIUM PHOSPHATE IN WATER,
    THEN  A TEASPOON OF SALT IN A GLASS OF WARM WATER AND REPEAT UNTIL
    VOMIT IS CLEAR. SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID CONTACT WITH SKIN, EYES AND
    CLOTHING. AVOID BREATHING DUST. USE NORMAL PERSONAL HYGIENE AND
    HOUSEKEEPING. STORE IN COOL DRY AREA AWAY FROM OTHER INCOMPATIBLE
    MATERIALS. PRODUCT IS SLIGHTLY HYDRO SCOPIC AND SHOULD BE STORED N
    A DRY AREA TO PREVENT MOISTURE PICK UP AND CAKING.
