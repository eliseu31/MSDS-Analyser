=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH IMMEDIATELY WITH WATER. IF IRRITATION PERSISTS,
    GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. INGESTION: IF SWALLOWED IN QUANTITY, GIVE 1
    TO 2 GLASSES OF WATER, AND INDUCE VOMITING. CALL MD. SKIN: WASH
    WITH PLEN TY OF SOAP AND HOT WATER. INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH
    AIR. SUPPORT BREATHING (GIVE O*2/ARTF RESP) .

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:=21.1C, 70.F
Lower Limits:2%
Upper Limits:12%
Extinguishing Media:DRY CHEMICAL, CO*2, OR ALCOHOL FOAM, (WATER MAY BE
    INEFFECTIVE).
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE NIOSH APPROVED SCBA AND FULL PROTECTIVE
    EQUIPMENT . DO NOT ALLOW RUNOFF TO ENTER WATERWAYS INHABITED BY
    AQUATIC ORGANISMS.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:NONE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:DO NOT ALLOW SPILL TO ENTER WATERWAYS
    INHABITED BY AQUATIC ORGANISMS. SOAK UP WITH ABSORBENT MATERIAL AND
    PLACE IN CONTAINER FOR DISPOSAL.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID CONTACT WITH SKIN AND EYES.
    AVOID INHALATION OF VAPORS. DO NOT USE OR STORE NEAR HEAT OR OPEN
    FLAME. STORE AWAY FROM CHILDREN.

=============  Exposure Controls/Personal Protection  =============
