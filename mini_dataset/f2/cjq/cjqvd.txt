=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:GET MEDICAL ASSISTANCE FOR ALL CASES OF OVEREXPOSURE. SKIN:
    IMMEDIATELY WASH THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP AND WATER. EYES: IMMEDIATELY
    FLUSH THOROUGHLY WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. INHALATON:
    REMOVE TO  FRESH AIR; GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION IF BREATHING HAS
    STOPPED. INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING; IF CONSCIOUS, GIVE
    WATER FREELY AND GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
