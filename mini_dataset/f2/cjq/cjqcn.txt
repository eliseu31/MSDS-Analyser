=====================  Hazards Identification  =====================

Reports of Carcinogenicity:NTP:NO    IARC:NO	OSHA:NO
Health Hazards Acute and Chronic:SKIN CONTACT-REPEATED OR PROLONGED
    SKIN CONTACT MAY CAUSE IRRITATION. EYE CONTACT-DIRECT CONTACT WITH
    PRODUCT MAY CAUSE IRRITATION. INHALATION-IRRITATION OF MIST MAY
    IRRITATE RESPIRATORY PASSAGES. ING ESTION-MAY BE HARMFUL IF
    SWALLOWED.

=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN CONTACT-WASH AFFECTED AREA THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP & WATER.
    IF IRRITATION OCCURS, SEE A PHYSICIAN IMMEDIATELY. REMOVE ALL
    CONTAMINATED CLOTHING & LAUNDER BEFORE REUSE. EYE CONTACT-FLUSH
    WITH LARGE A MOUNTS OF WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. IF IRRITATION OCCURS,
    SEE A  PHYSICIAN IMMEDIATELY. INHALATION-REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF
    UNCONSCIOUS ADMINISTER ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATON. CALL A PHYSICIAN
    IMMEDIATELY. GIVE  OXYGEN IF NEEDED.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Autoignition Temp:=401.C, 753.8F
Extinguishing Media:FOAM, CARBON MONOXIDE, DRY CHEMICAL.
Fire Fighting Procedures:FIREFIGHTERS SHOULD WEAR PROTECTIVE GEAR
    INCLUDING SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING APPARATUS.USE EXTINGUISHING
    MEDIA APPROPRIATE FOR SURROUNDING FIRE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:NOTIFY EMERGENCY RESPONSE PERSONNEL. EVACUATE
    AREA AND REMOVE IGNITION SOURCE. BUILD DIKE TO CONTAIN SPILL.
    REMOVE FREE LIQUID. DO NOT FLUSH INTO SEWER OR OPEN WATER STREAM.
    PICKUP WITH INERT ABSORBEN T AND PLACE IN CLOSED CONTAINER FOR
    DISPOSAL.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

    WHILE USING PRODUCT.
