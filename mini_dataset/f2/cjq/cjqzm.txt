=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING, GIVE
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN. GET
    MEDICAL ATTENTION. INGESTION: INDUCE VOMITING IMMEDIATELY AS
    DIRECTED BY MED ICAL PERSONNEL. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN
    UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN: WIPE OFF EXCESS
    MATERIAL FROM SKIN THEN IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR
    AT LEAST 15 MINUT ES. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING & SHOES. GET
    MEDICAL ATTENTION. WASH CLOTHING BEFORE REUSE. THOROUGHLY CLEAN
    SHOES BEFORE REUSE. EYES: IMMED FLUSH W/PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LST
    15 MINS, LIFTING LOWER  & UPPER LIDS OCCAS. GET MED ATTN IMMED.
