=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHAL: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. SUPPORT BRTHG  (GIVE OXYG/ARTF
    RESP) . EYES: IF SYMPS DEVELOP, MOVE TO FRESH AIR. FLUSH GENTLY
    W/WATER WHILE HOLDING EYELIDS APART FOR AT LST 15 MINS. IF SYMPS
    PERSIS T/THERE IS ANY VISUAL DFCLTY, SEEK MED ATTN. SKIN: REMOVE
    CONTAMD CLTHG. WASH EXPOSED AREA W/SOAP & WATER. IF SYMPS PERSIST,
    SEEK MED ATTN. LAUNDER CLTHG BEFORE REUSE. INGEST: SEEK MED ATTN.
    IF INDIVI DUAL IS DROWSY/UNCON, DO NOT GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH;
    PLACE INDIVIDUAL ON LEFT SIDE W/HEAD DOWN. CONT PHYS, MED
    FACILITY/POIS CTL CTR FOR ADVICE ABOUT WHETHER TO INDUCE VOMIT.
    (SUDPAT)
