    BRTHG IS DFCLT, GIVE OXYG. CALL PHYS IMMED. INGEST: DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMIT. GIVE LG QTYS OF WATER. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO UNCON
    PERSO N. GET MED ATTN IMMED. SKIN CONT: IMMED FLUSH W/PLENTY OF
    WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINS WHILE REMOVING CONTAMD CLTHG & SHOES. CALL
    PHYS IMMED. WASH CLTHG BEFORE REUSE. EYE CONT: IMMED FLUSH W/GENTLE
    BUT LG  STREAM OF WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINS, LIFTING LOWER & UPPER
    EYELIDS OCCASIONALLY. CALL PHYS IMMED.
