=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING GIVE
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION, PEFERABLY MOUTH-TO-MOUTH. IF BREATHING IS
    DIFFICULT GIVE OXYGEN. GET MEDICAL ASSISTANCE. EYE CONT: FLUSH EYES
    IMMEDIATELY  WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LST 15 MINUTES. GET
    MEDICAL ASSISTANCE. SKIN CONT: IMMEDIATELY WASH SKIN WITH SOAP &
    PLENTY OF WATER. SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION IF IRRITATION DEVELOPS OR
    PERSISTS. INGESTION  (SWALLOWING): RINSE MOUTH OUT WITH WATER. GET
    IMMEDIATE MEDICAL ATTENTION.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:WATER SPRAY, CARBON DIOXIDE, DRY CHEMICAL, ALCOHOL
    OR POLYMER FOAM.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR NIOSH APPROVED SCBA AND FULL PROTECTIVE
    EQUIPMENT .
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:WHEN HEATED TO DECOMPOSITION, EMITS TOXIC
    FUMES OF OXIDES OF NITROGEN (NO*X) AND SULFUR (SO*X). AS WITH ANY
    OTHER ORGANIC MATERIAL, COMBUSTION WILL PRODUCE CARBON DIOXIDE AND
    CARBON MONOXIDE.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WEARING NIOSH APPRVD RESP, HEAVY RUBB GLOVES,
    CHEM SFTY GOGGS & RUBB BOOTS, DAMPEN DRY MATLS TO AVOID CREATING
    DUST, SWEEP MATERIAL ONTO PAPER, PLACE IN BAG & HOLD FOR WASTE
    DISPOSAL. VENTILATE AREA A ND WASH SPILL AREA THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP
    AND WATER AFTER MATERIAL PICK-UP IS COMPLETE.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP IN TIGHTLY CLOSED CONTAINER, IN A
    COOL, DRY, WELL VENTILATED AREA. HYGROSCOPIC. AVOID MOISTURE.
Other Precautions:CAUTION. MAY BE HARMFUL BY INHALATION, INGESTION, OR
    SKIN ABSORPTION. MAY CAUSE IRRITATION. AVOID BREATHING DUST. AVOID
    SKIN OR EYE CONTACT. DO NOT TAKE INTERNALLY.
