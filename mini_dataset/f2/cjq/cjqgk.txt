=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE: FLUSH WITH LUKEWARM WATER FOR 15 MINUTES. CONTACT A
    PHYSICIAN. SKIN: DO NOT REMOVE WITH SOLVENT. USE WATERLESS	HAND
    CLEANER FOLLOWED BY SOAP AND WATER. REMOVE AND CLEAN CONTAMINATED
    CLOTHING. IN HALE: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. RESTORE BREATHING IF
    NECESSARY. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. INGEST: RINSE MOUTH. CALL A
    PHYSICIAN OR POISON CONTROL CENTER IMMEDIATELY. DO NOT INDUCE
    VOMITING UNLESS ADVISED BY A  PHYSICIAN.
