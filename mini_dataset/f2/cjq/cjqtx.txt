First Aid:EYE CONTACT: FLUSH WITH LOTS OF CLEAN WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES. IF IRRITATION OF REDNESS DEVELOPS AND PERSISTS, SEEK
    MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN CONTACT: WASH AFFECTED AREAS THOROUGHLY
    WITH MILD SOAP  AND WATER. INHALATION: MOVE VICTIM INTO FRESH AIR.
    INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. GIVE LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER.
    (IF AVAILABLE, GIVE SEVERAL GLASSS OF MILK.)
