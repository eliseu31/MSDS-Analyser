=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE CONTACT: IMMEDIATELY WASH/FLUSH EYES WITH WATER FOR
    MINUMUM 10 MINUTES. SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN CONTACT: WASH OFF
    IN FLOWING WATER OR SHOWER. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND SHOES.
    SEEK MEDI CAL ATTENTION, IF IRRITATION DEVELOPS. INHALATION: MOVE
    TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING, GIVE MOUTH TO MOUTH RESUSCITATION.
    IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN. SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    INGESTION: IF  SWALLOWED, DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. SEEK PROMPT
    MEDICAL ATTENTION. NOTE TO PHYSICIAN: TREATMENT SHOULD BE DIRECTED
    AT THE CONTROL OF SYMPTOMS AND THE CLINICAL CONDITIONS. THERE ARE
    NO SPECIFIC ANTIDOT ES.
