=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH AT ONCE W/COOL RUNNING H*2O. REMOVE CONTACT
    LENSES. HOLDING EYELIDS APART, CONTINUE FLUSHING FOR AT LEAST 15
    MIN. SKIN: FLUSH SKIN W/PLENTY OF COOL RUNNING WATER. WASH
    THOROUGHLY W/SOAP &  H*2O. WASH CONTAMD CLOTHING WELL BEFORE REUSE.
    INGEST: RINSE MOUTH; THEN DRINK 1 OR 2 LARGE GLASSES OF H*2O. DO
    NOT INDUCE VOMITING. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCONSCIOUS
    PERSON. CALL THE ECO LAB MEDICAL EMERGENCY NUMBER IMMEDIATELY,
    1-800-328-0026. INHAL: IN CASE OF BREATHING DIFFICULTY, MOVE IMMED
    TO FRESH AIR. IF INGESTION, CALL MED EMERGENCY NUMBER
    1-800-328-0026. IRRIT/DISCOMFORT PERS ISTS, CALL A MD.
