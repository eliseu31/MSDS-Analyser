=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING, GIVE
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN.
    CALL A PHYSICIAN. INGESTION: IF SWALLOWED, INDUCE VOMITING
    IMMEDIATELY BY GIVING  TWO GLASSES OF WATER AND STICKING FINGER
    DOWN THROAT. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN UNCONSCIOUS PERSON.
    CALL PHYSICIAN IMMEDIATELY. SKIN: REMOVE ANY CONTAMINATED CLOTHING.
    WASH SKIN WITH SOAP OR  MILD DETERGENT AND WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY. EYES: WASH WITH PLENTY
    OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES, LIFTING LOWER AND UPPER EYELIDS
    OCCASIONALLY. GET MEDICAL  ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:CARBON DIOXIDE, DRY CHEMICAL OR SAND.
Fire Fighting Procedures:WEAR NIOSH APPROVED SCBA AND FULL PROTECTIVE
    EQUIPMENT .
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:FLAMMABLE SOLID. HEATS SPONTANEOUSLY IN
    CONTACT WITH MOISTURE AND AIR. MAY IGNITE IN THE PRESENCE OF
    COMBUSTIBLE MATERIALS. NOT CONSIDERED TO BE AN EXPLOSION HAZARD.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:VENTILATE AREA OF LEAK OR SPILL. CLEAN-UP
    PERSONNEL REQUIRE PROTECTIVE CLOTHING AND NIOSH APPROVED
    RESPIRATORY PROTECTION FROM DUST. SPILLS: PICK UP AND PLACE IN A
    SUITABLE CONTAINER FOR RECLAMATION O R DISPOSAL IN A METHOD THAT
    DOES NOT GENERATE DUST.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:KEEP IN TIGLTY CLOSED CONTAINER.
    PROTECT FROM PHYSICAL DAMAGE. STORE IN COOL, DRY, VENTILATED AREA
    AWAY FROM SOURCES OF HEAT, MOISTURE & INCOMPATIBILITIES. KEEP AWAY
    FROM HEAT, SPARKS & FLAME. KEEP FR OM CONTACT WITH CLOTHING & OTHER
    COMBUSTIBLE MATERIALS. AVOID CONTACT WITH EYES, SKIN & CLOTHING.
Other Precautions:WARNING: FLAMMABLE! MAY IGINITE WITH MOISTURE AND
    AIR. HARMFUL IF SWALLOWED. CAUSES IRRITATION.
