=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES,
    OCCASIONALLY LIFTING UPPER & LOWER LIDS. GET MEDICAL AID
    IMMEDIATELY. SKIN: GET MEDICAL AID. FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF SOAP &
    WATER FOR AT LEAST 15  MINUTES WHILE REMOVING CONTAMINATED CLOTHING
    & SHOES. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING & SHOES. INGEST: IF VICTIM IS
    CONSCIOUS & ALERT, GIVE 2-4 CUPFULS OF MILK OR WATER. GET MEDICAL
    AID IMMEDIATELY. INH AL: GET MEDICAL AID IMMEDIATELY. REMOVE FROM
    EXPOSURE TO FRESH AIR IMMEDIATELY. IF NOT BREATHING, GIVE
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN.
    NOTES TO MD: TREAT SYMPTOMATICALL Y & SUPPORTIVELY.
