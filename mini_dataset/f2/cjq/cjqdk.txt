    GENERALLY THE PRODUCT DOES NOT IRRITATE THE SKIN. INHALATION: AFTER
    INHALATION, SUPPLY FRESH AIR; CONSULT DOCTOR IN CASE OF COMPLAINTS.
    EYES:  AFTER EYE CONTACT, RINSE OPENED EYE FOR AT LEAST SEVERAL
    MINUTES UNDER RUNNING WATER. INGESTION: AFTER SWALLOWING, INDUCE
    VOMITING AND CALL FOR MEDICAL HELP.
