=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES;
    CALL PHYSICIAN. SKIN: REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING, FLUSH WELL WITH
    WATER, CALL PHYSICIAN. INHALATION: GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION IF
    BREATH ING HAS STOPPED AND CALL PHYSICIAN. INGESTION: IF CONSCIOUS,
    GIVE WATER AND CALL PHYSICIAN IMMEDIATELY. DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Flash Point Method:TCC
Flash Point:=42.8C, 109.F

Fire Fighting Procedures:USE NIOSH APPROVED SCBA AND FULL PROTECTIVE
    EQUIPMENT . USE WATER SPRAY TO KEEP FIRE-EXPOSED CONTAINERS COOL,
    TO FLUSH SPILLS AWAY FROM EXPOSURES AND DILUTE SPILLS TO
    NON-FLAMMABLE MIXTURES.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WEAR GLOVES AND SOAK UP SPILLED SOLUTION WITH
    DISPOSABLE TOWELS. SMALL SPILLS SHOULD BE FLUSHED WITH LARGE
    QUANTITIES OF WATER. LARGE SPILLS SHOULD BE COLLECTED FOR DISPOSAL.

    CONTAINERS. DO NOT INGEST.
