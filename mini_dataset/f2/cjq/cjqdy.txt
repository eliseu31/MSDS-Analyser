=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE CONTACT: FLUSH EYES WITH LARGE QUANTITIES OF WATER FOR AT
    LEAST 15 MINUTES. CONSULT A PHYSICIAN. SKIN CONTACT: WASH AFFECTED
    AREA WITH SOAP AND WATER. IF IRRITATION PERSISTS, CONSULT A
    PHYSICIAN.	INHALATION: REMOVE TO AN UNCONTAMINATED AREA,
    ADMINISTER OXYGEN IF NECESSARY. IF VICTIM HAS STOPPED BREATHING
    BEGIN CPR. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. INGESTION: IF SWALLOWED, GIVE
    WATER OR MILK AND INDUCE V OMITING. GET IMMEDIATE MEDICAL
    ATTENTION.
