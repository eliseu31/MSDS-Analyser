=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: IMMEDIATELY FLUSH W/COPIOUS QUANTITIES OF WATER FOR 15
    MINS. SKIN: WASH THOROUGHLY W/SOAP & WATER. INHALATION: GENTLY BLOW
    NOSE & IRRIGATE W/CLEAN WATER. OBTAIN MEDICAL ATTENTION IN ALL
    CASES.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:SHOULD BE DILUTED W/WATER, CLEANED UP W/PAPER
    TOWELS & PLACED IN A SUITABLE CONTAINER FOR DISPOSAL.

    REFRIGERATOR 36-46F.
