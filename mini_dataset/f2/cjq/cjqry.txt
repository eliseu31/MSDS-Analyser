=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:SKIN: FLUSH WITH WATER. SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION IF IRRITATION
    PERSISTS. EYES: FLUSH WITH LARGE AMOUNT OF WATER, HOLDING EYELIDS
    OPEN. SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION IF IRRITATION PERSISTS.  INHALATION:
    GET TO  FRESH AIR. SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION IF IRRITATION PERSISTS.
    INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. DRINK LARGE AMOUNT OF WATER.
    SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.
