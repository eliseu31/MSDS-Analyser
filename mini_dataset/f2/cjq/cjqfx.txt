=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

    KEEP WARM AND QUIET. SKIN: WASH AFFECTED AREA THOROUGHLY W/SOAP &
    WATER. REMOVE CONTAMINATED CLOTHING AND LAUNDER BEFORE RE-USE.
    EYES: FLUS H WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES.
    GET MEDICAL ATTENTION. INGEST: GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
