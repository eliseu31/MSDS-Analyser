=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES-IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH WATER.  REMOVE CONTACTS, CONTINUE
    FLUSHING. HAVE EYES EXAMINED & TESTED BY MEDICAL PERSONNEL IF
    IRRITATION DEVELOPS/PERSISTS. SKIN-WASH SKIN WITH SOAP & WATER.
    REMOVE CONTA MINATED CLOTHES. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IF IRRITATION
    DEVELOPS/PERSISTS. WASH CLOTHES BEFORE REUSE. INGESTED-DO NOT
    INDUCE VOMITING! GET IMMEDIATE MEDICAL ATTENTION. NOTHING BY MOUTH
    IF VICTIM UNCONSCI OUS. INHALED-REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. GIVE
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION IF BREATHING STOPPED. GIVE OXYGEN IF
    BREATHING DIFFICULT. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:LARGE SPILLS: SHUT OFF LEAK IF POSSIBLE &
    SAFE. WEAR SCBA & APPROPRIATE PERSONAL PROTECTIVE GEAR. ABSORB WITH
    INERT MATERIAL THEN PLACE IN A CHEMICAL WASTE CONTAINER FOR
    DISPOSAL. DO NOT FLUSH TO SEWE R. AVOID RUN-OFF TO STORM SEWERS &
    DITCHES WHICH LEAD TO WATERWAYS. SMALL SPILL: ABSORB WITH INERT
    MATERIALTHEN PLACE IN A CHEMICAL WASTE DISPOSAL CONTAINER.
Neutralizing Agent:NONE SPECIFIED BY MANUFACTURER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:AVOID PROLONGED/REPEATED CONTACT WITH
    EYES, SKIN & CLOTHING. WASH HANDS BEFORE EATING. USE WITH ADEQUATE
    VENTILATION. AVOID BREATHING PRODUCT VAPOR OR MIST. DO NOT REUSE
    CONTAINER. STORE IN A COOL, DR Y, WELL-VENTILATED AREA AWAY FROM
    HEAT, SPARKS OR FLAMES. KEEP CONTAINER CLOSED WHEN NOT IN USE.
Other Precautions:DO NOT STORE IN DIRECT SUNLIGHT.  KEEP OUT OF REACH
    OF CHILDREN.
