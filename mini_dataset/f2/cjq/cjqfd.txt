=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES CONTACT: FLUSH WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER. GET MEDICAL
    ATTENTION. SKIN CONTACT: WASH WITH LARGE AMOUNTS OF WATER.
    INHALATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. INGESTION: IF VICTIM IS CONSCIOUS
    AND ALERT, GI VE 2-4 CUPFULS OF MILK AND WATER. GET MEDICAL AID
    IMMEDIATELY.
