=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH AT ONCE WITH COOL RUNNING WATER. REMOVE CONTACT
    LENSES. HOLDING EYELIDS APART, CONTINUE FLUSHING FOR 15 MINUTES.
    SKIN: FLUSH SKIN WITH COOL WATER; THEN WASH THOROUGHLY WITH SOAP
    AND WATER.	WASH CONTAMINATED CLOTHING WELL BEFORE REUSE. IF
    SWALLOWED RINSE MOUTH, THEN DRINK 1 OR 2 LARGE GLASSES OF WATER. DO
    NOT INDUCE	VOMITING . NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN
    UNCONSCIOUS PERSON. INHA LATION: REMOVE TO FRESH AIR. APPLY
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION/OXYGEN IF NECESSARY . IF IRRITATION OR
    DISCOMFORT PERSISTS, CALL A PHYSICIAN.
