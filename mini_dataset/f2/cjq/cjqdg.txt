=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INGESTION: CALL MD. IF SWALLOWED, DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. IF
    CONSCIOUS, GIVE WATER, MILK OR MILK OF MAGNESIA. INHALATION: REMOVE
    TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING, GIVE ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. IF
    BREATHI NG IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN. SKIN: FLUSH WITH WATER. EYES:
    IMMEDIATELY FLUSH WITH PLENTY OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES.

=====================  Fire Fighting Measures  =====================

Extinguishing Media:USE WATER SPRAY.
Fire Fighting Procedures:USE NIOSH APPROVED SCBA AND FULL PROTECTIVE
    EQUIPMENT . MOVE CONTAINERS FROM FIRE AREA IF IT CAN BE DONE
    WITHOUT RISK. USE WATER TO KEEP FIRE-EXPOSED CONTAINERS COOL.
Unusual Fire/Explosion Hazard:STRONG OXIDIZER. CONT W/COMBUST MATLS,
    FLAMM MATLS, OR POWDERED METALS CAN CAUSE FIRE/EXPLO. NOTE:
    DECOMPOSES AT MELTING POINT. TOXIC GASES PRDCD: SULFUR DIOXIDE.
    EXPLO DATA-SENSITIVITY TO MECHANICAL  IMPACT & STATIC DISCHARGE:
    NONE IDENTIFIED. HAZARD RATING: HLTH - 2; FLAMMABILITY - 0;
    REACTIVITY - 3.

==================  Accidental Release Measures  ==================

Spill Release Procedures:WEAR NIOSH APPROVED SELF-CONTAINED BREATHING
    APPARATUS AND FULL PROTECTIVE CLOTHING. KEEP COMBUSTIBLES (WOOD,
    PAPER, OIL, ETC) AWAY FROM SPILLED MATERIAL. WITH CLEAN SHOVEL,
    CAREFULLY PLACE MATERIAL I NTO CLEAN, DRY CONTAINER AND COVER;
    REMOVE FROM AREA. FLUSH SPILL AREA WITH WATER.

======================	Handling and Storage  ======================

Handling and Storage Precautions:SAF-T-DATA STORAGE COLOR CODE: YELLOW
    (REACTIVE). KEEP CONTAINER TIGHTLY CLOSED. STORE SEPARATELY AND
    AWAY FROM FLAMMABLE AND COMBUSTIBLE MATERIALS. ISOLATE FROM
    INCOMPATIBLE MATERIALS.
Other Precautions:KEEP FROM CONTACT WITH CLOTHING. DO NOT GET IN EYES,
    ON SKIN, ON CLOTHING. AVOID BREATHING DUST. USE WITH ADEQUATE
    VENTILATION. KEEP AWAY FROM SOURCES OF IGNITION - NO SMOKING.
