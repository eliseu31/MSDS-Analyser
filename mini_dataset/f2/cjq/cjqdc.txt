=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:INHALATION: REMOVE VICTIM TO FRESH AIR. IF NOT BREATHING GIVE
    ARTIFICIAL RESPIRATION. IF BREATHING IS DIFFICULT, GIVE OXYGEN.
    CALL MD. EYES: FLUSH WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES . SKIN: FLUSH WITH COPIOUS AMOUNTS OF WATER FOR AT LEAST 15
    MINUTES. INGESTION: IF INGESTION OCCURS GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
