=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYE CONT: FLUSH WITH WATER FOR AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. IF
    IRRITATED, SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION. SKIN CONT: WASH WITH SOAP AND
    WATER. IF IRRITATED, SEEK MEDICAL ATTENTION. INHALATION: REMOVE TO
    FRESH AIR. RE SUSCITATE IF NECESSARY. GET MEDICAL ATTENTION.
    INGESTION: DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. DRINK TWO LARGE GLASSES OF
    WATER. GET IMMEDIATE MEDICAL ATTENTION.
