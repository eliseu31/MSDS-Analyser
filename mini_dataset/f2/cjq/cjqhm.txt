=======================  First Aid Measures  =======================

First Aid:EYES: FLUSH IMMEDIATELY WITH PLENTY OF COOL RUNNING WATER FOR
    AT LEAST 15 MINUTES. REMOVE CONTACT LENSES, IF USED AND THEN FLUSH
    AGAIN. SKIN: FLUSH WITH WATER, THEN WASH WITH SOAP AND WATER. WASH
    CONT AMINATED CLOTHING WELL BEFORE REUSE. INGESTION: RINSE MOUTH.
    DO NOT INDUCE VOMITING. IN CASE OF NAUSEA OR SPONTANEOUS VOMITING,
    KEEP VICTIM'S HEAD BELOW HIPS. NEVER GIVE ANYTHING BY MOUTH TO AN
    UNCONS CIOUS PERSON. IF INGESTION, CALL THE ECOLAB EMERGENCY NUMBER
    IMMEDIATELY (1-800-328-0026); IN CASE OF PERSISTENT EYE OR SKIN
    IRRITATION, CALL A POISON CONTROL CENTER OR PHYSICIAN.
